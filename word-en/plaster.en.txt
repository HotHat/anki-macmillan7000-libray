plaster 
▶noun
1 the plaster covering the bricks: plasterwork, stucco, pargeting.
2 a statuette made of plaster: plaster of Paris, gypsum.
▶verb
1 bread plastered with butter: cover thickly, smother, spread, smear, cake, coat, slather.
2 his hair was plastered down with sweat: flatten (down), smooth down, slick down.

chemist 
ˈkeməst 
(abbreviation chem.) 
▶noun
1 an expert in chemistry; a person engaged in chemical research or experiments.
2 British a drugstore. 
• a pharmacist. ORIGIN late Middle English (denoting an alchemist): from French chimiste, from modern Latin chimista, from alchimista ‘alchemist,’ from alchimia (see alchemy) .

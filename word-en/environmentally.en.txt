environmentally 
inˌvīrənˈmen(t)əlē

▶adverb
1 in a way that relates to the natural world and the impact of human activity on its condition: alternatives to environmentally damaging pesticides 
measures designed to promote environmentally friendly farming 
the village was suffering economically, socially, and environmentally.
2 in a way that relates to a person's surroundings: [sentence adverb] : environmentally speaking, it's a good job change for me.

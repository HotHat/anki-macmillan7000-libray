ant

▶noun a small insect, often with a sting, that usually lives in a complex social colony with one or more breeding queens. It is wingless except for fertile adults, which often form large mating swarms, and is proverbial for industriousness. [Family Formicidae, order Hymenoptera: several subfamilies.]

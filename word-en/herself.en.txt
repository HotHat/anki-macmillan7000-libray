herself 
hərˈself

▶pronoun [third person singular]
1 [reflexive] used as the object of a verb or preposition to refer to a female person or animal previously mentioned as the subject of the clause: she had to defend herself 
Jo made herself a cup of tea.
2 [emphatic] she or her personally (used to emphasize a particular female person or animal mentioned): she told me herself.

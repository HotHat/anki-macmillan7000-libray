November 
nōˈvembər nəˈvembər

▶noun
1 the eleventh month of the year, in the northern hemisphere usually considered the last month of autumn: the store opened in November 
[as modifier] : November days.
2 a code word representing the letter N, used in radio communication. ORIGIN Old English, from Latin, from novem ‘nine’ (being originally the ninth month of the Roman year).

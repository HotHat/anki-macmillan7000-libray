Christmas 
ˈkrisməs

▶noun (plural Christmases) the annual Christian festival celebrating Christ's birth, held on December 25 in the Western Church. 
• the period immediately before and after December 25: we had guests over Christmas. 
▶exclam. informal expressing surprise, dismay, or despair. DERIVATIVES Christmassy 
ˈkrisməsē 
adjectiveORIGIN Old English Crīstes mæsse (see Christ, Mass) .

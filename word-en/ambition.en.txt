ambition 
amˈbiSH(ə)n

▶noun a strong desire to do or to achieve something, typically requiring determination and hard work: her ambition was to become a model 
he achieved his ambition of making a fortune. 
• desire and determination to achieve success: life offered few opportunities for young people with ambition. ORIGIN Middle English: via Old French from Latin ambitio(n-), from ambire ‘go around (canvassing for votes).’

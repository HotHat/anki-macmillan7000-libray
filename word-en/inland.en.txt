inland 
ˈinˌland ˈinˌlənd

▶adjective situated in the interior of a country rather than on the coast: the deserts of inland Australia. 
• [attributive] chiefly British carried on within the limits of a country; domestic: a network of waterways that allowed inland trade. 
▶adverb 
inˈland 
in or toward the interior of a country: the path turned inland and met the road. 
▶noun (the inland) the parts of a country remote from the sea or borders; the interior. DERIVATIVES inlander

haul 
▶verb
1 she hauled the basket along: drag, pull, tug, heave, lug, hump, draw, tow; informal yank.
2 a contract to haul coal: transport, convey, carry, ship, ferry, move.
▶noun the thieves abandoned their haul: booty, loot, plunder; spoils, stolen goods, ill-gotten gains; informal swag, boodle.

bacon 
ˈbākən

▶noun cured meat from the sides and belly of a pig, having distinct strips of fat and typically served in thin slices. 
• cured meat from the back of a pig. 
PHRASES
bring home the bacon informal
1 supply material provision or support; earn a living.
2 achieve success. save someone's bacon rescue someone from danger or difficulty: only hard braking and a quick turn on to the hard shoulder saved our bacon.ORIGIN Middle English: from Old French, from a Germanic word meaning ‘ham, flitch’; related to back .

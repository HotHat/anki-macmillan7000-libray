thermal 
ˈTHərməl

▶adjective relating to heat. 
• another term for geothermal. 
• (of a garment) made of a fabric that provides exceptional insulation to keep the body warm: thermal underwear. 
▶noun
1 an upward current of warm air, used by gliders, balloons, and birds to gain height.
2 (usually thermals) a thermal garment, especially underwear. DERIVATIVES thermally 
ˈTHərməlē 
adverbORIGIN mid 18th century (in the sense ‘relating to hot springs’): from French, from Greek thermē ‘heat.’

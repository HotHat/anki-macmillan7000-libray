improvement 
imˈpro͞ovmənt

▶noun an example or instance of improving or being improved: an improvement in East–West relations. 
• the action of improving or being improved: there's still room for improvement. 
• a thing that makes something better or is better than something else: home improvements 
it's an improvement on the last cake I made. ORIGIN late Middle English emprowement (in the sense ‘profitable management or use; profit’), from Anglo-Norman French, from emprower (see improve) .

president 
▶noun
1 the president of the United States: head of state, chief executive, premier, prime minister.
2 the president of the society: head, chief, director, leader, governor, principal, master; informal prez.
3 the president of the company: chairman, chairwoman, chief executive (officer), CEO; owner, managing director.QUOTE When I was a boy, I was told that anybody could become President. I’m beginning to believe it.Clarence Darrow, American lawyer

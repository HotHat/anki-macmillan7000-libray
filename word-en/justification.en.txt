justification 
ˌjəstəfəˈkāSH(ə)n

▶noun
1 the action of showing something to be right or reasonable: the justification of revolutionary action 
he made a speech in justification of his career. 
• good reason for something that exists or has been done: there is no justification for an increase in charges 
all these incidents were used again as a justification for my dismissal.
2 Theology the action of declaring or making righteous in the sight of God.
3 Printing the action or manner of justifying a line of type or piece of text.

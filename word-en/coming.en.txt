coming 
ˈkəmiNG

▶adjective
1 due to happen or just beginning: work is due to start in the coming year.
2 likely to be important or successful in the future: he was the coming man of French racing. 
▶noun [in singular] an arrival or an approach: the coming of a new age. 
PHRASES
comings and goings the busy movements of a person or group of people, especially in and out of a place.not know if one is coming or going informal be confused, especially as a result of being very busy.

decoration 
ˌdekəˈrāSH(ə)n

▶noun
1 the process or art of decorating or adorning something: the lavish decoration of cloth with gilt. 
• ornamentation: pearwood inlaid with floral decoration of stained woods. 
• a thing that serves as an ornament: Christmas tree decorations. 
• paint or wallpaper applied when decorating a room: an authority on English furniture and decoration.
2 a medal or award conferred as an honor.

sudden 
ˈsədn

▶adjective occurring or done quickly and unexpectedly or without warning: a sudden bright flash. 
▶adverb literary or informal suddenly: sudden there swooped an eagle downward. 
PHRASES
all of a sudden suddenly: I feel really tired all of a sudden.DERIVATIVES suddenness 
ˈsədnˌnəs 
nounORIGIN Middle English: from Anglo-Norman French sudein, from an alteration of Latin subitaneus, from subitus ‘sudden.’

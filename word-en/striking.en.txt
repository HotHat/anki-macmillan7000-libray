striking 
ˈstrīkiNG

▶adjective
1 attracting attention by reason of being unusual, extreme, or prominent: the murder bore a striking similarity to an earlier shooting 
[with clause] : it is striking that no research into the problem is occurring. 
• dramatically good-looking or beautiful: she is naturally striking 
a striking landscape.
2 [attributive] (of an employee) on strike: striking mine workers. 
▶noun the action of striking: substantial damage was caused by the striking of a submerged object. 
PHRASES
within striking distance see distance.

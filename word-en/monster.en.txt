monster 
▶noun
1 her husband is a monster: brute, fiend, beast, devil, demon, barbarian, savage, animal; informal swine, pig.
2 the boy's a little monster: rascal, imp, monkey, wretch, devil; informal horror, scamp, scalawag, tyke, varmint, hellion; archaic scapegrace, rapscallion.
3 he's a monster of a man: giant, mammoth, colossus, leviathan, titan; informal jumbo.
▶adjective informal a monster truck. See huge.

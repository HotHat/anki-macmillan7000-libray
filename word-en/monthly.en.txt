monthly 
ˈmənTHlē

▶adjective [attributive] done, produced, or occurring once a month: the council held monthly meetings. 
▶adverb once a month; every month; from month to month: most of us get paid monthly. 
▶noun (plural monthlies)
1 a magazine that is published once a month.
2 (monthlies) informal a menstrual period.

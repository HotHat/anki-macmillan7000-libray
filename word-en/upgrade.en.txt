upgrade 
▶verb 
ˈəpˌɡrād ˌəpˈɡrād 
[with object] raise (something) to a higher standard, in particular improve (equipment or machinery) by adding or replacing components: the cost of upgrading each workstation is around $300 
(as adjective upgraded) : upgraded computers. 
• raise (an employee) to a higher grade or rank. 
▶noun 
ˈəpˌɡrād

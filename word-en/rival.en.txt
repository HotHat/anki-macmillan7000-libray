rival 
▶noun
1 his rival for the nomination: opponent, challenger, competitor, contender; adversary, antagonist, enemy; informal frenemy; literary foe. ANTONYMS ally.
2 the tool has no rival: equal, match, peer, equivalent, counterpart, like.

pedal 
BrE ˈpɛd(ə)l, AmE ˈpɛdl 
A. noun 
① (on bicycle) 脚蹬子 jiǎodēngzi 
② Auto 踏板 tàbǎn 
③ Music (on piano, harp) 踏板 tàbǎn (on organ) 脚键盘 jiǎojiànpán 
B.  transitive verb(present participle etc. -ll- BrE, -l- AmE ) 
① (operate by pedalling) 用脚蹬 yòng jiǎo dēng to pedal a bicycle 骑自行车 qí zìxíngchē 
② (cycle) 骑车走过 qí chē zǒuguo ‹distance›he pedalled 90 miles that day 他那天骑车走了90英里 
C.  intransitive verb(present participle etc. -ll- BrE, -l- AmE ) 
① (use pedal) 踩踏板 cǎi tàbǎn to pedal along 一直踩踏板 to pedal hard/furiously 用力/拼命蹬车 
② (cycle) 骑自行车 qí zìxíngchē to pedal down the hill 骑车下山

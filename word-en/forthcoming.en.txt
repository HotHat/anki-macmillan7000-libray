forthcoming 
fôrTHˈkəmiNG

▶adjective
1 planned for or about to happen in the near future: the forthcoming baseball season.
2 [predicative often with negative] (of something required) ready or made available when wanted or needed: financial support was not forthcoming. 
• (of a person) willing to divulge information: their daughter had never been forthcoming about her time in Europe. DERIVATIVES forthcomingness noun

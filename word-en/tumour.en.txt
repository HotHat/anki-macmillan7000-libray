tumor 
ˈt(y)o͞omər 
(British tumour) 
▶noun a swelling of a part of the body, generally without inflammation, caused by an abnormal growth of tissue, whether benign or malignant. 
• archaic a swelling of any kind. DERIVATIVES tumorous

massacre 
ˈmasəkər

▶noun an indiscriminate and brutal slaughter of people: the attack was described as a cold-blooded massacre 
she says he is an accomplice to massacre. 
• informal a heavy defeat of a sports team or contestant. 
▶verb [with object] deliberately and violently kill (a large number of people). 
• informal inflict a heavy defeat on (a sports team or contestant). ORIGIN late 16th century: from French, of unknown origin.

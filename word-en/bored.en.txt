▶adjective feeling weary because one is unoccupied or lacks interest in one's current activity: she got bored with staring out of the window 
they would hang around all day, bored stiff. DERIVATIVES boredly adverbusage: The traditional constructions for bored are bored by or bored with. The construction bored of emerged more recently, and is extremely common, especially in informal language. Although it is perfectly logical by analogy with constructions such as tired of, it is not fully accepted in standard English.

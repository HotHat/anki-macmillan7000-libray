woolen 
ˈwo͝olən 
(British woollen) 
▶adjective [attributive] made wholly or partly of wool: thick woolen blankets. 
• relating to the production of wool: the woolen industry 
a woolen mill. 
▶noun (usually woolens) an article of clothing made of wool. ORIGIN late Old English wullen (see wool, -en2) .

indeed 
▶adverb
1 there was, indeed, quite a furor: as expected, to be sure; in fact, in point of fact, as a matter of fact, in truth, actually, as it happens/happened, if truth be told, admittedly; archaic in sooth.
2 “May I join you?” “Yes, indeed.”: certainly, assuredly, of course, naturally, without (a) doubt, without question, by all means, yes; informal you bet, I'll say; informal indeedy.
3 you are indeed clever: very, extremely, exceedingly, tremendously, immensely, singularly, decidedly, particularly, remarkably, really.

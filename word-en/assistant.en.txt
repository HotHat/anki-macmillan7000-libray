assistant 
əˈsistənt

▶noun a person who ranks below a senior person: the managing director and his assistant 
[as modifier] : an assistant manager. 
• [with adjective or noun modifier] a person who helps in particular work: a laboratory assistant. ORIGIN late Middle English: from Old French, or from medieval Latin assistent- ‘taking one's stand beside,’ from the verb assistere (see assist) .

cutting 
ˈkədiNG

▶noun
1 the action of someone or something that cuts: the cutting of the cake 
tax-cutting.
2 (often cuttings) a piece cut off from something, especially what remains when something is being trimmed or prepared: grass cuttings. 
• a piece cut from a plant for propagation. 
• British a clipping from a newspaper or periodical.
3 an open passage excavated through higher ground for a railroad, road, or canal. 
▶adjective capable of cutting something: the cutting blades of the hedge trimmer. 
• (especially of a comment) causing emotional pain; hurtful: a cutting remark. 
• (of the wind) bitterly cold. DERIVATIVES cuttingly

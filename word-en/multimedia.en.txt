multimedia 
ˌməltēˈmēdēə ˌməltīˈmēdēə

▶adjective (of art, education, etc.) using more than one medium of expression or communication: a multimedia art form. 
• (of computer applications) incorporating audio and video, especially interactively. 
▶noun the use of a variety of artistic or communicative media. 
• an extension of hypertext allowing the provision of audio and video material cross-referenced to a computer text.

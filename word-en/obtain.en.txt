obtain 
əbˈtān äbˈtān

▶verb
1 [with object] get, acquire, or secure (something): an opportunity to obtain advanced degrees.
2 [no object] formal be prevalent, customary, or established: the price of silver fell to that obtaining elsewhere in the ancient world. DERIVATIVES obtainer noun. obtainment nounORIGIN late Middle English: from Old French obtenir, from Latin obtinere ‘obtain, gain.’

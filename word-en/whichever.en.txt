whichever 
ˌ(h)wiCHˈevər

▶rel. det. & pronoun used to emphasize a lack of restriction in selecting one of a definite set of alternatives: [as determiner] : choose whichever brand you prefer 
[as pronoun] : their pension should be increased annually in line with earnings or prices, whichever is the higher. 
• regardless of which: [as determiner] : they were in a position to intercept him whichever way he ran 
[as pronoun] : whichever they choose, we must accept it.

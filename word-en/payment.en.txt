payment 
ˈpāmənt

▶noun
1 the action or process of paying someone or something, or of being paid: ask for a discount for payment by cash 
three interest-free monthly payments.
2 an amount paid or payable: an interim compensation payment of $2500. 
• something given as a reward or in recompense for something done: a suit with a velvet collar that I got as payment for being in the show. ORIGIN late Middle English: from Old French paiement, from payer ‘to pay.’

pair 
▶noun
1 a pair of gloves: set (of two), matching set, two of a kind.
2 the pair were arrested: two, couple, duo, brace, twosome, duplet; twins.
3 a pair of lines of poetry: couplet; Prosody distich.
4 the happy pair: couple, man/husband and wife.
▶verb a cardigan paired with a matching skirt: match, put together, couple, twin.
PHRASES
pair off/up Rachel paired up with Tommy: get together, team up, form a couple, make a twosome, hook up, marry.

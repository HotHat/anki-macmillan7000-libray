deterrent 
dəˈtərənt

▶noun a thing that discourages or is intended to discourage someone from doing something. 
• a nuclear weapon or weapons system regarded as deterring an enemy from attack. 
▶adjective able or intended to deter: the deterrent effect of heavy prison sentences. ORIGIN early 19th century: from Latin deterrent- ‘deterring,’ from the verb deterrere (see deter) .

discussion 
dəˈskəSH(ə)n

▶noun the action or process of talking about something, typically in order to reach a decision or to exchange ideas: the proposals are not a blueprint but ideas for discussion 
the specific content of the legislation was under discussion. 
• a conversation or debate about a certain topic: discussions about environmental improvement programs. 
• a detailed treatment of a particular topic in speech or writing. ORIGIN Middle English (denoting judicial examination): via Old French from late Latin discussio(n-), from discutere ‘investigate’ (see discuss) .

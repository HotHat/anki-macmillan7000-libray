pork 
pôrk

▶noun
1 the flesh of a pig used as food, especially when uncured.
2 short for pork barrel. 
▶verb
1 [with object] vulgar slang, chiefly US (of a man) have sexual intercourse with.
2 [no object] informal stuff oneself with food; overeat: I porked out on the roast pig. ORIGIN Middle English: from Old French porc, from Latin porcus ‘pig.’

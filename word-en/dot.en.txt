dot 
▶noun a pattern of tiny dots: spot, speck, fleck, speckle; decimal point, period, pixel.
▶verb
1 spots of rain dotted his shirt: spot, fleck, mark, stipple, freckle, sprinkle; literary bestrew, besprinkle.
2 the streets are dotted with restaurants: scatter, pepper, sprinkle, strew.
PHRASES
on the dot informal ring the bell at 1:15 on the dot: precisely, exactly, sharp, prompt, dead on, on the stroke of ——; informal on the button, on the nose.

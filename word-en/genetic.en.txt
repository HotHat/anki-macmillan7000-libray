genetic 
jəˈnedik

▶adjective
1 relating to genes or heredity: all the cells in the body contain the same genetic information. 
• relating to genetics: an attempt to control mosquitoes by genetic techniques.
2 relating to origin; arising from a common origin: the genetic relations between languages. DERIVATIVES genetical adjectiveORIGIN mid 19th century ( sense 2): from genesis, on the pattern of pairs such as antithesis, antithetic.

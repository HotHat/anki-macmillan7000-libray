agreed 
əˈɡrēd

▶adjective [attributive] discussed or negotiated and then accepted by all parties: the agreed time 
the agreed upon percentage. 
• [predicative] (of two or more parties) holding the same view or opinion on something: all the republics are agreed on the necessity of a common defense policy 
[with clause] : we are agreed that what is needed is a catchy title.

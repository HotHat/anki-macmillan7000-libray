football 
ˈfo͝otˌbôl

▶noun
1 a form of team game played in North America with an oval ball on a field marked out as a gridiron. 
• British term for soccer. 
• play in a game of football, especially when stylish and entertaining: his team played some impressive football.
2 a ball used in football, either oval (as in American football) or round (as in soccer), typically made of leather or plastic and filled with compressed air. 
• a topical issue or problem that is the subject of continued argument or controversy: the use of education as a political football.

finished 
ˈfiniSHt

▶adjective (of an action, activity, or piece of work) having been completed or ended: a preparatory drawing for the finished painting. 
• [predicative] (of a person) having completed or ended an action or activity: they'll be finished here in an hour. 
• [predicative] having lost effectiveness, power, or prestige: he was told he was finished at the club. 
• (of an object or room) having been given a particular decorative surface as the final stage in its manufacture or decoration: [in combination] : plastic-finished lining paper. 
• [attributive] (of livestock) having completed fattening before slaughter: a reduction in prices for finished cattle.

discovery 
dəˈskəv(ə)rē

▶noun (plural discoveries)
1 the action or process of discovering or being discovered: the discovery of the body 
he made some startling discoveries. 
• a person or thing discovered: the drug is not a new discovery.
2 Law the compulsory disclosure, by a party to an action, of relevant documents referred to by the other party. ORIGIN mid 16th century: from discover, on the pattern of the pair recover, recovery.

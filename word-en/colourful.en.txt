colorful 
ˈkələrfəl 
(British colourful) 
▶adjective
1 having much or varied color; bright: a colorful array of fruit.
2 full of interest; lively and exciting: a controversial and colorful character 
a colorful account of the meeting. 
• (of a person's life or background) involving variously disreputable activities: he gained a playboy reputation during a colorful bachelorhood. 
• (of language) vulgar or rude: colorful words usually impolite in public meetings. DERIVATIVES colorfully 
-f(ə)lē

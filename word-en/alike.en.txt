alike 
▶adjective all the doors looked alike: similar, (much) the same, indistinguishable, identical, uniform, interchangeable, cut from the same cloth; informal like (two) peas in a pod, like Tweedledum and Tweedledee, much of a muchness. ANTONYMS different.
▶adverb great minds think alike: similarly, (just) the same, in the same way/manner/fashion, equally, likewise, identically.

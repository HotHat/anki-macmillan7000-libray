please 
▶verb
1 he'd do anything to please her: make happy, give pleasure to, make someone feel good; delight, charm, amuse, entertain; satisfy, gratify, humor, oblige, content, suit. ANTONYMS annoy.
2 do as you please: like, want, wish, desire, see fit, think fit, choose, will, prefer.
▶adverb please sit down: if you please, if you wouldn't mind, if you would be so good; kindly, pray; archaic prithee.

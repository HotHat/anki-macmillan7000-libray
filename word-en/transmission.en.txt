transmission 
tranzˈmiSHən transˈmiSHən

▶noun
1 the action or process of transmitting something or the state of being transmitted: the transmission of the HIV virus. 
• a program or signal that is broadcast or sent out: television transmissions.
2 the mechanism by which power is transmitted from an engine to the wheels of a motor vehicle. ORIGIN early 17th century: from Latin transmissio (see trans-, mission) .

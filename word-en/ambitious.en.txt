ambitious 
amˈbiSHəs

▶adjective having or showing a strong desire and determination to succeed: his mother was hard-working and ambitious for her four children. 
• (of a plan or piece of work) intended to satisfy high aspirations and therefore difficult to achieve: the scope of the book is very ambitious 
an ambitious enterprise. DERIVATIVES ambitiously 
amˈbiSHəslē 
adverb. ambitiousness 
amˈbiSHəsnəs 
nounORIGIN late Middle English: from Old French ambitieux or Latin ambitiosus, from ambitio (see ambition) .

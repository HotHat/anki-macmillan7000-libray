postal 
ˈpōstəl

▶adjective [attributive] relating to the post office or the mail: increased postal rates 
postal services. 
• chiefly British done through the mail: a postal ballot 
a postal survey. 
▶noun (in full postal card) US informal another term for postcard. 
PHRASES
go postal US informal become crazed and violent, especially as the result of stress. [with reference to cases in which disgruntled employees of the US Postal Service have shot colleagues.]DERIVATIVES postally adverbORIGIN mid 19th century: from French, from poste ‘postal service.’

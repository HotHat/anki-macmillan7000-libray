accuracy 
ˈakyərəsē

▶noun (plural accuracies) the quality or state of being correct or precise: we have confidence in the accuracy of the statistics 
she hit the ball with great accuracy. 
• technical the degree to which the result of a measurement, calculation, or specification conforms to the correct value or a standard: the accuracy of radiocarbon dating 
accuracies of 50–70%. Compare with precision.

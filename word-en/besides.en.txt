besides 
bəˈsīdz

▶preposition in addition to; apart from: I have no other family besides my parents 
besides being a player, he was my friend. 
▶adverb in addition; as well: I'm capable of doing the work, and a lot more besides. 
• moreover; anyway: I had no time to warn you. Besides, I wasn't sure. usage: See usage at beside .

consultation 
ˌkänsəlˈtāSH(ə)n

▶noun the action or process of formally consulting or discussing: they improved standards in consultation with consumer representatives 
consultations between the two governments. 
• a meeting with an expert or professional, such as a medical doctor, in order to seek advice. ORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin consultatio(n-), from the verb consultare (see consult) .

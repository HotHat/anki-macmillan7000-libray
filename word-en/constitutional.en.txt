constitutional 
ˌkänstəˈt(y)o͞oSH(ə)n(ə)l

▶adjective
1 relating to an established set of principles governing a state: a constitutional amendment. 
• in accordance with or allowed by an established set of principles governing a state: a constitutional monarchy.
2 relating to someone's physical or mental condition: a constitutional weakness. 
▶noun dated a walk, typically one taken regularly to maintain or restore good health.

producer 
prəˈd(y)o͞osər

▶noun
1 a person, company, or country that makes, grows, or supplies goods or commodities for sale: an oil producer. 
• a person or thing that makes or causes something: the mold is the producer of the toxin aflatoxin.
2 a person responsible for the financial and managerial aspects of making of a movie or broadcast or for staging a play, opera, etc. 
• a person who supervises the making of a musical recording, especially by determining the overall sound.

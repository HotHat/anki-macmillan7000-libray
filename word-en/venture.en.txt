venture 
▶noun a business venture: enterprise, undertaking, project, initiative, scheme, operation, endeavor, speculation, plunge, gamble, gambit, experiment.
▶verb
1 we ventured across the country: set out, go, travel, journey.
2 may I venture an opinion? put forward, advance, proffer, offer, volunteer, air, suggest, submit, propose, moot.
3 I ventured to ask her to come and dine with me: dare, be/make so bold as, presume; take the liberty of, stick one's neck out, go out on a limb.

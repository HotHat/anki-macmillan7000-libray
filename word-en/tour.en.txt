tour 
▶noun
1 we enjoyed a two-week tour of Italy: trip to/through, excursion to/through, journey to/through, expedition to/through, jaunt to/through, outing to/through; trek to/through, safari to/through; archaic peregrination to/through.
2 a tour of the factory: visit, inspection, guided tour.
▶verb
1 this hotel is well placed for touring the Cariboo: travel around, explore, discover, vacation in, visit.
2 the governor toured the factory: visit, go around/through, walk around/through, inspect; informal check out.
PHRASES
tour of duty his tour of duty in the Middle East: stint, stretch, spell, turn, assignment, period of service.

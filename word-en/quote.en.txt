quote 
▶verb
1 he quoted a sentence from the book: recite, repeat, reproduce, retell, echo, parrot, iterate; take, extract.
2 she quoted one case in which a girl died: cite, mention, refer to, name, instance, specify, identify; relate, recount; allude to, point out, present, offer, advance.
▶noun
1 a Shakespearean quote. See quotation.
2 a quote from the contractor: estimate, price, bid, costing, charge, figure, tender.

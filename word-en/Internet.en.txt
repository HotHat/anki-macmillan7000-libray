Internet 
ˈin(t)ərˌnet

▶noun (the Internet) a global computer network providing a variety of information and communication facilities, consisting of interconnected networks using standardized communication protocols: the guide is also available on the Internet 
[as modifier] : Internet access 
an Internet connection. ORIGIN 1970s (denoting a computer network connecting two or more smaller networks): from inter- ‘reciprocal, mutual’ + network .

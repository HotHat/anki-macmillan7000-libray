spreadsheet 
ˈspredˌSHēt

▶noun an electronic document in which data is arranged in the rows and columns of a grid and can be manipulated and used in calculations: we have a color-coded Excel spreadsheet of all of our trip expenses 
William spent most of the day updating the Risk Assessment spreadsheet. 
• a computer program used to create and manipulate spreadsheets. 
▶verb [no object] (usually as noun spreadsheeting) use or create a spreadsheet.

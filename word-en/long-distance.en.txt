long distance 
ˈlɔŋ ˈˌdɪstəns

▶adjective (usually long-distance)
1 traveling or operating between distant places: a long-distance truck driver 
long-distance phone calls.
2 denoting or relating to a race distance of
6 miles or 10,000 meters (6 miles 376 yds), or longer: a long-distance runner. 
▶adverb between distant places: traveling long distance.

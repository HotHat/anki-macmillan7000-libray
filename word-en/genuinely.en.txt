genuinely 
ˈjenyo͞oənlē

▶adverb
1 in a truthful way: they speak genuinely about how proud they are. 
• used to emphasize sincerity or seriousness: both parties genuinely believe they're right 
it is genuinely an honor to be on the campaign.
2 to the fullest degree; properly: people don't genuinely understand what they're dealing with. 
• [as submodifier] really (used for emphasis): this is a genuinely funny film.

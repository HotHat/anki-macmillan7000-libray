unnecessary 
ˌənˈnesəˌserē

▶adjective not needed: a fourth Chicago airport is unnecessary. 
• more than is needed; excessive: the police used unnecessary force. 
• (of a remark) not appropriate and likely to be offensive or impertinent. 
▶pl. noun (unnecessaries) unnecessary things. DERIVATIVES unnecessariness

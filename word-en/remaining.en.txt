remaining 
rəˈmāniNG

▶adjective
1 still existing, present, or in use; surviving: Lilly was my last remaining close relative 
the few remaining employees are working part-time.
2 not yet used, dealt with, or resolved; outstanding: they advertised for any remaining creditors to come forward.
3 still to happen; future: it will likely take six wins in the eight remaining games.

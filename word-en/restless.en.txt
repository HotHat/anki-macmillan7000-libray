restless 
ˈres(t)ləs

▶adjective (of a person or animal) unable to rest or relax as a result of anxiety or boredom: the audience grew restless and inattentive. 
• offering no physical or emotional rest; involving constant activity or motion: a restless night. DERIVATIVES restlessly

analyze 
ˈanlˌīz 
(British analyse) 
▶verb [with object] examine methodically and in detail the constitution or structure of (something, especially information), typically for purposes of explanation and interpretation: we need to analyze our results more clearly. 
• discover or reveal (something) through detailed examination: I intend to analyze the sexism in such texts 
[with clause] : he tried to analyze exactly what was going on. 
• psychoanalyze (someone). 
• identify and measure the chemical constituents of (a substance or specimen). 
• Grammar resolve (a sentence) into its grammatical elements; parse. DERIVATIVES analyzable

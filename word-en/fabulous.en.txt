fabulous 
ˈfabyələs

▶adjective extraordinary, especially extraordinarily large: fabulous riches. 
• informal amazingly good; wonderful: a fabulous two-week vacation. 
• having no basis in reality; mythical: fabulous creatures. DERIVATIVES fabulosity 
ˌfabyəˈläsədē 
noun. fabulousness nounORIGIN late Middle English (in the sense ‘known through fable, unhistorical’): from French fabuleux or Latin fabulosus ‘celebrated in fable,’ from fabula (see fable) .

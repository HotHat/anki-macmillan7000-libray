bicycle 
ˈbīsək(ə)l

▶noun a vehicle composed of two wheels held in a frame one behind the other, propelled by pedals and steered with handlebars attached to the front wheel.bicycles 
▶verb [no object] ride a bicycle in a particular direction: they had spent the day bicycling around the island. DERIVATIVES bicyclist 
ˈbīsəkləst 
nounORIGIN mid 19th century: from bi- ‘two’ + Greek kuklos ‘wheel.’

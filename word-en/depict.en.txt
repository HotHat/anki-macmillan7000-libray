depict 
dəˈpikt

▶verb [with object] show or represent by a drawing, painting, or other art form. 
• portray in words; describe: youth is depicted as a time of vitality and good health. DERIVATIVES depicter 
dəˈpiktər 
nounORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin depict- ‘portrayed,’ from the verb depingere, from de- ‘completely’ + pingere ‘to paint.’

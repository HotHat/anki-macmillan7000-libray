formation 
fôrˈmāSH(ə)n

▶noun
1 the action of forming or process of being formed: the formation of the Great Rift Valley.
2 a structure or arrangement of something: a cloud formation. 
• a formal arrangement of aircraft in flight or troops: a battle formation 
the helicopters hovered overhead in formation. 
• Geology an assemblage of rocks or series of strata having some common characteristic. DERIVATIVES formational 
-SHənl 
adjectiveORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin formation-, from formare ‘to form’ (see form) .

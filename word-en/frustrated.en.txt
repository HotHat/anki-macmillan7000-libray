frustrated 
ˈfrəˌstrādəd

▶adjective feeling or expressing distress and annoyance, especially because of inability to change or achieve something: young people get frustrated with the system. 
• [attributive] (of a person) unable to follow or be successful in a particular career: a frustrated actor. 
• [attributive] prevented from progressing, succeeding, or being fulfilled: our parents may want us to fulfill their own frustrated dreams. 
• (of a person or sexual desire) unfulfilled sexually: jealousies and frustrated passions. DERIVATIVES frustratedly adverb

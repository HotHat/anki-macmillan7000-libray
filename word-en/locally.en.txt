locally 
ˈlōkəlē

▶adverb
1 with reference to a particular area or one's neighborhood: I shop locally wherever possible 
locally produced goods 
he served on several committees both nationally and locally.
2 Computing in a way that applies or is only available for use in one part of a program: the service control function is installed locally. 
• in a way that can be accessed without the use of a network: should the data be stored locally or remotely?

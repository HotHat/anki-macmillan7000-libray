jog 
▶verb
1 he jogged along the road: run slowly, trot, lope, dog-trot; dated jog-trot.
2 something jogged her memory: stimulate, prompt, stir, activate, refresh; prod, jar, nudge.
▶noun
1 he set off at a jog: run, trot, lope, dog-trot; dated jog trot.
2 a jog in the road: bend, turn, curve, corner, zigzag, kink, dogleg.

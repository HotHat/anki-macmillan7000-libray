masculine 
ˈmaskyələn

▶adjective
1 having qualities or appearance traditionally associated with men, especially strength and aggressiveness: he is outstandingly handsome and robust, very masculine. 
• relating to men; male: a masculine voice.
2 Grammar of or denoting a gender of nouns and adjectives, conventionally regarded as male. 
▶noun (the masculine) the male sex or gender: the masculine as the norm. 
• Grammar a masculine word or form. DERIVATIVES masculinely adverbORIGIN late Middle English (in grammatical use): via Old French from Latin masculinus, from masculus ‘male.’

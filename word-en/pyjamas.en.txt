pajamas 
pəˈjäməz pəˈjaməz 
(British pyjamas) 
▶pl. noun a suit of loose pants and jacket or shirt for sleeping in: a pair of pajamas 
(as modifier pajama) : pajama bottoms. 
• [in singular] (pajama) a pair of loose pants tied by a drawstring around the waist, worn by both sexes in some Asian countries. DERIVATIVES pajamaed 
pəˈjäməd, -ˈjaməd 
(British pyjamaed) adjectiveORIGIN early 19th century: from Urdu and Persian, from pāy ‘leg’ + jāma ‘clothing.’

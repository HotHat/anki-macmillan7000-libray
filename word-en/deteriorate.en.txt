deteriorate 
dəˈtirēəˌrāt

▶verb [no object] become progressively worse: relations between the countries had deteriorated sharply 
(as adjective deteriorating) : deteriorating economic conditions. DERIVATIVES deteriorative 
-ˌrātiv 
adjectiveORIGIN late 16th century (used transitively in the sense ‘make worse’): from late Latin deteriorat- ‘worsened,’ from the verb deteriorare, from Latin deterior ‘worse.’

southern 
ˈsəT͟Hərn

▶adjective
1 [attributive] situated in the south or directed toward or facing the south: the southern hemisphere. 
• (of a wind) blowing from the south.
2 living in or originating from the south: the southern rural poor. 
• relating to or characteristic of the south or its inhabitants: a faintly southern accent. ORIGIN Old English sūtherne (see south, -ern) .

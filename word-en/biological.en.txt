biological 
ˌbīəˈläjək(ə)l 
(abbreviation biol.) 
▶adjective
1 relating to biology or living organisms. 
• relating to or involving the use of microorganisms or toxins of biological origin as weapons of war. 
• (of a detergent or other cleaning product) containing enzymes to assist the process of cleaning.
2 (of a member of a person's family) genetically related; related by blood: the rights of the biological father. 
▶noun (usually biologicals) a therapeutic substance, such as a vaccine or drug, derived from biological sources: an international biotechnology company with interests in biologicals, agriculture, and pharmaceutical products.

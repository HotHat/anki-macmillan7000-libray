CHap

▶verb (chaps, chapping, chapped) [no object] (of the skin) become cracked, rough, or sore, typically through exposure to cold weather. 
• [with object] (usually as adjective chapped) (of the wind or cold) cause (skin) to crack through exposure to cold weather: chapped lips. 
▶noun a cracked or sore patch on the skin. ORIGIN late Middle English: of unknown origin.

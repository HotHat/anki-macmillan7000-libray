pot 
▶noun
1 pots and pans: cooking utensil, pan, saucepan, casserole, stewpot, stockpot, kettle.
2 earthenware pots: flowerpot, planter, jardinière.
3 Jim raked in half the pot: bank, kitty, pool, purse, jackpot.
PHRASES
go to pot informal they've certainly let the old homestead go to pot: deteriorate, decline, degenerate, go to (rack and) ruin, go downhill, go to seed, become run-down; informal go to the dogs, go down the tubes, go haywire.

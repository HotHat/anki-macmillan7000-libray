negotiation 
nəˌɡōSHēˈāSH(ə)n

▶noun (also negotiations) discussion aimed at reaching an agreement: a worldwide ban is currently under negotiation 
negotiations between unions and employers 
negotiation of the deals. 
• the action or process of transferring ownership of a document. ORIGIN late 15th century (denoting an act of dealing with another person): from Latin negotiatio(n-), from the verb negotiari (see negotiate) .

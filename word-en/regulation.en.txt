regulation 
ˌreɡ(y)əˈlāSH(ə)n

▶noun
1 a rule or directive made and maintained by an authority: planning regulations. 
• [as modifier] in accordance with regulations; of the correct type: regulation army footwear. 
• [as modifier] informal of a familiar or predictable type; formulaic; standardized: a regulation Western parody.
2 the action or process of regulating or being regulated: the regulation of financial markets.

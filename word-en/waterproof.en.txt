waterproof 
ˈwôdərˌpro͞of ˈwädərˌpro͞of

▶adjective impervious to water: a waterproof hat. 
• not liable to be washed away by water: waterproof ink. 
▶noun British a garment, especially a coat, that keeps out water. 
▶verb [with object] make impervious to water. DERIVATIVES waterproofer noun. waterproofness noun

essay 
▶noun 
ˈesā 

1 a short piece of writing on a particular subject.
2 formal an attempt or effort: a misjudged essay. 
• a trial design of a postage stamp yet to be accepted. 
▶verb 
eˈsā 
[with object] formal attempt or try: essay a smile. ORIGIN late 15th century (as a verb in the sense ‘test the quality of’): alteration of assay, by association with Old French essayer, based on late Latin exagium ‘weighing,’ from the base of exigere ‘ascertain, weigh’; the noun (late 16th century) is from Old French essai ‘trial.’

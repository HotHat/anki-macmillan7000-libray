institutional 
ˌinstəˈt(y)o͞oSH(ə)n(ə)l

▶adjective of, in, or like an institution or institutions: institutional care 
an institutional investor. 
• unappealing or unimaginative: institutional chocolate-colored paint. 
• expressed or organized in the form of institutions: institutional religion. 
• established as a convention or norm in an organization or culture: institutional racism. 
• (of advertising) intended to create prestige rather than immediate sales. DERIVATIVES institutionalism

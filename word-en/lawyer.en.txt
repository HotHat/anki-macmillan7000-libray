lawyer 
ˈloiər

▶noun a person who practices or studies law; an attorney or a counselor. 
▶verb [no object] North American practice law; work as a lawyer: (as noun lawyering) : lawyering is a craft that takes a long time to become proficient at. 
• [with object] (of a lawyer) work on the legal aspects of (a contract, lawsuit, etc.): there is always a danger that the deal will be lawyered to death. DERIVATIVES lawyerly adjective

picnic 
ˈpikˌnik

▶noun an outing or occasion that involves taking a packed meal to be eaten outdoors. 
• a packed meal taken on an outing and eaten outdoors. 
▶verb (picnics, picnicking, picnicked) [no object] have or take part in a picnic. 
PHRASES
no picnic informal used of something difficult or unpleasant: chemotherapy is no picnic.DERIVATIVES picnicker

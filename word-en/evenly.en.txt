evenly 
ˈēvənlē

▶adverb
1 so as to present a flat or uniform surface or line: scoop cream onto the cake and spread evenly.
2 in equal numbers, amounts, or values: the jackpot will be split evenly among the holders of the three tickets. 
• with each having an equal chance: the match was evenly balanced throughout the first half 
an evenly contested game. 
• with equal space or time between each; regularly: a series of evenly spaced dots 
he was breathing evenly, fast asleep. 
• calmly and without strong emotion or variation: he speaks evenly and softly.
3 used to indicate a number that is divisible by two without a remainder: signals are numbered evenly, 2, 4, 6, 8, etc.

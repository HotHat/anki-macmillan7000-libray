finally 
ˈfīn(ə)lē

▶adverb after a long time, typically involving difficulty or delay: he finally arrived to join us. 
• as the last in a series of related events or objects: a referendum followed by local, legislative, and, finally, presidential elections. 
• [sentence adverb] used to introduce a final point or reason: finally, it is common knowledge that travel broadens the horizons. 
• in such a way as to put an end to doubt and dispute: to dispel finally the belief that auditors were clients of the company.

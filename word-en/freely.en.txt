freely 
ˈfrēlē

▶adverb not under the control of another; as one wishes: I roamed freely. 
• without restriction or interference: air can freely circulate. 
• in copious or generous amounts: she drank freely to keep up her courage. 
• openly and honestly: you may speak freely. 
• willingly and readily; without compulsion: I freely confess to this failing.

excessive 
ikˈsesiv

▶adjective more than is necessary, normal, or desirable; immoderate: he was drinking excessive amounts of brandy. DERIVATIVES excessiveness 
ikˈsesivnəs 
nounORIGIN late Middle English: from Old French excessif, -ive, from medieval Latin excessivus, from Latin excedere ‘surpass’ (see exceed) .

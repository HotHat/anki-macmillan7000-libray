cruel 
ˈkro͞o(ə)l

▶adjective (crueler, cruelest; British crueller, cruellest) willfully causing pain or suffering to others, or feeling no concern about it: people who are cruel to animals 
a cruel remark. 
• causing pain or suffering: the winters are long, hard, and cruel. ORIGIN Middle English: via Old French from Latin crudelis, related to crudus (see crude) .

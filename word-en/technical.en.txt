technical 
ˈteknək(ə)l

▶adjective
1 relating to a particular subject, art, or craft, or its techniques: technical terms 
a test of an artist's technical skill. 
• (especially of a book or article) requiring special knowledge to be understood: a technical report.
2 of, involving, or concerned with applied and industrial sciences: an important technical achievement.
3 resulting from mechanical failure: a technical fault.
4 according to a strict application or interpretation of the law or rules: the arrest was a technical violation of the treaty. 
▶noun Basketball short for technical foul.

describe 
dəˈskrīb

▶verb [with object]
1 give an account in words of (someone or something), including all the relevant characteristics, qualities, or events: the police said the man was described as white,
6 ft. tall, with mousy, cropped hair.
2 mark out or draw (a geometric figure): on the diameter of a circle an equilateral triangle is described. DERIVATIVES describable 
dəˈskrībəb(ə)l 
adjective. describer 
dəˈskrībər 
nounORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin describere, from de- ‘down’ + scribere ‘write.’

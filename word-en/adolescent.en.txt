adolescent 
ˌadəˈles(ə)nt

▶adjective (of a young person) in the process of developing from a child into an adult. 
• relating to or characteristic of adolescence: his adolescent years 
adolescent problems. 
▶noun an adolescent boy or girl. ORIGIN late Middle English (as a noun): via French from Latin adolescent- ‘coming to maturity,’ from adolescere, from ad- ‘to’ + alescere ‘grow, grow up,’ from alere ‘nourish.’ The adjective dates from the late 18th century.

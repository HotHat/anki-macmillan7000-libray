recount
1 
riˈkount

▶verb [reporting verb] tell someone about something; give an account of an event or experience: [with object] : I recounted the tale to Steve 
[with clause] : he recounts how they often talked of politics. 
▶noun an act or instance of giving an account of an event or experience: one woman's recount of a prolonged battle with “huge centipedes.” ORIGIN late Middle English: from Old Northern French reconter ‘tell again,’ based on Old French counter (see count1) .

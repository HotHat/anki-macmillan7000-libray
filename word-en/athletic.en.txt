athletic 
ˌaTH(ə)ˈledik

▶adjective
1 physically strong, fit, and active: big, muscular, athletic boys.
2 [attributive] relating to athletes or athletics: athletic events 
an athletic club. DERIVATIVES athletically 
aTH(ə)ˈledək(ə)lē 
adverbORIGIN mid 17th century: from French athlétique or Latin athleticus, from Greek athlētikos, from athlētēs (see athlete) .

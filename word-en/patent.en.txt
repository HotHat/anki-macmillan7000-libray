patent 
▶noun 
ˈpatnt 

1 a government authority or license conferring a right or title for a set period, especially the sole right to exclude others from making, using, or selling an invention: he took out a patent for an improved steam hammer. Compare with letters patent.
2 short for patent leather.

1

2 Medicine

3 
ˈpatnt

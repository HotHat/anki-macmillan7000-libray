slender 
ˈslendər

▶adjective (slenderer, slenderest)
1 (of a person or part of the body) gracefully thin: her slender neck. 
• (especially of a rod or stem) of small girth or breadth: slender iron railings.
2 (of something abstract) barely sufficient in amount or basis: a slender majority of four. DERIVATIVES slenderly 
ˈslendərlē 
adverb. slenderness 
ˈslendərnəs 
nounORIGIN late Middle English: of unknown origin.

increase 
▶verb 
inˈkrēs 
become or make greater in size, amount, intensity, or degree: [no object] : car use is increasing at an alarming rate 
[with object] : we are aiming to increase awareness of social issues 
(as adjective increasing) : the increasing numbers of students. 
▶noun 
ˈinkrēs 
an instance of growing or making greater: an increase from sixteen to eighteen clubs 
some increase in inflation. 
PHRASES
on the increase becoming greater, more common, or more frequent.DERIVATIVES increasable 
inˈkrēsəb(ə)l 
adjectiveORIGIN Middle English (formerly also as encrease): from Old French encreistre, from Latin increscere, from in- ‘into’ + crescere ‘grow.’

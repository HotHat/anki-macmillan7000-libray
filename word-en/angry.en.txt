angry 
ˈaNGɡrē

▶adjective (angrier, angriest) having a strong feeling of or showing annoyance, displeasure, or hostility; full of anger: why are you angry with me? 
an angry customer 
Christine had made him angry 
I'm angry that she didn't call me. 
• (of the sea or sky) stormy, turbulent, or threatening: the wild, angry sea. 
• (of a wound or sore) red and inflamed: her skin was splotched with angry red burns.

friendly 
ˈfren(d)lē

▶adjective (friendlier, friendliest) kind and pleasant: they were friendly to me 
she gave me a friendly smile. 
• [predicative] (of a person) on good or affectionate terms: I was friendly with one of the local farmers. 
• (of a contest) not seriously or unpleasantly competitive or divisive: friendly rivalry between the two schools. 
• Soccer (of a match) not affecting a team's league standings. 
• [in combination] denoting something that is adapted for or is not harmful to a specified thing: an environment-friendly agronomic practice. 
• favorable or serviceable: trees providing a friendly stage on which seedlings begin to grow. 
• Military (of troops or equipment) of, belonging to, or in alliance with one's own forces. 
▶adverb (also friendlily 
-ləlē
) in a friendly manner.

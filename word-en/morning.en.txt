morning 
ˈmôrniNG

▶noun the period of time between midnight and noon, especially from sunrise to noon: I toiled in the fields from morning till night 
it was a little after eight in the morning 
it was a beautiful sunny morning. 
• sunrise: a hint of steely light showed that morning was on its way. 
▶adverb (mornings) informal every morning: mornings, she'd sleep late. 
▶exclam. informal short for good morning. 
PHRASES
morning, noon, and night all the time.ORIGIN Middle English: from morn, on the pattern of evening.

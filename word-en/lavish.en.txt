lavish 
▶adjective
1 lavish parties: sumptuous, luxurious, costly, expensive, opulent, grand, splendid, rich, fancy, posh; informal fancy-schmancy. ANTONYMS meager.
2 lavish hospitality: generous, liberal, bountiful, openhanded, unstinting, unsparing, free, munificent, extravagant, prodigal. ANTONYMS frugal.
3 lavish amounts of champagne: abundant, copious, plentiful, liberal, prolific, generous; literary plenteous. ANTONYMS scant.
▶verb she lavished money on her children: give freely to, spend generously on, bestow on, heap on, shower with.

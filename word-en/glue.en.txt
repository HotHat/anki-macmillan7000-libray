glue 
ɡlo͞o

▶noun an adhesive substance used for sticking objects or materials together. 
▶verb (glues, gluing or glueing, glued) [with object] fasten or join with or as if with glue: the wood is cut up into small pieces which are then glued together. 
• (be glued to) informal be paying very close attention to (something, especially a television or computer screen): I was glued to the television when the Olympics were on. DERIVATIVES gluelike

channel 
▶noun

2 the water ran down a channel: duct, gutter, conduit, trough, culvert, sluice, spillway, race, drain.
3 a channel for their extraordinary energy: use, medium, vehicle, way of harnessing; release (mechanism), safety valve, vent.
4 a channel of communication: means, medium, instrument, mechanism, agency, vehicle, route, avenue.
▶verb

2 many countries channel their aid through charities: convey, transmit, conduct, direct, guide, relay, pass on, transfer.

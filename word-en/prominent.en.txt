prominent 
ˈprämənənt

▶adjective
1 important; famous: she was a prominent member of the city council.
2 projecting from something; protuberant: a man with big, prominent eyes like a lobster's. 
• situated so as to catch the attention; noticeable: the new housing developments are prominent landmarks. ORIGIN late Middle English (in the sense ‘projecting’): from Latin prominent- ‘jutting out,’ from the verb prominere. Compare with eminent .

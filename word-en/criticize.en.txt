criticize 
ˈkridəˌsīz

▶verb [with object]
1 indicate the faults of (someone or something) in a disapproving way: states criticized the failure to provide an adequate and permanent compensation 
technicians were criticized for defective workmanship.
2 form and express a sophisticated judgment of (a literary or artistic work): a literary text may be criticized on two grounds: the semantic and the expressive. DERIVATIVES criticizable 
ˈkridəˌsīzəb(ə)l

bandage 
ˈbandij

▶noun a strip of material used to bind a wound or to protect an injured part of the body: her leg was swathed in bandages 
a sterile adhesive bandage with nonstick pad. 
▶verb [with object] bind (a wound or a part of the body) with a protective strip of material: bandage the foot so that the ankle is supported 
the doctors bandaged up his wounds. DERIVATIVES bandaging

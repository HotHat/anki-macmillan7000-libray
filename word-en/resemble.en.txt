resemble 
rəˈzembəl

▶verb [with object] have qualities or features, especially those of appearance, in common with (someone or something); look or seem like: some people resemble their dogs 
they seemed to resemble each other closely. DERIVATIVES resembler 
-blər 
noun ( rare)ORIGIN Middle English: from Old French resembler, based on Latin similare (from similis ‘like’).

underscore 
▶noun 
ˈəndərˌskôr 
a line drawn under a word or phrase for emphasis. 
• (on a computer or typewriter keyboard) a short horizontal line (_) on the baseline. 
▶verb 
ˈəndərˌskôr ˌəndərˈskôr 
[with object] underline (something). 
• emphasize: the company underscored the progress made with fuel cells.

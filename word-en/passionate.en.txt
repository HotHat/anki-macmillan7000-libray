passionate 
ˈpaSH(ə)nət

▶adjective showing or caused by strong feelings or a strong belief: passionate pleas for help 
he's passionate about football. 
• showing or caused by intense feelings of sexual love: a passionate kiss. DERIVATIVES passionateness nounORIGIN late Middle English (also in the senses ‘easily moved to passion’ and ‘enraged’): from medieval Latin passionatus ‘full of passion,’ from passio (see passion) .

underline 
▶verb 
ˌəndərˈlīn 
[with object] draw a line under (a word or phrase) to give emphasis or indicate special type. 
• emphasize (something): the improvement in retail sales was underlined by these figures. 
▶noun 
ˈəndərlīn 

1 a line drawn under a word or phrase, especially for emphasis.
2 the line of the lower part of an animal's body.

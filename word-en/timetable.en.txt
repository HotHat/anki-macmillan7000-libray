timetable 
ˈtīmˌtābəl

▶noun a chart showing the departure and arrival times of trains, buses, or planes. 
• a plan of times at which events are scheduled to take place, especially toward a particular end: the timetable for a military coup. 
▶verb [with object] schedule (something) to take place at a particular time: German lessons were timetabled on Wednesday and Friday.

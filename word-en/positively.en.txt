positively 
ˈpäzədivlē ˈpäztivlē

▶adverb in a positive way, in particular: 
• with certainty; so as to leave no room for doubt: experts could not positively identify the voices. 
• [as submodifier] used to emphasize that something is the case, even though it may seem surprising or unlikely: some of the diets may be positively dangerous.

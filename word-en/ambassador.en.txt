ambassador 
amˈbasədər

▶noun an accredited diplomat sent by a country as its official representative to a foreign country: the French ambassador to Portugal. 
• a person who acts as a representative or promoter of a specified activity: he is a good ambassador for the industry. DERIVATIVES ambassadorial 
amˌbasəˈdôrēəl 
adjective. ambassadorship 
amˈbasədərˌSHip

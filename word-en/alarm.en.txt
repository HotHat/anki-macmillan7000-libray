alarm 
▶noun
1 we spun around in alarm: fear, anxiety, apprehension, trepidation, nervousness, unease, distress, agitation, consternation, disquiet, perturbation, fright, panic. ANTONYMS calmness, composure.
2 sound the alarm 
a smoke alarm: warning, alert, distress signal; siren, bell, horn, whistle, air horn; archaic tocsin.
▶verb the news had alarmed her: frighten, scare, panic, unnerve, distress, agitate, upset, disconcert, shock, dismay, disturb; informal rattle, spook, scare the living daylights out of. ANTONYMS calm, reassure.

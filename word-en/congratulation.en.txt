congratulation 
kənˌɡraCHəˈlāSH(ə)n kənˌɡrajəˈlāSH(ə)n

▶noun 
• (congratulations) words expressing praise for an achievement or good wishes on a special occasion: our congratulations to the winners 
[as exclamation] : congratulations on a job well done! the action of expressing congratulations: he began pumping the hand of his son in congratulation. ORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin congratulatio(n-), from the verb congratulari (see congratulate) .

flock 
▶noun
1 a flock of sheep: herd, drove.
2 a flock of birds: flight, congregation, covey, clutch.
3 flocks of people: crowd, throng, horde, mob, rabble, mass, multitude, host, army, pack, swarm, sea; informal gaggle.
▶verb
1 people flocked around the stars outside the stage door: gather, collect, congregate, assemble, converge, mass, crowd, throng, cluster, swarm.
2 tourists flock to the place: stream, go in large numbers, swarm, crowd, troop.

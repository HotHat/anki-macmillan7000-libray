eastern 
ˈēstərn

▶adjective
1 [attributive] situated in the east, or directed toward or facing the east: eastern Long Island 
the eastern slopes of the mountain. 
• (of a wind) blowing from the east.
2 (usually Eastern) living in or originating from the east, in particular the regions or countries lying to the east of Europe: an Eastern monk. 
• relating to or characteristic of the East or its inhabitants: an eastern religion. DERIVATIVES easternmost 
ˈēstərnˌmōst 
adjectiveORIGIN Old English ēasterne (as east, -ern) .

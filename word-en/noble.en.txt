noble 
▶adjective
1 a noble family: aristocratic, patrician, blue-blooded, high-born, titled; archaic gentle. ANTONYMS humble.
2 a noble cause: righteous, virtuous, good, honorable, upright, decent, worthy, moral, ethical, reputable; magnanimous, unselfish, generous. ANTONYMS dishonorable.
3 a noble pine forest: magnificent, splendid, grand, stately, imposing, dignified, proud, striking, impressive, majestic, glorious, awesome, monumental, statuesque, regal, imperial. ANTONYMS unimpressive.
▶noun Scottish nobles: aristocrat, nobleman, noblewoman, lord, lady, peer, peeress, peer of the realm, patrician; informal aristo.

sprint

▶verb [no object] run at full speed over a short distance: I saw Charlie sprinting through the traffic toward me. 
▶noun an act or short spell of running at full speed. 
• a short, fast race in which the competitors run a distance of 400 meters or less: the 100 meters sprint. 
• a short, fast race or exercise in cycling, swimming, horse racing, etc. ORIGIN late 18th century (as a dialect term meaning ‘a bound or spring’): related to Swedish spritta .

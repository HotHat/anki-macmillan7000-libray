skull 
skəl

▶noun a framework of bone or cartilage enclosing the brain of a vertebrate; the skeleton of a person's or animal's head. 
• informal a person's head or brain: a skull crammed with too many thoughts. 
▶verb [with object] hit (someone) on the head.

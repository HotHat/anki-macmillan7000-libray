blaze 
▶noun
1 firemen fought the blaze: fire, flames, conflagration, inferno, holocaust; forest fire, wildfire, bush fire.
2 a blaze of light: glare, gleam, flash, burst, flare, streak, radiance, brilliance, beam.
▶verb
1 the fire blazed for hours: burn, be alight, be on fire, be in flames, flame.
2 headlights blazed: shine, flash, flare, glare, gleam, glint, dazzle, glitter, glisten.

student 
ˈst(y)o͞odnt

▶noun a person who is studying at a school or college. 
• [as modifier] denoting someone who is studying in order to enter a particular profession: a group of student nurses. 
• a person who takes an interest in a particular subject: a student of the free market. DERIVATIVES studentship 
ˈst(y)o͞odntˌSHip 
noun (British). studenty adjective (British informal)ORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin student- ‘applying oneself to,’ from the verb studere, related to studium ‘painstaking application.’

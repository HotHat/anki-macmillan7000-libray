acceptable 
əkˈseptəb(ə)l

▶adjective
1 able to be agreed on; suitable: has tried to find a solution acceptable to everyone. 
• adequate; satisfactory: an acceptable substitute for champagne. 
• pleasing; welcome: some coffee would be most acceptable.
2 able to be tolerated or allowed: pollution in the city had reached four times the acceptable level.

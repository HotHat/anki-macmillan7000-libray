uneven 
ˌənˈēvən

▶adjective not level or smooth: the floors are cracked and uneven. 
• not regular, consistent, or equal: the uneven distribution of resources. 
• (of a contest) not equally balanced: Fran struggled briefly but soon gave up the uneven contest. DERIVATIVES unevenness 
ˌənˈēv(ə)nnəs 
nounORIGIN Old English unefen ‘not corresponding exactly’ (see un-1, even1) .

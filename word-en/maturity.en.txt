maturity 
məˈCHo͝orədē məˈto͝orədē

▶noun the state, fact, or period of being mature: their experience, maturity, and strong work ethic 
the delicate style of his maturity. 
• the time when an insurance policy, security, etc., matures. 
• an insurance policy, security, etc., having a fixed maturity date. ORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin maturitas, from maturus (see mature) .

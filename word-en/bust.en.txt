bust
1 
bəst

▶noun
1 a woman's chest as measured around her breasts: a 36-inch bust. 
• a woman's breasts, especially considered in terms of their size: selecting clothes that would minimize her big bust.
2 a sculpture of a person's head, shoulders, and chest. ORIGIN mid 17th century (denoting the upper part or torso of a large sculpture): from French buste, from Italian busto, from Latin bustum ‘tomb, sepulchral monument.’

patient 
ˈpāSHənt

▶adjective able to accept or tolerate delays, problems, or suffering without becoming annoyed or anxious: be patient, your time will come. 
▶noun
1 a person receiving or registered to receive medical treatment.
2 Linguistics the semantic role of a noun phrase denoting something that is affected or acted upon by the action of a verb. ORIGIN Middle English: from Old French, from Latin patient- ‘suffering,’ from the verb pati .

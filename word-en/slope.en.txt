slope 
▶noun
1 the slope of the roof: gradient, incline, angle, slant, inclination, pitch, decline, ascent, declivity, rise, fall, tilt, tip, downslope, upslope, grade, downgrade, upgrade.
2 a grassy slope: hill, hillside, hillock, bank, sidehill, escarpment, scarp; literary steep.
3 the ski slopes: piste, run, trail.
▶verb the garden sloped down to a stream: slant, incline, tilt; drop away, fall away, decline, descend, shelve, lean; rise, ascend, climb.

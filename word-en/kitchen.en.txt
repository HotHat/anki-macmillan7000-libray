kitchen 
ˈkiCH(ə)n

▶noun
1 a room or area where food is prepared and cooked. 
• a set of fixtures, cabinets, and appliances that are sold together and installed in a room where food is prepared: a complete kitchen at a bargain price. 
• cuisine: the dried shrimp pastes of the Thai kitchen.
2 informal the percussion section of an orchestra.
3 [as modifier] (of a language) in an uneducated or domestic form: kitchen Swahili. ORIGIN Old English cycene; related to Dutch keuken and German Küche, based on Latin coquere ‘to cook.’

lively 
ˈlīvlē

▶adjective (livelier, liveliest) full of life and energy; active and outgoing: she joined a lively team of reporters. 
• (of a place or atmosphere) full of activity and excitement: Barcelona's many lively bars. 
• intellectually stimulating or perceptive: a lively discussion 
her lively mind. 
• (of a vessel) buoyant and responsive in a sea. 
PHRASES
look lively see look. DERIVATIVES livelily 
-ləlē 
adverbORIGIN Old English līflic ‘living, animate’ (see life, -ly1) .

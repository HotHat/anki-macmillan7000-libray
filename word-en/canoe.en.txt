canoe 
kəˈno͞o

▶noun a narrow, keelless boat with pointed ends, propelled by a paddle or paddles.canoe 
▶verb (canoes, canoeing, canoed) [no object] travel in or paddle a canoe: he had once canoed down the Nile. DERIVATIVES canoeist 
kəˈno͞oəst 
nounORIGIN mid 16th century: from Spanish canoa, from Arawak, from Carib canaoua .

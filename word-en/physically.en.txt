physically 
ˈfizik(ə)lē

▶adverb
1 in a manner relating to the body as opposed to the mind: physically demanding work 
I found her physically attractive. 
• in a way that involves bodily contact or activity: he physically assaulted a colleague.
2 in a way that relates to the real world and things perceived through the senses as opposed to the mind: cyberspace is a virtual place; it does not physically exist. 
• in terms of what is possible in the real world: it is physically impossible to check every car.

never 
ˈnevər

▶adverb
1 at no time in the past or future; on no occasion; not ever: they had never been camping in their lives 
I will never ever forget it.
2 not at all: he never turned up. 
PHRASES
never fear see fear. never mind see mind. never say die see die1. well I never! informal expressing great surprise or indignation: Well I never—that's not like you!ORIGIN Old English nǣfre, from ne ‘not’ + ǣfre ‘ever.’

roof 
ro͞of ro͝of

▶noun (plural roofs) the structure forming the upper covering of a building or vehicle.roofs 
• the top inner surface of a covered area or space; the ceiling: the roof of the cave fell in. 
• used to signify a house or other building, especially in the context of hospitality or shelter: helping those without a roof over their heads 
they slept under the same roof. 
▶verb [with object] (usually be roofed) cover with a roof: the yard had been roughly roofed over with corrugated iron. 
• function as the roof of: fan vaults roof these magnificent buildings.

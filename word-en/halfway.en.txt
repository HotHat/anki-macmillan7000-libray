halfway 
ˈhafˌwā

▶adverb & adjective at or to a point equidistant between two others: [as adverb] : he stopped halfway down the passage 
[as adjective] : she reached the halfway point. 
• in the middle of a period of time: [as adverb] : halfway through the night. 
• [as adverb] to some extent: I'm incapable of doing anything even halfway decent. 
PHRASES
meet someone halfway see meet1.

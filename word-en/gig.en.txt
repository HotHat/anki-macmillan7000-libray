gig
1 
ɡiɡ

▶noun
1 chiefly historical a light two-wheeled carriage pulled by one horse.
2 a light, fast, narrow boat adapted for rowing or sailing. 
▶verb [no object] travel in a gig. ORIGIN late 18th century: apparently a transferred sense of obsolete gig ‘a flighty girl,’ which was also applied to various objects or devices that whirled.

publicly 
ˈpəblik(ə)lē

▶adverb so as to be seen by other people; in public: some weep publicly. 
• [often sentence adverb] used in reference to views expressed to others and not necessarily genuinely felt: publicly, officials criticized the resolution, but privately they thought it tolerable. 
• by a government or the public rather than an independent, commercial company: publicly funded organizations 
a publicly owned company. usage: Note that the spelling is publicly, ending with the suffix -ly, not publically.

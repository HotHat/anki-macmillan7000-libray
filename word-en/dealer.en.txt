dealer 
ˈdēlər

▶noun
1 a person or business that buys and sells goods: a car dealer. 
• a person who buys and sells shares, securities, or other financial assets as a principal (rather than as a broker or agent). See also broker-dealer. 
• informal a person who buys and sells drugs: he posed as a dealer willing to buy heroin.
2 the player who distributes the cards at the start of a game or hand.

communicative 
kəˈmyo͞onəˌkādiv kəˈmyo͞onikədiv

▶adjective ready to talk or impart information: the patient was alert and communicative. 
• relating to the conveyance or exchange of information: the communicative process in literary texts. DERIVATIVES communicatively adverbORIGIN late Middle English: from late Latin communicativus, from communicat- ‘shared,’ from the verb communicare (see communicate) .

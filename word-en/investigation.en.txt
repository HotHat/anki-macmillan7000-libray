investigation 
inˌvestəˈɡāSH(ə)n

▶noun the action of investigating something or someone; formal or systematic examination or research: he is under investigation for receiving illicit funds. 
• a formal inquiry or systematic study: an investigation has been launched into the potential impact of the oil spill. DERIVATIVES investigational 
-SHənl 
adjectiveORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin investigatio(n-), from the verb investigare (see investigate) .

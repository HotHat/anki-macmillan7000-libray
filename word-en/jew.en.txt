Jew 
jo͞o

▶noun a member of the people and cultural community whose traditional religion is Judaism and who trace their origins through the ancient Hebrew people of Israel to Abraham. 
▶verb [with object] (jew someone down) offensive bargain with someone in a miserly or petty way. ORIGIN Middle English: from Old French juiu, via Latin from Greek Ioudaios, via Aramaic from Hebrew yĕhūḏī, from yĕhūḏāh ‘Judah’ (see Judah) .

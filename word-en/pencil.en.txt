pencil 
▶noun
1 a sharpened pencil: lead pencil, mechanical pencil, colored pencil; grease pencil; eyebrow pencil, lip pencil.
2 a pencil of light: beam, ray, shaft, finger, gleam.
▶verb
1 he penciled his name inside the cover: write, write down, jot down, scribble, note, take down.
2 pencil a line along the top of the molding: draw, trace, sketch.

erosion 
əˈrōZHən

▶noun the process of eroding or being eroded by wind, water, or other natural agents: the problem of soil erosion. 
• the gradual destruction or diminution of something: the erosion of support for the party. 
• Medicine the gradual destruction of tissue or tooth enamel by physical or chemical action. 
• Medicine a place where surface tissue has been gradually destroyed: patients with gastric erosions. DERIVATIVES erosional

afternoon 
ˌaftərˈno͞on

▶noun the time from noon or lunchtime to evening: I telephoned this afternoon 
I'll be back at three in the afternoon 
she worked on Tuesday afternoons 
[as modifier] : the afternoon sunshine. 
▶adverb (afternoons) informal in the afternoon; every afternoon. 
▶exclam. informal short for good afternoon.

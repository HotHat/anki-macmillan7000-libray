brake
1 
brāk

▶noun a device for slowing or stopping a moving vehicle, typically by applying pressure to the wheels: he slammed on his brakes 
[as modifier] : a brake pedal. 
• a thing that slows or hinders a process: managers have a duty to put the brakes on growth when it is unsustainable. 
▶verb [no object] make a moving vehicle slow down or stop by using a brake: drivers who brake abruptly 
(as adjective braking) : an anti-lock braking system. ORIGIN late 18th century: of unknown origin.

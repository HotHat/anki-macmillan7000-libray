survival 
sərˈvīvəl

▶noun the state or fact of continuing to live or exist, typically in spite of an accident, ordeal, or difficult circumstances: the animal's chances of survival were pretty low 
figurative : he was fighting for his political survival. 
• an object or practice that has continued to exist from an earlier time: his shorts were a survival from his army days. 
PHRASES
survival of the fittest Biology the continued existence of organisms that are best adapted to their environment, with the extinction of others, as a concept in the Darwinian theory of evolution. Compare with natural selection.

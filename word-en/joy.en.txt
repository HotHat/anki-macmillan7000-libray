▶noun a feeling of great pleasure and happiness: tears of joy 
the joy of being alive. 
• a thing that causes joy: the joys of Manhattan. 
▶verb [no object] literary rejoice: I felt shame that I had ever joyed in his discomfiture or pain. ORIGIN Middle English: from Old French joie, based on Latin gaudium, from gaudere ‘rejoice.’

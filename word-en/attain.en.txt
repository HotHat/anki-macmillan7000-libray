attain 
əˈtān

▶verb [with object] succeed in achieving (something that one desires and has worked for): clarify your objectives and ways of attaining them 
he attained the rank of admiral 
human beings can attain happiness. 
• reach (a specified age, size, or amount): dolphins can attain remarkable speeds in water. ORIGIN Middle English (in the senses ‘bring to justice’ and ‘reach (a state)’): from Old French ateindre, from Latin attingere, from ad- ‘at, to’ + tangere ‘to touch.’

praise 
prāz

▶verb [with object] express warm approval or admiration of: we can't praise Chris enough—he did a brilliant job. 
• express one's respect and gratitude toward (a deity), especially in song: we praise God for past blessings. 
▶noun the expression of approval or admiration for someone or something: the audience was full of praise for the whole production. 
• the expression of respect and gratitude as an act of worship: give praise to God.

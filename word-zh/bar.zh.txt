bar 
BrE bɑː, AmE bɑr 

A.  noun 
① (strip of wood, metal) 长条 chángtiáo the gate had a bar across it 大门上着门闩 behind bars 在狱中 
② (block) (of soap, chocolate etc.) 块 kuài a bar of gold 一根金条 
③ (place for drinking) 酒吧 jiǔbā 
④ (counter) 吧台 bātái 
⑤ (obstacle) 障碍 zhàng'ài a bar on people coming here to live 人们来这里居住的障碍 
⑥ Law (in court) the bar 围栏 wéilán the prisoner at the bar 受审讯的刑事被告 
⑦ the Bar Law (profession) 大律师职业 dàlǜshī zhíyè to be called to the Bar 获得大律师资格 
⑧ BrE Sport (crossbar) 球门横梁 qiúmén héngliáng 
⑨ Music 小节 xiǎojié 
⑩ BrE (in electric fire) 供热电阻丝 gōngrè diànzǔsī 
⑪ (of light, colour) 带 dài bars of sunlight 一道道阳光 a bar of red across the sky 横贯天空的一抹红 
B.  transitive verb(present participle etc. -rr-) 
① (fasten) 闩上 shuānshang ‹door›
② (block) 阻挡 zǔdǎng ‹way›the police barred the entrance 警察封锁了入口 
③ (exclude) 禁止 jìnzhǐ to bar [somebody] from doing [something]; 禁止某人做某事 a no holds barred contest 激烈的竞争 
C.  preposition 除…外 chú… wài they all turned up to the party bar one 除一人缺席外，他们都出席了晚会 bar none 没有例外地

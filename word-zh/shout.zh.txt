shout 
BrE ʃaʊt, AmE ʃaʊt 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (speak at full volume) 大喊 dà hǎn to shout at or to or for [somebody] to do [something]; 喊某人做某事 that's nothing much to shout about! 用不着这么嚷嚷！ to shout for help 高声呼救 to shout for joy/with excitement 高兴/兴奋得大喊大叫 
② (talk angrily) 愤怒地大声嚷嚷 fènnù de dàshēng rāngrang ; (scold) 呵斥 hēchì to shout at [somebody]; 呵斥某人 
B.  transitive verb 高声说出 gāoshēng shuōchū ‹answer, question›; 大声喊 dàshēng hǎn ‹order, slogan›people were shouting abuse 人们在破口大骂 to shout one's head off 拼命喊叫

① (loud cry) 喊叫 hǎnjiào a shout of protest 抗议的呼声 shout of excitement 兴奋的叫声 to give a shout of laughter/pain 发出笑声/痛苦的叫声 to give [somebody] a shout colloquial 告诉某人 give me a shout when you're ready 你准备好了就喊我一声

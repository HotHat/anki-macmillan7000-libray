curse 
BrE kəːs, AmE kərs 

A.  noun 
① (spell) 咒语 zhòuyǔ to put a curse on … 对…下咒 duì… xiàzhòu to be or live under a curse 受诅咒 
② (swear word) 骂人话 màrénhuà 
③ (affliction) 祸害 huòhai the curse of poverty 贫困这个祸患之源 to be a curse to [somebody]; 对某人来说是个祸害 
B.  transitive verb 
① (put curse on) 对…下咒 duì… xiàzhòu to be cursed; «person, place, enterprise» 被施了魔咒 to be cursed with [something]; 受某事物的折磨 
② (express anger about) 咒骂 zhòumà to curse [somebody]/[something] for [something]; 因某事物而咒骂某人/某事物 I curse the day that he was born! 他一出娘胎我就诅咒他！ 
C.  intransitive verb 诅咒 zǔzhòu to curse and swear 谩骂 to curse at [somebody]/[something]; 诅咒某人/某事物

permit 
A.  /BrE ˈpəːmɪt, AmE ˈpərmɪt
noun 
① Countable(document) 许可证 xǔkězhèng to apply for a permit 申请许可证 a gun/fishing permit 持枪/钓鱼许可证 
② Uncountable(official or formal permission) 许可 xǔkě by permit only 经许可者除外 
B.  /BrE pəˈmɪt, AmE pərˈmɪt
transitive verb(present participle etc. -tt-) 
① (authorize) 准许 zhǔnxǔ to permit [somebody] to do [something]; 允许某人做某事 to permit [somebody] [something]; 允许某人某事 are we permitted to smoke here? 这里可以抽烟吗？ 
② (make possible) 使有可能 shǐ yǒu kěnéng the good weather will permit people to get out and about 好天气将使人们能外出活动 
C.  /BrE pəˈmɪt, AmE pərˈmɪt
intransitive verb(present participle etc. -tt-) 
① (make possible) 使有可能 shǐ yǒu kěnéng weather permitting 天气好的话 if time/weather permits 如果时间够/天气好的话 
② formal (allow) to/to not permit of [something] «passage» 容许/不容许某事物 róngxǔ/bù róngxǔ mǒu shìwù each text permitted of several interpretations 每篇文本都可以有几种解释 
D.  /BrE pəˈmɪt, AmE pərˈmɪt
reflexive verb(present participle etc. -tt-) to permit oneself [something] 允许自己做某事 yǔnxǔ zìjǐ zuò mǒu shì she permitted herself one drink only 她只准自己喝一杯酒

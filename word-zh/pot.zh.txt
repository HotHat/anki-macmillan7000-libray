1 
BrE pɒt, AmE pɑt

② (container) (for jam, fish paste, yogurt, etc.) 罐 guàn (for tea, coffee) 壶 hú (for paint, glue, ink) 桶 tǒng 
③ (contents) (jam, fish paste, yogurt, etc.) 一罐 yī guàn (tea, coffee) 一壶 yī hú (paint, glue, ink) 一桶 yī tǒng 
④ (piece of pottery) 陶盆 táopén to make or throw a pot 制作陶罐

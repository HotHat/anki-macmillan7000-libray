satisfaction 
BrE satɪsˈfakʃ(ə)n, AmE ˌsædəsˈfækʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Uncountable and countable(pleasure) 满足感 mǎnzúgǎn to get or derive satisfaction out of or from [something] 从某事物中获得满足感 I assure you I derive no satisfaction whatever from your defeat 我向你保证，我对你的失败没有半点幸灾乐祸 to express one's satisfaction (at [something]) 表示（对某事物）满意 with satisfaction 心满意足地 
② Uncountable(fulfilment) 满足 mǎnzú (gratification) 满意 mǎnyì the satisfaction of your every wish is our desire 我们的目标是满足您每一个心愿 the satisfaction of consumer demand 消费者需求的满足 the matter was settled to everyone's satisfaction 这件事以令大家都满意的方式得到了解决 
③ Uncountable(reparation) 补偿 bǔcháng to seek/demand satisfaction 寻求/要求补偿 
④ UncountableLaw (payment of debt) 清偿 qīngcháng (fulfilment of obligation or claim) 履行 lǚxíng in full and final satisfaction 一次性清偿 he received no satisfaction from the company 他没有获得公司的赔偿 
⑤ Uncountable(self-satisfaction) 自满 zìmǎn a look or an expression of satisfaction 自鸣得意的表情

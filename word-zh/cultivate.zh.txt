③ (improve, educate) 培养 péiyǎng ‹interests, tastes›; 陶冶 táoyě ‹mind›
④ (develop) 逐渐形成 zhújiàn xíngchéng ‹sense of responsibility›; 建立 jiànlì ‹friendship, relationship›
⑤ (win friendship of) 结交 jiéjiāo ‹person›

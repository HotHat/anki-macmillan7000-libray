vision 
BrE ˈvɪʒ(ə)n, AmE ˈvɪʒən 

A.  noun 
① Uncountable(ability to see, sight) 视力 shìlì to have poor/good/blurred vision 视力差/好/模糊 to come into vision 进入视野 
② Uncountable(imaginative foresight) 眼力 yǎnlì to have/lack vision 有/缺乏眼力 a woman of vision 有远见的女子 
③ Countable(conception, idea) 想象 xiǎngxiàng to have visions of the future 想象未来 a romantic vision of the past 对于过去的浪漫想象 
④ Countable(mental picture) 幻象 huànxiàng to have visions of [something] 有某事物的幻象 I had visions of all sorts of disasters befalling them 我头脑中是各种各样的灾难降落到他们身上的画面 to appear to [somebody] in a vision 出现在某人的幻象中 
⑤ Countableespecially Religion (dream, hallucination) 幻觉 huànjué the answer came to him in a vision 他在神示中想到了这个答案 
⑥ Countable(image) 形象 xíngxiàng a vision of loveliness/hell 可爱/可怕的形象 
⑦ UncountableTelevision 图像 túxiàng sound and vision 音像 
B.  transitive verb AmE 想象 xiǎngxiàng to vision [something] 想象某事物

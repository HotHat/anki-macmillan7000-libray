remote 
BrE rɪˈməʊt, AmE rəˈmoʊt 

A.  adjective 
① (distant in space, time) 遥远的 yáoyuǎn de ‹place, planet›in the remote future/past 在遥远的将来/过去 in the remote distance 在很远的远处 stories of remote antiquity 远古时期的传说 
② (isolated) 偏远的 piānyuǎn de ‹farmhouse, village›
③ (distantly connected) 关系远的 guānxì yuǎn de remote relatives/ancestors 远房亲戚/远古先人 a remote cause 远因 a remote effect 间接后果 
④ (slight) 细微的 xìwēi de ‹resemblance, possibility, link›I haven't the remotest idea what you mean! 我根本不知道你是什么意思！ I don't see the remotest connection between the two incidents 我看不出两个事件之间有一丝一毫的联系 
⑤ (aloof) 冷傲的 lěng'ào de ‹person, manner›

effort 
BrE ˈɛfət, AmE ˈɛfərt 
noun 
① Countable and uncountable(energy) 努力 nǔlì to put a lot of effort into [something] 在某事上花费很多精力 it's not worth the effort to write 不值得费心去写 to spare no effort 不遗余力 
② Uncountable and countable(difficulty) 费劲 fèijìn with/without effort 费力/不费力地 it's an effort to climb the stairs 爬楼梯很吃力 
③ Countable(attempt) 试图 shìtú to make every effort 尽全力 in an effort to escape [something] 为逃避某事 efforts at [something]/doing [something]; 做某事的尝试 to thwart [somebody's] efforts 阻挠某人的企图 
④ Countable(initiative) 有组织的活动 yǒu zǔzhī de huódòng the war effort 战事 the United Nations peacekeeping effort 联合国维和行动 
⑤ Countable(result) 成就 chéngjiù [somebody's] latest effort 某人的最新成果

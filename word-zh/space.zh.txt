space

A.  noun 
① Uncountable(room) 空间 kōngjiān to take up a lot of space 占很大地方 the amount of space occupied by [something] 某物所占的空间 space to do [something]; 做某事所需的空间 to have some space to exercise in 有一点可供锻炼的地方 space for [something]/[somebody]; 可供某物占用/某人使用的空间 there's ample space for parking 有足够的地方停车 to leave some space for dessert 留出肚子吃甜点 living/storage space 生活/储存空间 to make space for [somebody]/[something] 为某人/某物腾出地方 a feeling or an impression of space 空旷感 a player in space Sport 单刀直入的运动员 he's got no sense of space 他没有空间感 to invade [somebody's] (personal) space 侵占某人的（个人）空间 (a journey in) space and time 时空（旅行） to stare into space 茫然直视前方 
② Uncountable(outer space) 太空 tàikōng 
③ Countable(empty place, gap) 空隙 kòngxì (blank area) 空地 kòngdì a small/narrow space 小的/窄的空当 some empty spaces on the bookshelves 书架上的几处空隙 there's a space next to me 我旁边空着 a space to park one's car 可供停车的地方 a fear of enclosed or confined spaces 对封闭空间的恐惧 leave a space after your name 在名字后面留空 large, empty spaces in the crowd 人群中大片的空隙 there's a space on page three for your article 第三版上有空隙可登你的文章 in/into a space 在/进入空隙中 a space of two metres (between …) （…间）两米的间隙 open spaces 开阔的空地 a country of wide open spaces 幅员广袤的国家 watch this space! colloquial 请关注本版！ 
④ Uncountable(amount of airtime) 时段 shíduàn (amount of paper) 版面 bǎnmiàn the newspaper gives space to local news/young writers 这家报纸刊登地方新闻/年轻作者的文章 a space for the public to air their views 供公众发表意见的时段 to buy/sell space in a newspaper (for advertising) 购买/出售报纸版面 in her book, she devotes a lot of space to this theory 她在书中花了大量篇幅阐述这一理论 
⑤ CountablePrinting 空白 kòngbái to type a space 键入一个空格 to leave a space 空一格 spaces between the words/lines 字间距/行间距 
⑥ Countable(interval of time) 期间 qījiān a short space of time 一会儿 after a space of about fifteen minutes 约15分钟后 there will be a space of two weeks between appointments 每隔两周约见一次 in or within the space of two hours 两小时内 
⑦ Countable(zone) 区域 qūyù green spaces 绿地 a women-only space 女性专用区 [somebody's] private space 某人的私人领域 
⑧ Uncountable(freedom, scope) 余地 yúdì to give each other space 互留余地 space to develop one's talents 发展才干的余地 
⑨ CountableMusic (on stave) 线间空白 xiàn jiān kòngbái

groan 
BrE ɡrəʊn, AmE ɡroʊn 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (in pain) 呻吟 shēnyín (in protest, pleasure) 发哼声 fā hēngshēng to groan in or with pain 痛苦地呻吟 he always groans at my jokes 他总是对我讲的笑话报以哼声 to groan inwardly 暗自叫苦 ‘I've been hit!’he groaned “我被打中了！”他呻吟着说 
② (complain) 抱怨 bàoyuàn to groan about or over [somebody]/[something]; 抱怨某人/某事物 poor people groaning beneath harsh taxation 被苛捐杂税压得透不过气来的贫民 
③ (creak) 发吱嘎声 fā zhīgā shēng to groan under the weight of [something] 在某物的重压下吱嘎作响 
④ figurative (be laden with) 受重压 shòu zhòngyā a table groaning with food 摆满食物的桌子 
B.  noun 
① (of pain) 呻吟 shēnyín ; (of pleasure, protest) 哼声 hēngshēng to give a groan of dismay 发出灰心的叹息声 
② (complaint) 抱怨声 bàoyuàn shēng 
③ figurative (creaking sound) 吱嘎声 zhīgā shēng

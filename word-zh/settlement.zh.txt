settlement 
BrE ˈsɛt(ə)lm(ə)nt, AmE ˈsɛdlmənt 
noun 
① Countable(village) 定居点 dìngjūdiǎn stone-age settlements 石器时代的村落 
② Uncountable(colonization, occupation) 殖民 zhímín 
③ Countable(agreement) 协议 xiéyì to reach a settlement (with [somebody]) （和某人）达成协议 
④ Uncountable(resolution) 解决 jiějué pending settlement of the financial details 财务细节问题的悬而未决 
⑤ Countable(arrangement between the parties to a lawsuit) 和解 héjiě [尤指庭外和解]
⑥ Uncountable(payment) 支付 zhīfù a cheque in settlement of your account or invoice 用于结账的支票 
⑦ Countable(conveyance of property) 财产转让 cáichǎn zhuǎnràng settlement on [somebody]; 对某人的财产让与 
⑧ Uncountable(subsidence) 沉降 chénjiàng

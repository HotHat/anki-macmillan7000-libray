rat 
BrE rat, AmE ræt 

A.  noun 
① Zoology 老鼠 lǎoshǔ (to look) like a drowned rat colloquial （看上去）像只落汤鸡 to smell a rat figurative 起疑心 
② colloquial pejorative (person) 卑鄙小人 bēibǐ xiǎorén 
B.  intransitive verb(present participle etc. -tt-) 
① colloquial (inform on) to rat on [somebody]; 告发某人 gàofā mǒu rén 
② dated (renege on) to rat on [something]; 背弃做某事的承诺 bèiqì zuò mǒu shì de chéngnuò they rated on the deal at the last minute 他们在最后一刻违约了 
C.  rats exclamation colloquial 该死 gāisǐ Rats! I've lost my ticket 真要命！我的票丢了

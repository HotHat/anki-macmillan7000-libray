③ figurative (record) 刻画 kèhuà to capture [something] on film 用胶片拍下某物 to capture a/[somebody's] likeness 画像/给某人画像 
B.  noun (of person) 俘获 fúhuò (of animal) 捕获 bǔhuò (of place) 占领 zhànlǐng

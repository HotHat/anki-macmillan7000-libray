satisfy 
BrE ˈsatɪsfʌɪ, AmE ˈsædəsˌfaɪ 

A.  transitive verb 
① (fulfil) 满足 mǎnzú ‹need, curiosity, desire›; 使满意 shǐ mǎnyì I need someone who can satisfy me both physically and intellectually 我需要一个身体和精神上都能使我满意的人 
② (comply with, meet) 符合 fúhé ‹terms, condition›; 达到 dádào ‹requirement›; 偿还 chánghuán ‹debt›our firm is unable to satisfy the demand for our goods 本公司无法满足对我们商品的需求 
③ (persuade, convince) 使…确信 shǐ… quèxìn ‹public›; 向…证实 xiàng… zhèngshí ‹jury, court›the employer wanted to satisfy himself as to the applicant's honesty 雇主想弄清楚求职者是否诚实 
B.  intransitive verb «food, book» 令人满意 lìng rén mǎnyì to fail to satisfy 未令人满意

smack
1 
BrE smak, AmE smæk 

A.  noun 
① Countable(sharp slap) 猛拍 měng pāi (sharp blow) 猛击 měng jī (slap on face) 打耳光 dǎ ěrguāng if you don't behave, you'll get a smack 如果你不规矩些，你就要挨揍 
② Countable(sharp sound) (of object) 撞击声 zhuàngjī shēng (of waves) 拍击声 pāijī shēng (of hand) 掌掴声 zhǎng guāi shēng the books fell to the floor with a smack 那些书啪的一声掉到了地板上 the smack of waves against the harbour wall 海浪拍打港口护堤的啪啪声 
③ Countable(loud kiss) 响吻 xiǎngwěn the boy's aunt gave him a big smack on the cheek 男孩的姨妈在他脸上亲了一个响吻 
④ Countable(flavour, taste) 味道 wèidao 
⑤ Countable(hint) 暗示 ànshì (trace) 痕迹 hénjì 
⑥ Uncountablecolloquial (heroin) 海洛因 hǎiluòyīn 
B.  transitive verb 
① (strike) 猛拍 měng pāi ‹person, hand, leg›; 掴 guāi ‹face›; 猛击 měng jī ‹ball›she is always smacking her children 她老是打孩子 she smacked him in the face 她扇了他一耳光 
② (open and close noisily) 咂 zā ‹lips›
③ (smash) 啪地一放 pā de yī fàng (drive) 冲撞 chōngzhuàng he smacked his hand down flat on the table 他用手拍了一下桌子 he smacked his car into a lamp post 他开车猛地撞在了灯柱上 
C.  intransitive verb 暗示 ànshì to smack of [something]; 有某事物的味道 this drink smacks of gin 这种饮料有杜松子酒味 their remarks smacked of bigotry 他们的话有点偏执 
D.  adverbcolloquial 
① (unexpectedly) 猛然 měngrán (sharply) 猛烈地 měngliè de he ran smack into his boss 他和上司撞了个满怀 the car went smack into the lamp post 汽车猛地撞在了灯柱上 he hit him smack on the jaw 他猛击他的下巴 
② (directly, precisely) 恰好 qiàhǎo smack in front of … 在…的正前方 he lives smack in the middle of town 他住在市中心

constitute 
BrE ˈkɒnstɪtjuːt, AmE ˈkɑnstəˌt(j)ut 
transitive verb 
① (represent) «act, words» 形成 xíngchéng ‹violation, admission›their action constituted a threat to national security 他们的行为是对国家安全的威胁 
② (form) 成为 chéngwéi they constituted the majority of the group 他们占这个集体的多数 
③ (compose) 构成 gòuchéng ‹part, whole›oxygen constitutes one of the elements of water 氧是构成水的元素之一 
④ formal (set up) 成立 chénglì ‹organization, body›

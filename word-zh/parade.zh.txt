parade 
BrE pəˈreɪd, AmE pəˈreɪd 

A.  noun 
① Countable(procession) 游行行列 yóuxíng hángliè a parade of floats 游行彩车队伍 
② Uncountable and countableMilitary 阅兵 yuèbīng to be on parade 在接受检阅 
③ Uncountable and countable(exhibition of models) 展示 zhǎnshì 
④ Uncountable and countablefigurative pejorative (display) 炫耀 xuànyào to make a parade of [something] 暴露 ‹emotion› 夸耀 kuāyào ‹knowledge›to be on parade 在炫耀自己 
⑤ Countable BrE (row of shops) 一排商店 yī pái shāngdiàn (street) 有一排商店的街 yǒu yī pái shāngdiàn de jiē South Parade 南商业街 
B.  transitive verb 
① (assemble) 使…列队 shǐ… lièduì ‹soldiers›
② (walk through) 在…招摇而行 zài… zhāoyáo ér xíng ‹street, corridor›
③ (display) 巡行展示 xúnxíng zhǎnshì ‹flags, signs›
④ especially pejorative (flaunt) 炫耀 xuànyào ‹knowledge, success, wealth›
⑤ (advertise) to parade [something]/[somebody] as [something] 把某物/某人标榜为某物 bǎ mǒu wù/mǒu rén biāobǎng wéi mǒu wù to parade oneself as a supporter of the party 宣称自己为该党的支持者 
C.  intransitive verb 
① Military «soldiers» 列队集合 lièduì jíhé 
② (march in procession) 游行 yóuxíng 
③ especially pejorative (strut) 招摇过市 zhāoyáo guò shì to parade up and down the street 在街上招摇地走来走去 
④ (masquerade) to parade as [something] 冒充某物 màochōng mǒu wù myth parading as fact 外表看似真实的神话 PHRASAL VERB parade about, parade around intransitive verb 四处招摇 sìchù zhāoyáo

provoke 
BrE prəˈvəʊk, AmE prəˈvoʊk 
transitive verb 
① (annoy) 激怒 jīnù the snake is quite harmless, unless provoked 这种蛇除非被激怒，否则不会伤人 
② (incite) «behaviour, remark» 挑衅 tiǎoxìn to provoke [somebody]/[something] to do [something] or into doing [something]; 刺激某人/某物做某事 
③ (cause, arouse) 引起 yǐnqǐ ‹laughter, criticism›; 激起 jīqǐ ‹indignation, riot›his new book has provoked a great deal of controversy 他的新书引起了很大的争议

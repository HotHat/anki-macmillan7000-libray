reservation 
BrE rɛzəˈveɪʃ(ə)n, AmE ˌrɛzərˈveɪʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Countable(booking) 预订 yùdìng I should like to make a reservation for a double room 我想预订一个双人间 to confirm/cancel a reservation 确认/取消预订 
② Uncountable(setting aside) 预留 yùliú the reservation of positions for non-Americans 为非美人士预留的职位 
③ Countable and uncountable(doubt, qualification) 保留意见 bǎoliú yìjiàn with reservations 有所保留 without reservation 毫无保留 I support the plan without reservation 我毫无保留地支持这个计划 legal reservations 法律上的质疑 
④ Countable(area of land) 居留地 jūliúdì a wildlife reservation 野生动物保护区

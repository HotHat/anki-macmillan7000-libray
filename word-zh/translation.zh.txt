translation 
BrE transˈleɪʃ(ə)n, trɑːns-, -nz-, AmE trænsˈleɪʃ(ə)n, trænzˈleɪʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Countable(of book, text, sentence, poem) 译文 yìwén a free/literal translation 意译/直译 to make a translation (of [something]) 翻译（某物） in translation 以译本形式 I never read a book in translation if I can read it in the original 如果我能读懂原著就绝不读译本 the play loses a lot in translation 这部剧的译本大为失色 
② Uncountable(activity) 翻译 fānyì simultaneous translation 同声传译 
③ Countable(as test) 翻译测试 fānyì cèshì I passed the comprehension but failed the translation 我的阅读理解及格了，但翻译没通过

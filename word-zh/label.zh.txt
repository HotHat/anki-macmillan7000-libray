label 
BrE ˈleɪb(ə)l, AmE ˈleɪbəl 

A.  noun 
① (on clothing, jar, luggage, diagram) 标签 biāoqiān a price/warning label 价格标签/警示标示 an address label 地址签条 to put a label on [something] 在某物上贴标签 
② figurative 称号 chēnghào I hated the label ‘housewife’ 我讨厌“家庭主妇”这个称谓 his critics hung or stuck the label (of) ‘superficial’on his work 评论家给他的作品扣上了“肤浅”的帽子 
③ Music 唱片公司 chàngpiàn gōngsī 
④ (in fashion) 时装品牌 shízhuāng pǐnpái 
⑤ Computing 标识符 biāozhìfú 
⑥ (in dictionary) 标注 biāozhù 
B.  transitive verb(present participle etc. -ll-) 
① (attach label to) 贴标签于 tiē biāoqiān yú a machine for labelling wine bottles 给酒瓶贴标签的机器 the air-freshener was labelled ‘ozone-friendly’ 这种空气清新剂标明“对臭氧层无害” 
② figurative 把…归类 bǎ… guīlèi her work is difficult to label accurately 她的作品很难准确归类 he is usually labelled as an Impressionist 人们通常称他为印象派艺术家 
③ (tag in dictionary) 给…加标注 gěi… jiā biāozhù

assign 
BrE əˈsʌɪn, AmE əˈsaɪn 
transitive verb 
① (allocate) 分配 fēnpèi ‹room, task›to assign [something] to [somebody], to assign [somebody] [something]; 把某事物分配给某人 I have assigned a difficult project to my students 我布置给学生们一个很难的课题 
② (delegate) 指派 zhǐpài to assign [somebody] to do [something] 派某人去做某事 the newspaper assigned
18 reporters to the case 报社派出了18位记者去报道这一事件 
③ (attribute) 把…归于 bǎ… guīyú ‹title, motive›to assign a motive to [somebody]/a crime 确定某人/罪案的动机 
④ (fix) 确定 quèdìng ‹date›the date assigned for the conference is
13 March 会议定于3月13日召开 would you care to assign a date to this piece of pottery? 请您确定这件陶器的年代好吗？

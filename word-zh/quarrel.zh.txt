quarrel 
BrE ˈkwɒr(ə)l, AmE ˈkwɔrəl, ˈkwɑrəl 

A.  noun 
① (argument) 争吵 zhēngchǎo a quarrel about or over [something] 为某事物的争吵 to have a quarrel (with [somebody]) （和某人）吵架 to pick a quarrel (with [somebody]) （向某人）寻衅 
② (difference of opinion) 分歧 fēnqí an intellectual quarrel 知识方面的分歧 
③ (feud) 长期不和 chángqī bùhé a long-standing quarrel 夙怨 to start a quarrel 产生不和 
④ (reason for disagreement) to have no quarrel (with [somebody]/[something]) （对某人/某事物）没有怨言 (duì mǒu rén/mǒu shìwù)méiyǒu yuànyán 
B.  intransitive verb(present participle etc. -ll-, AmE -l-) 
① (argue) 争吵 zhēngchǎo 
② (disagree) to quarrel with [something]; 不赞成 bù zànchéng ‹idea, approach›

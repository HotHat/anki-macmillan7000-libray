all right 
A.  adjective 
① (satisfactory) 合意的 héyì de is the coffee all right ? 这咖啡还行吗？ she's all right (agreeable) 她招人喜欢 (attractive) 她有魅力 you look all right 你气色不错 would you like some more wine? — I'm all right for the moment, thanks 再来点葡萄酒好吗？──我暂时不要了，谢谢 it's all right by me if it's all right by you 如果你不反对，我也没意见 it's an all right sort of place 那个地方还凑合 
② predicative (safe) 安好的 ānhǎo de how are you? — all right, thanks 你怎么样？──没事，谢谢 will the children be all right on their own? 孩子们独自待着会平安无事吗？ 
③ (well) 健康的 jiànkāng de she had flu, but she's all right now 她患了流感，但现在康复了 
④ (acceptable) 适宜的 shìyí de that's (quite) all right! (accepting thanks) 别客气！ (accepting apology) 不要紧！ is that all right with you? 那对你来说合适吗？ it's all right (for [somebody]) to do [something]; （某人）做某事没问题 it's all right for some, isn't it? 有些人就是运气好，是不是？ 
⑤ predicative (able to manage) 能对付的 néng duìfu de don't worry: we're all right 别担心，我们维持得下去 to be all right for; 有足够的 ‹money, time, work›if we won this competition, we'd be all right for life, wouldn't we? 如果我们赢了这场竞赛，一辈子的花销就有了，对不对？ 
B.  adverb 
① (satisfactorily) 合意地 héyì de to get along or on all right 进展顺利 their business is doing all right 他们的生意做得不错 
② (without a doubt) 确实 quèshí he's an idiot, all right! 他是个十足的白痴！ that's the man, all right 就是那个人，没错 
C.  exclamation 
① (giving agreement) [表示同意]would you like to help? — all right 你愿意帮忙吗？──可以 
② (conceding a point) [表示让步承认]all right, I know it wasn't the most diplomatic approach 当然了，我知道这不是最有策略的方法 all right, all right, I'm sorry I suggested it! 算啦，很抱歉我提出了这个建议！ 
③ (seeking consensus) [表示寻求共识]I want you to complete these two pages, all right? 请填写这两页，好吗？ 
④ (seeking information) [表示问讯，也稍有挑战或威胁意]all right, what's going on here? 喂，这里是怎么回事？ all right, what's the joke? 哎，乱开什么玩笑？ 
⑤ (introducing topic) [用于引出话题]all right, the next thing to do is to take a vote 就这样吧，下面该进行表决了 
D.  noun Uncountable BrE colloquial she's a bit of all right; 她蛮性感的 tā mán xìnggǎn de

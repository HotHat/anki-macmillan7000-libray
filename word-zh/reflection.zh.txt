reflection 
BrE rɪˈflɛkʃ(ə)n, AmE rəˈflɛkʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Uncountable(act of reflecting) 反射 fǎnshè the laws of the reflection of light 光的反射定律 
② Countable(image) 映像 yìngxiàng to see one's reflection in a window 在窗户上看到自己的影子 
③ Countable(representation) 反映 fǎnyìng the time taken is a reflection of the degree of difficulty involved 花多少时间要看有多大的难度 
④ Uncountable(thought) 深思 shēnsī on reflection 经深思熟虑 lost in reflection 陷入沉思 
⑤ Countable(idea) 想法 xiǎngfǎ (remark) 意见 yìjiàn reflection on [something]; 对某事物的看法 the reflection that …; …的想法 
⑥ Countable(criticism) 非议 fēiyì to be a reflection on [somebody]/[something]; 是对某人/某事物的指责 no reflection on you, but … 不是想批评你，但是…

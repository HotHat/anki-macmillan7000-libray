hour 
BrE ˈaʊə, AmE aʊ(ə)r 

A.  noun 
① (60 minutes) 小时 xiǎoshí a solid or full hour 整整1小时 within or inside the hour 在1小时内 a one-hour examination 1小时的考试 an hour (away) from London 距离伦敦有1小时的路程 to be paid by the hour 按小时付报酬 
② (top of the hour) 整点 zhěngdiǎn the clock struck the hour 钟整点敲响 the bus leaves on the hour 公共汽车整点发车 
③ (part of day) 时段 shíduàn at an early hour 清早时分 who can that be at this hour? 这时候会是谁呢？ 
④ (point in time) 时刻 shíkè in [somebody's] hour of need 在某人困难的时候 the country's finest hour 国家最繁盛的时期 his last hour was at hand 他大限将至 
B.  hours noun plural 
① (a long time) 很长时间 hěn cháng shíjiān to wait for hours 等很久 it took hours to get there 花了好长时间才到那里 
② (opening time) (for offices) 工作时间 gōngzuò shíjiān (for shops) 营业时间 yíngyè shíjiān business/office hours 营业/办公时间 after hours (of shop, pub) 打烊后 (of office) 下班后 visiting hours (in hospital) 探视时间 
③ (as schedule) to keep early/late hours 早睡早起/晚睡晚起 zǎo shuì zǎo qǐ/wǎn shuì wǎn qǐ to keep regular/strange hours 作息有规律/怪异 zuòxī yǒu guīlǜ/guàiyì 
④ (part of day) 时段 shíduàn the early or small or AmE colloquial wee hours (of the morning) 凌晨 at all hours 在任何时间 to do [something] at all hours of the day and night 不分昼夜随时做某事 till all hours 到很晚 
⑤ (on 24-hour clock) 点钟 diǎn zhōng the train leaves at 1400 hours 列车于14点发车 
⑥ Religion [天主教的] 祈祷礼拜 qídǎo lǐbài a book of hours 祈祷书

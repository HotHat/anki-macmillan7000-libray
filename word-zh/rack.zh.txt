rack 
BrE rak, AmE ræk 

A.  noun 
① (stand) 支架 zhījià a rack of clothes 一架子衣服 a letter rack 信插 
② (shelf) 行李架 xínglijià 
③ (torture) 拉肢刑具 lā zhī xíngjù to be on the rack figurative 备受煎熬 
④ to go to rack and ruin 变得一团糟 biàn de yītuán zāo they let the house go to rack and ruin 房子越来越破旧，他们也不管 
B.  transitive verb 
① figurative (torment) «fear, guilt, pain» 使…痛苦不堪 shǐ… tòngkǔ bùkān ‹person, body, mind›to be racked with; 深感 ‹guilt, shame, fear›an industry racked by crisis 受危机打击的产业 to rack one's brain(s) 绞尽脑汁 
② (draw off) 从沉渣中榨取 cóng chénzhā zhōng zhàqǔ to rack off 从酒糟中榨取 the wine is racked (off) into large oak casks 葡萄酒被榨出抽取到大橡木桶里

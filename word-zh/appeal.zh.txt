appeal 
BrE əˈpiːl, AmE əˈpil 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (call, request) 呼吁 hūyù to appeal for [something]; 呼吁某事 the manager appealed for calm 经理呼吁保持冷静 to appeal to [somebody] for/to do [something]; 恳请某人给予某物/做某事 he appealed to the court for mercy 他恳求法庭宽恕 
② Law 上诉 shàngsù to appeal to a higher court 向上级法院申诉 to appeal against [something]; 不服某事提出上诉 
③ Sport 诉诸裁判 sù zhū cáipàn 
④ (attract, interest) 有吸引力 yǒu xīyǐnlì does this colour appeal to you? 这种颜色你喜欢吗？ 
B.  noun 
① (call, request) 呼吁 hūyù an appeal for [something]; 对某事的呼吁 an appeal to [somebody]; 向某人发出的呼吁 he made an appeal to the citizens for tolerance 他恳请公民要宽容 
② Law 上诉 shàngsù to make or lodge an appeal against [something] 不服某事提出上诉 
③ Sport 诉诸裁判 sù zhū cáipàn

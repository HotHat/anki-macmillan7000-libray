cane 
BrE keɪn, AmE keɪn 

A.  noun 
① Countable(stem of sugar, bamboo) 茎 jīng 
② Uncountable(rattan) 藤条 téngtiáo (bamboo) 竹竿 zhúgān (sugar cane) 甘蔗 gānzhe 
③ Countable(of raspberry, blackberry) 主茎 zhǔ jīng 
④ Countable(for walking) 手杖 shǒuzhàng (for riding) 马鞭 mǎbiān 
⑤ Uncountable and countable BrE (for punishment) 笞条 chītiáo to get the cane; 遭受鞭笞 to give [somebody] the cane; 鞭笞某人 
B.  transitive verb 
① BrE (punish) 用笞条打 yòng chītiáo dǎ 
② colloquial (defeat) 打败 dǎbài

source 
BrE sɔːs, AmE sɔrs 

A.  noun 
① (of river) 源头 yuántóu the source of the Nile 尼罗河发源地 
② (origin) 来源 láiyuán at source 在源头 our wages are taxed at source 我们的工资下发前已扣税 a source of income 收入来源 
③ (of information) (person) 提供消息者 tígōng xiāoxi zhě (place) 消息来源 xiāoxi láiyuán to hear [something] from a reliable source 从可靠人士那里听说某事 an original source for … …的原始出处 to cite one's sources 引用原始资料 sources close to [somebody]/[something] 接近某人/某物的消息人士 
④ (of trouble, problem) 根源 gēnyuán a or the source of … …的缘由 you're a constant source of disappointment to your parents 你总是让父母失望 
B.  transitive verb 获得 huòdé the coffee is sourced from overseas 这咖啡是进口的 to source a supply of [something] 得到某物的供给

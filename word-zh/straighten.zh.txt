BrE ˈstreɪt(ə)n, AmE ˈstreɪtn

② (make upright or level) 使端正 shǐ duānzhèng I straightened my tie and walked in 我把领带正了正，走了进去 
③ (tidy) 使整洁 shǐ zhěngjié to straighten the room 整理房间

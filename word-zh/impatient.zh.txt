impatient 
BrE ɪmˈpeɪʃ(ə)nt, AmE ɪmˈpeɪʃənt 
adjective 
① (quick-tempered) 急躁的 jízào de ‹person›; 不耐烦的 bù nàifán de ‹glance›to become or get impatient with [somebody]; 对某人不耐烦 
② (intolerant) 不宽容的 bù kuānróng de ‹person, reply, gesture›to be impatient at [something]/with [somebody]; 对某事/某人无法忍受 to be impatient of [something]/[somebody]; 不能容忍某人/某事 
③ (eager) 急切的 jíqiè de to be impatient to do [something]/for [something]; 迫不及待地要做某事/要得到某物 to be impatient for [something] to happen; 热切期待某事发生

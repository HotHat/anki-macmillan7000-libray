deadly 
BrE ˈdɛdli, AmE ˈdɛdli 

A.  adjective 
① figurative (lethal) 致命的 zhìmìng de ‹disease, weapon, blow›the cold was deadly 冷得要死 
② (intense) 极度的 jídù de ‹fear, hatred›in deadly earnest 非常认真地 with deadly accuracy 极其精确地 
③ (effective) 有效的 yǒuxiào de his aim is deadly 他弹无虚发 she uses wit with deadly effect 她把自己的机智诙谐发挥得淋漓尽致 
④ colloquial (dull, boring) 乏味的 fáwèi de ‹concert, lecture, party›
B.  adverb 
① (extremely) 极其 jíqí ‹serious, earnest, boring›
② (suggestive of death) 死一般地 sǐ yībān de to be deadly quiet/pale 死一般地寂静/苍白

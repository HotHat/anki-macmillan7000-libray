negative 
BrE ˈnɛɡətɪv, AmE ˈnɛɡədɪv 

A.  adjective 
① (saying no) 否定的 fǒudìng de ‹statement, reply›to give a negative answer 给予否定的答复 
② (pessimistic, unhelpful, unfavourable) 消极的 xiāojí de ‹response, attitude, criticism›to be negative about [something]; 对某事持消极态度 don't be so negative! 不要太悲观！ 
③ (harmful) 负面的 fùmiàn de ‹feelings, experience, influence›
④ Physics, Electronics 负的 fù de ‹charge, particle, ion›negative terminal/wire 负极端子/负极引线 
⑤ Medicine 阴性的 yīnxìng de ‹result, test›
⑥ Mathematics 负的 fù de ‹number, quantity›
⑦ Photography 负的 fù de ‹photograph, image›
B.  noun 
① (refusal) 否定 fǒudìng to answer or reply in the negative 作出否定回答 
② Linguistics (word) 否定词 fǒudìngcí (grammatical construction) 否定式 fǒudìngshì in the negative 否定的 fǒudìng de 
③ Photography 底片 dǐpiàn 
④ Physics, Electronics 负电 fùdiàn 
⑤ (disadvantage, down side) 负面因素 fùmiàn yīnsù the negatives outweigh the positives 负面因素超过了正面因素

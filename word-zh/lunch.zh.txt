lunch 
BrE lʌn(t)ʃ, AmE lən(t)ʃ

B.  intransitive verb 吃午餐 chī wǔcān to lunch with [somebody]; 同某人一起吃午饭 to lunch at home/the pub/a restaurant; 在家/酒吧/餐馆吃午饭 to lunch on or off [something]; 午饭吃某物

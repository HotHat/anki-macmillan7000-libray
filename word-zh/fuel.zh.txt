fuel 
BrE fjuː(ə)l, AmE ˈfju(ə)l 

A.  noun 
① (for heat, power) 燃料 ránliào to run/be out of fuel; 用尽/没有燃料 to take on fuel; 加燃料 jiā ránliào fuel prices/crisis 燃料价格/危机 
② (food, drink) 营养物 yíngyǎngwù 
③ (for anger, hatred) 刺激因素 cìjī yīnsù to add fuel to one's anger 使某人更是怒火中烧 to add fuel to the flames or fire 火上加油 
B.  transitive verb(present participle etc. -ll-, AmE -l-) 
① (power) «oil, gas, coal» 用作…的燃料 yòngzuò… de ránliào ‹furnace, vehicle›to be fuelled by oil/gas 以石油/天然气为燃料 
② (put fuel into) 给…加燃料 gěi… jiā ránliào ‹plane, ship, rocket›
③ figurative (intensify) 使…加剧 shǐ… jiājù ‹inflation, crisis, unrest›
C.  intransitive verb(present participle etc. -ll-, AmE -l-) «driver, pilot, plane» 加燃料 jiā ránliào a fuelling stop 加油站

alone 
BrE əˈləʊn, AmE əˈloʊn 

A.  adjectiveattributive 
① (on one's own) 独自的 dúzì de all alone 独自一人 to leave [somebody] alone literal 让某人独自待着 (in peace) 不打扰某人 leave that bike alone! 别动那辆自行车！ 
② (isolated) 单独的 dāndú de she alone believed him 只有她一个人相信他 not to be alone in [something]/in doing [something] 在某事上不单一个人/不单一个人做某事 to stand alone figurative 独一无二 
B.  adverb 
① (on one's own) 单独地 dāndú de to live alone 独居 
② (on one's own) 独自地 dúzì de to go it alone 独自干 I can manage alone 我自己一个人就能应付 
③ (exclusively) 仅仅 jǐnjǐn for this reason alone 仅仅因为这个原因 man does not live by bread alone proverb 人不能只靠面包活着

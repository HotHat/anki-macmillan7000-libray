govern 
BrE ˈɡʌv(ə)n, AmE ˈɡəvərn 

A.  transitive verb 
① Politics (administer) 治理 zhìlǐ ‹nation›(rule) 统治 tǒngzhì ‹kingdom›to be governed by [somebody]/[something]; 被某人/某组织统治 
② (control) «regulation, principle» 支配 zhīpèi ‹action, use›laws that govern the manufacture of foodstuffs 指导食品生产的法律 
③ (determine) «factor, values» 决定 juédìng ‹behaviour, relationship›self-interest governs all his actions 他的所作所为皆取决于一己私利 
④ figurative (restrain) 抑制 yìzhì to govern one's feelings 克制情绪 
⑤ Linguistics «verbs» 支配 zhīpèi ‹case›
⑥ Electricity, Technology «valve» 调节 tiáojié ‹flow, speed›

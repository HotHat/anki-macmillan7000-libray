fond 
BrE fɒnd, AmE fɑnd 
adjective 
① attributive (affectionate) 深情的 shēnqíng de ‹gesture, embrace›a fond wife 深情的妻子 fond memories 充满柔情的记忆 absence makes the heart grow fonder proverb 离别情更浓 
② attributive (indulgent) 溺爱的 nì'ài de ‹parents›
③ attributive (deeply felt) 热切的 rèqiè de ‹hope, ambition›
④ attributive (foolishly optimistic) 盲目的 mángmù de ‹ambition, belief›in the fond hope that … 痴痴地希望… 
⑤ predicative (having a soft spot for) 喜欢的 xǐhuan de to be fond of [somebody]; 喜欢某人 
⑥ predicative (keen on) 爱好的 àihào de to be fond of [something]/doing [something]; 爱好某事/做某事 
⑦ predicative (irritatingly prone) 好…的 hǎo… de to be fond of doing [something]; 好做某事

decline 
BrE dɪˈklʌɪn, AmE dəˈklaɪn 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (become smaller) «rate, sales» 下降 xiàjiàng ; «quantity, demand, number» 减少 jiǎnshǎo 
② (wane) «health» 恶化 èhuà ; «business» 衰退 shuāituì ; «power, strength» 衰弱 shuāiruò ; «empire» 衰落 shuāiluò ; «influence, support, confidence» 减退 jiǎntuì ; «status» 下降 xiàjiàng 
③ (refuse) 婉拒 wǎnjù 
④ Linguistics «word» 变格 biàngé 
B.  transitive verb 
① 婉拒 wǎnjù ‹offer, honour, promotion›to decline to do [something]; 婉拒做某事 
② Linguistics 使…变格 shǐ… biàngé ‹word›
C.  noun 
① Uncountable(decrease) 下降 xiàjiàng a (small/steep) decline in [something]; 某物的（小幅/急剧）下降 to be on the or in decline; 在下降 a 5% decline to 175 下降5%至175 
② Uncountable and countable(waning) 衰退 shuāituì to be on the or in decline; 在衰退 a decline in [something]; 某事的衰退 to go or fall into (a) decline; 陷入衰退 his decline into madness 他发疯的过程

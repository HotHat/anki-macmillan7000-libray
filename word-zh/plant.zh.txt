plant

② Countable(factory) 工厂 gōngchǎng (power station) 发电站 fādiànzhàn 
③ Uncountable(machinery) 大型机器 dàxíng jīqì plant maintenance/manufacturing/costs 设备保养/生产/成本 
④ Countable(spy) 卧底 wòdǐ 
⑤ Countablecolloquial (piece of evidence) 栽赃物品 zāizāng wùpǐn 
B.  transitive verb 
① (put in ground) 播种 bōzhǒng ‹seed›; 栽种 zāizhòng ‹bulb, flower, bush, tree›; 种植 zhòngzhí ‹crop›
② (put plants in) 在…栽种 zài… zāizhòng ‹orchard, garden, field›fields planted with maize 种植了玉米的田地 
③ (put down) 安放 ānfàng ‹large object›; 竖立 shùlì ‹flagpole›to plant one's feet firmly 两脚稳稳地站立 to plant a flag on the summit 在山顶插上旗帜 to plant a spade in the soil 把铲子插在泥土里 
④ (place on body) 给予 jǐyǔ ‹punch, kick›to plant a kiss on [somebody's] cheek 在某人的脸颊上吻一下 to plant a blow on [somebody's] head 对准某人的头打一拳 
⑤ (hide for use) 秘密放置 mìmì fàngzhì ‹explosive, listening device›
⑥ (place to produce result) 将…栽赃 jiāng… zāizāng ‹stolen goods, evidence, drugs›to plant [something] on [somebody]; 把某物栽赃于某人 to plant a question 事先策划好问题 
⑦ (place as helper) 安插 ānchā ‹spy, informer›
⑧ (embed) 灌输 guànshū ‹idea, rumour›to plant [something] in [somebody's] mind or head 把某事物灌输进某人的头脑 to plant the seeds of doubt in [somebody's] mind 在某人心中播下疑虑的种子 
⑨ (found, establish) 建立 jiànlì ‹colony, settlement›

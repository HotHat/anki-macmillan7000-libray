picnic 
BrE ˈpɪknɪk, AmE ˈpɪkˌnɪk 

A.  noun 
① (outing, occasion) 野餐 yěcān to go for or on a picnic; 去野餐 qù yěcān to have a picnic; 野餐 yěcān 
② (meal) 野餐食物 yěcān shíwù to make or pack a picnic; 准备野餐食物 it's no picnic! colloquial 这不是件轻松愉快的事！ 
B.  intransitive verb (present participle etc. -ck-) 去野餐 qù yěcān to picnic off [something]; 野餐吃某物

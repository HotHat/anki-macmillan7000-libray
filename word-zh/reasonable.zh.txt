reasonable 
BrE ˈriːz(ə)nəb(ə)l, AmE ˈriz(ə)nəb(ə)l 
adjective 
① (sensible) 明智的 míngzhì de ‹person›be reasonable! 讲点道理！ to be reasonable about [something]; 对某事很理智 to be reasonable in [something]; 在某方面通情达理 
② (justified) 合乎情理的 héhū qínglǐ de ‹interpretation, conclusion, suspicion, attitude›it is reasonable for [somebody] to do [something] 某人做某事是合理的 beyond reasonable doubt 确切无疑 
③ (not too expensive) 合理的 hélǐ de ‹cost, price›; (fair) 公道的 gōngdao de ‹fee, offer›all reasonable claims will be considered 所有公平合理的要求都会考虑 
④ colloquial (moderately good) 尚好的 shàng hǎo de ‹weather, health, access›the food is reasonable 食物还算不错

recruit 
BrE rɪˈkruːt, AmE rəˈkrut 

A.  noun 
① (in armed forces) 新兵 xīn bīng ; (in police force) 新警员 xīn jǐngyuán 
② (in company, department) 新成员 xīn chéngyuán a recruit to [something]; 某处的新成员 a recruit from [something]; 从某处招来的新成员 
B.  transitive verb 
① (in armed forces, police) 征募 zhēngmù ‹soldier, mercenaries›
② (enrol) «club, party» 招收 zhāoshōu ‹member›; «society, movement, sect» 吸纳 xīnà ‹person, member›
③ (hire) «company, organization» 招聘 zhāopìn ‹staff, employee›to be recruited to do … 被招收来做… to recruit [somebody] from [something]; 从某处招来某人 
④ (muster) 召集 zhàojí ‹force, team, squad, corps›
C.  intransitive verb «army» 征兵 zhēngbīng ; «police» 招募新警员 zhāomù xīn jǐngyuán ; «party, movement» 招收新成员 zhāoshōu xīn chéngyuán

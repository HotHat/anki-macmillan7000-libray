correct 
BrE kəˈrɛkt, AmE kəˈrɛkt 

A.  adjective 
① (right) 正确的 zhèngquè de to be correct in every detail 每个细节都正确 would I be correct in thinking that you are going to help us? 您会帮我们吗？ 
② (appropriate) 合乎规范的 héhū guīfàn de ‹dress, language, procedure›; (proper) 得体的 détǐ de ‹manners, behaviour›
B.  transitive verb 
① (put right) 纠正 jiūzhèng ‹spelling, mistake, pronunciation›
② (mark errors in) 批改 pīgǎi ‹essay, papers›; 审订 shěndìng ‹proof, text›
③ (point out mistakes of) 指出…的错误 zhǐchū… de cuòwù ‹person›I was wrong: I stand corrected 我错了，承蒙指正 
④ (rectify) 矫正 jiǎozhèng ‹eyesight›; 调整 tiáozhěng ‹imbalance›
⑤ (adjust) 调节 tiáojié ‹headlights›to correct course Nautical, Aviation 校正航向

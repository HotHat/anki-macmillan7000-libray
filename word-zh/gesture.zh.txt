gesture 
BrE ˈdʒɛstʃə, AmE ˈdʒɛstʃər 

A.  nounUncountable and countable
① (movement, sign) 示意动作 shìyì dòngzuò ; (hand movement) 手势 shǒushì 
② figurative (act) 表示 biǎoshì a gesture of goodwill/solidarity 善意的/团结的表示 that was a nice gesture 那是个很好的姿态 to be (just) an empty gesture （只是）装装样子 
B.  intransitive verb 用动作示意 yòng dòngzuò shìyì to gesture at or towards [something]; 对某物作示意动作 to gesture to [somebody] (to do …); 向某人打手势示意（做…） 
C.  transitive verb 用手势表示 yòng shǒushì biǎoshì to gesture one's assent 用手势表示赞同 to gesture [somebody] towards a chair 示意某人在椅子上坐下

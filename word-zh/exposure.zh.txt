exposure 
BrE ɪkˈspəʊʒə, ɛkˈspəʊʒə, AmE ɪkˈspoʊʒər 
noun 
① Countable and uncountable(disclosure of crime, dishonesty etc.) 揭发 jiēfā to threaten with exposure 威胁要揭发 
② Uncountable(subjection) 暴露 bàolù exposure to the wind and sun 经受风吹日晒 exposure of children to radiation 孩童暴露于辐射中 
③ Uncountable(physical condition) 受寒 shòuhán to suffer from exposure; 受冻 to die of exposure; 冻死 
④ Uncountable(publicity) 亮相 liàngxiàng (coverage) 报道 bàodào to receive or get exposure in the media; 被媒体报道 
⑤ Uncountable(to financial losses) 承担金融风险 chéngdān jīnróng fēngxiǎn 
⑥ Countable and uncountablePhotography (exposing a film to light) 曝光 bàoguāng (length of exposing time) 曝光时间 bàoguāng shíjiān (picture) 底片 dǐpiàn to take an exposure; 照一张相片 a double exposure; 二次曝光

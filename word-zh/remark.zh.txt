remark 
BrE rɪˈmɑːk, AmE rəˈmɑrk 

A.  noun 
① Countable(comment) 言论 yánlùn to make some remarks 说几句话 opening/closing remarks 开场白/结束语 concluding remarks 结语 a personal remark 人身攻击 his throwaway remark caused a political furore 他随口说的一句话却引起了一场政治骚乱 
② Uncountable(notice) 注意 zhùyì worthy of remark 值得关注的 
B.  transitive verb 
① (comment) 评论道 pínglùn dào ; (mention) 说道 shuōdào 
② (notice) 注意到 zhùyì dào 
C.  intransitive verb 议论 yìlùn to remark on or upon [something]; 议论某事物 everyone remarked on her beauty and charm 人人都对她的美艳品头论足

freely 
BrE ˈfriːli, AmE ˈfrili 
adverb 
① (not being confined or obstructed) 自由地 zìyóu de I can turn my head more freely now 现在我可以更自如地转头 the traffic is flowing freely 交通很通畅 to breathe freely 顺畅地呼吸 thank heaven the inspector has gone away, I can breathe freely again figurative 谢天谢地，检查员走了，我现在可以松口气了 to talk or speak freely 畅谈 to be freely available «goods, information» 可随意获得 
② (willingly) 自愿地 zìyuàn de ‹agree, sacrifice›to admit [something] freely 爽快承认某事 to give one's consent freely 自愿同意 
③ (abundantly) 大量地 dàliàng de ‹give, use›to spend money freely 花钱大手大脚 the plant grows freely in the hedgerows 植物在灌木树篱中生长茂盛 
④ (not strictly) 不紧扣原文地 bù jǐn kòu yuánwén de freely translated 意译的

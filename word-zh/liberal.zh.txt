liberal 
BrE ˈlɪb(ə)r(ə)l, AmE ˈlɪb(ə)rəl 

A.  adjective 
① (open-minded, tolerant) 宽容的 kuānróng de ‹person›; 开明的 kāimíng de ‹intellectual, attitude, school, laws›
② Politics (favouring liberty and moderate reform) 自由的 zìyóu de ‹views, regime›a liberal democratic party 自由民主党 
③ Liberal Politics (relating to party) 自由党的 zìyóudǎng de ‹politician, supporter, policy›
④ (generous) 大量的 dàliàng de ‹supply, helping, quantity›; 慷慨的 kāngkǎi de ‹person, offer, donation›to be liberal with [something]; 慷慨给予某物 he's very liberal with his money 他花钱很大方 to make liberal use of [something] 大量使用某物 some examiners were too liberal 有些考官给分太松 liberal coating 厚厚的涂层 
⑤ (not literal) 不拘泥字面的 bù jūní zìmiàn de ‹account›a liberal translation 灵活的翻译 
B.  noun 
① (tolerant person) 宽容大度的人 kuānróng dàdù de rén I'm a liberal as far as marriage is concerned 我在婚姻问题上很开明 
② Politics (sb. favouring liberty and moderate reform) 自由主义者 zìyóu zhǔyìzhě 
③ Liberal Politics (member) 自由党党员 zìyóudǎng dǎngyuán (supporter) 自由党支持者 zìyóudǎng zhīchízhě

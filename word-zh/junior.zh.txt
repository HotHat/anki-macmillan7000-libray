junior 
BrE ˈdʒuːnɪə, AmE ˈdʒunjər 

A.  adjective 
① (younger) 较年幼的 jiào niányòu de to be junior to [somebody] by two years 比某人小两岁 
② (low-ranking) 级别较低的 jíbié jiào dī de ‹official, partner›to be junior to [somebody] (in [something]); （在某方面）地位比某人低 
③ attributive (for youth) 适合青少年的 shìhé qīngshàonián de ‹division, fashion›; 少年组的 shàoniánzǔ de ‹league, player›
④ ( also Junior); especially AmE (in name) 小 xiǎo Bob Mortimer junior 小鲍勃莫蒂默 
B.  noun 
① (younger person) 年少者 niánshàozhě to be x years [somebody's] junior, to be [somebody's] junior by x years; 小某人X岁 
② (low-ranking worker) 职位较低者 zhíwèi jiào dī zhě the office junior 办公室初级文员 
③ AmE colloquial (as form of address) 大儿子 dà érzi 
④ BrE School (junior school student) 小学生 xiǎoxuéshēng 
⑤ AmE School, University (third-year student) [四年制中学或大学的] 三年级学生 sān niánjí xuésheng

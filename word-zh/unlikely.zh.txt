unlikely 
BrE ʌnˈlʌɪkli, AmE ˌənˈlaɪkli 
adjective 
① (unexpected) 不大可能的 bù dà kěnéng de highly or most unlikely 极不可能的 it is/is not unlikely that … …是不大可能的/…并非不可能 to be unlikely to do [something]; 不大可能做某事 in the unlikely event of his refusing … 万一他拒绝… 
② (strange) 想不到的 xiǎngbudào de a very unlikely couple 很不般配的一对 
③ (probably untrue) 不大可信的 bù dà kěxìn de an unlikely story 不太真实的报道 
④ (not likely to succeed) 不大可能会成功的 bù dà kěnéng huì chénggōng de the most unlikely men have been successful here 最不可能成功的人在这里都获得了成功

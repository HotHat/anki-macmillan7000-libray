introduction 
BrE ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃ(ə)n, AmE ˌɪntrəˈdəkʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Countable(presentation) 介绍 jièshào to do/make/perform the introductions 作介绍 she was hoping for an introduction to the president 她希望被引见给总统 our next guest needs no introduction 我们的下一位客人就无需介绍了 a letter of introduction 介绍信 this document was my introduction to high society 这份文件是我进入上流社会的敲门砖 
② Countable(background information) 开场白 kāichǎngbái by way of introduction 作为开场白 
③ Countable(to book, article, speech) 序言 xùyán 
④ CountableMusic (preceding main section) 前奏 qiánzòu (opening passage) 序曲 xùqǔ 
⑤ Countable(first experience of sth.) 初次经历 chūcì jīnglì this concert was my introduction to classical music 这场音乐会使我第一次接触到古典音乐 an introduction to Paris nightlife 对巴黎夜生活的初次体验 
⑥ Countable(beginner's guide) 入门书 rùménshū An Introduction to Latin 《拉丁语入门》 
⑦ Uncountable(mentioning for first time) 初次提及 chūcì tíjí the introduction of these new characters complicates the plot 这些新人物的出现令情节变得复杂了 
⑧ Uncountable(insertion of needle, tube) 插入 chārù (addition of substance) 添入 tiānrù 
⑨ Uncountable and countable(bringing in) (of species, plant) 引进 yǐnjìn (of disease) 传入 chuánrù 
⑩ Uncountable(bringing into use or operation) 推行 tuīxíng the introduction of the new legislation/product 新法律的实施/新产品的推出 issues arising from the introduction of new technology 采用新技术引发的问题 
⑪ UncountablePolitics 提交讨论 tíjiāo tǎolùn the introduction of the bill 议案的提交 
⑫ Countable(thing newly brought in) 新推行的事物 xīn tuīxíng de shìwù the procedure is a new introduction 该程序是新推出的 these exotic vegetables are recent introductions to our supermarkets 这些进口蔬菜是我们超市新进的

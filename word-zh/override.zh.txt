override 
A.  /BrE əʊvəˈrʌɪd, AmE ˌoʊvərˈraɪd
transitive verb(past tense overrode /ˌəʊvəˈrəʊd/; past participle overridden /ˌəʊvəˈrɪdn/) 
① (overrule) 否决 fǒujué ‹opinion, decision›; 使…无效 shǐ… wúxiào ‹order, veto›
② (take precedence) 优先于 yōuxiān yú ‹consideration, rule›this order overrides all previous instructions 这个命令高于所有先前的指示 
③ (control) 手动控制 shǒudòng kòngzhì ‹machine›; 手控消除 shǒu kòng xiāochú ‹automatic system, settings›

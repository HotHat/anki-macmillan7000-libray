benefit 
BrE ˈbɛnɪfɪt, AmE ˈbɛnəfɪt 

A.  noun 
① Uncountable and countable(advantage) 益处 yìchu (good) 好处 hǎochu social benefits 社会效益 to be of benefit to [somebody]/[something]; 有益于某人/某事物 to be to [somebody's] benefit to do [something]; 做某事有利于某人 for the benefit of humankind 为了造福人类 I'm doing this for your benefit 我这样做是为你好 he's crying for your benefit ironic 他只是在哭给你看 with the benefit of hindsight/experience 借助后见之明/经验 to give [somebody] the benefit of the doubt 把某人往好处想 the benefits outweigh the disadvantages 利大于弊 
② Countable(fundraiser) 募捐活动 mùjuān huódòng a benefit match/performance 一场义赛/义演 
③ Uncountable and countable(allowance) 救济金 jiùjìjīn housing/maternity benefits 住房/生育补助金 to be on benefit(s) 靠领取救济金生活 
④ Countable(perk) 额外待遇 éwài dàiyù tax-free benefits 免税优惠 benefits in kind 实物津贴 
B.  transitive verb (present participle etc. -t- or -tt-) 有益于 yǒuyì yú to benefit [somebody]/[something] 对某人/某事物有益 
C.  intransitive verb (present participle etc. -t- or -tt-) 获益 huòyì to stand to benefit (from [something]); 可能会（从某事物中）获益

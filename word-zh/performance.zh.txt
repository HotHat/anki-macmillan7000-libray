performance 
BrE pəˈfɔːm(ə)ns, AmE pərˈfɔrməns 
noun 
① Countable(for entertainment) 表演 biǎoyǎn to give or put on a performance; 进行演出 a live performance 现场演出 a benefit/gala performance 慈善/庆典演出 
② Countable(manner or performing) 表现 biǎoxiàn he won a medal for his fine performance in the Olympics 他在奥运会上表现良好获得了一枚奖牌 his performance of King Lear left me cold 他演的李尔王没有打动我 the violinist gave a superb performance 小提琴家进行了精彩的演奏 his performance in the test wasn't up to scratch 他考得不好 
③ Uncountable(of duties, services) 履行 lǚxíng he is meticulous in the performance of his duties 他履行职责一丝不苟 
④ Uncountable(of vehicle, engine) 性能 xìngnéng 
⑤ UncountableBusn, Finance 业绩 yèjì the performance of the economy 经济状况 
⑥ Uncountablecolloquial (exaggerated behaviour) 夸张的行为 kuāzhāng de xíngwéi (disgraceful behaviour) 丢脸的行为 diūliǎn de xíngwéi what on earth did she mean by that performance in the cafe? 她在咖啡馆里那么丢人现眼到底是什么意思？ 
⑦ Uncountablecolloquial (fuss) 忙乱 mángluàn what a performance! 真够忙乱的！ starting up this old car is a bit of a performance 发动这辆旧车真是有些麻烦

search 
BrE səːtʃ, AmE sərtʃ 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (look, seek) 搜寻 sōuxún to search for [something]/[somebody]; 搜寻某物/某人 to search through [something]; 找遍某处 she spends hours searching through her wardrobe for or to find something to wear 她用了好几个小时在衣橱里翻找可穿的衣服 I don't like the idea of somebody searching through my belongings 我不喜欢别人翻我的东西 
② Computing 搜索 sōusuǒ to search for data/a file 搜索数据/文件 
B.  transitive verb 
① (examine) 搜查 sōuchá he was stopped and searched by an army patrol 他被巡逻兵拦住搜身 to search a catalogue/list 查目录/清单 to search one's memory 努力回忆 search me! colloquial 我怎么知道！ 
② (scan) 仔细察看 zǐxì chákàn ‹map›
③ Computing 搜索 sōusuǒ ‹file, document›
C.  noun 
① (hunt) 寻找 xúnzhǎo in search of … 寻找… he went into the kitchen in search of [something] to drink 他到厨房去找一点儿喝的 
② (of area, house, person) 搜查 sōuchá (into drawer, cupboard, handbag) 翻找 fān zhǎo a body search 搜身 a customs search 海关检查 right of search Law, Nautical 搜查权 
③ Computing 搜索 sōusuǒ to do a search 进行搜索 PHRASAL VERBS search about, search around: intransitive verb to search about for [something]/[somebody]; 四处寻找某物/某人 sìchù xúnzhǎo mǒu wù/mǒu rén search out transitive verb[search somebody/something out, search out somebody/something] 找出 zhǎochū she searched out her old school photographs to show me 她找出上学时的老照片给我看

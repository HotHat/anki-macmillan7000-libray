② figurative (of person, group) 势力范围 shìlì fànwéi within/outside [somebody's] orbit 在/不在某人的势力范围内 
B.  transitive verb 绕…运行 rào… yùnxíng 
C.  intransitive verb 绕轨道运行 rào guǐdào yùnxíng to orbit around [something]; 绕某物运行

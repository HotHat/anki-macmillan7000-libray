stab 
BrE stab, AmE stæb 

A.  transitive verb(present participle etc. -bb-) 
① (pierce) 刺 cì ‹person, animal›; 扎 zhā ‹food›to stab [somebody] to death 把某人刺死 to stab [somebody] in the heart 刺某人的心脏 to stab [somebody] in the back literal, figurative 在某人背后捅刀子 
② (poke hard) 戳 chuō to stab the air 强调自己的观点 
B.  intransitive verb(present participle etc. -bb-) 
① (aim blow) 刺 cì to stab at [somebody]/[something] with [something]; 用某物刺某人/某物 
② (tap, poke) 戳 chuō to stab at [somebody]/[something]; 捅某人/某物 to stab at [something] with one's finger 用手指捅某人 
C.  noun 
① (act) 刺 cì a stab wound in the chest 胸口上被刺的伤口 a stab in the back literal, figurative 背后捅的一刀 
② (wound) 刺破的伤口 cìpò de shāngkǒu 
③ (feeling, pain) 突发的一阵 tūfā de yīzhèn a stab of pain/jealousy/fear 一阵剧痛/嫉妒/害怕 
④ colloquial (attempt) 尝试 chángshì to make or have a stab at [something]/at doing [something] 尝试某事物/做某事

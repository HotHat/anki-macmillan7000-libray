index 
BrE ˈɪndɛks, AmE ˈɪnˌdɛks 

A.  noun(plural indexes or indices) 
① (in book) 索引 suǒyǐn 
② (card catalogue) 卡片索引 kǎpiàn suǒyǐn 
③ (indication) 标志 biāozhì to be an index of [something]; 是某事的指标 
④ Economics, Finance 指数 zhǐshù cost-of-living index 生活成本指数 
B.  transitive verb 
① (provide an index to) 为…编索引 wèi… biān suǒyǐn this book is badly indexed 这本书的索引很乱 
② (record in an index) 将…编入索引 jiāng… biānrù suǒyǐn ‹terms, data›to be indexed under [something]; 在索引中被编入某条目下 
③ Economics «government» 使…与指数挂钩 shǐ… yǔ zhǐshù guàgōu ‹salaries, interest rates›to index [something] to [something]; 使某物与某物挂钩

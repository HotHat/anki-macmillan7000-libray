punishment 
BrE ˈpʌnɪʃm(ə)nt, AmE ˈpənɪʃmənt 
noun 
① Uncountable(action) 惩罚 chéngfá corporal or physical punishment 体罚 crime and punishment 罪与罚 
② Uncountable and countable(penalty) 处罚 chǔfá to inflict a punishment on [somebody] 对某人施以处罚 let the punishment fit the crime 按罪量刑 to take one's punishment bravely or like a man 勇敢地接受处罚 
③ Uncountablecolloquial (rough treatment) 粗暴对待 cūbào duìdài to withstand or stand up to punishment 经受折磨

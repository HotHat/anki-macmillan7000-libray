succeed 
BrE səkˈsiːd, AmE səkˈsid 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (achieve success) 成功 chénggōng to succeed in business 做生意获成功 to succeed in one's exams 通过考试 I only succeeded in making things worse 我结果却把事情弄得更糟 nothing succeeds like success proverb 一事成，百事顺 if at first you don't succeed, try, try again proverb 如果一开始不成功，那就努力努力再努力 
② (accede) to succeed to 继承 jìchéng ‹throne, title, property›
B.  transitive verb 
① (take place of) 接替 jiētì ‹person›she succeeded her father as chairman 她接替父亲任董事长 
② (follow) 接续 jiēxù her embarrassment was succeeded by fear 她尴尬之后感到害怕

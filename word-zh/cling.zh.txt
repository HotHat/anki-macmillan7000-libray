cling 
BrE klɪŋ, AmE klɪŋ 
intransitive verb(past tense, past participle clung)

② to cling to [something] figurative (refuse to give up) 不愿放弃某事物 bù yuàn fàngqì mǒu shìwù to cling to the hope that … 抱着…的希望 bàozhe… de xīwàng

② predicative (available to use) 预备好的 yùbèi hǎo de dinner's ready 饭好啦 have your excuses ready! 找好借口吧！ ready for [something]; 为某事预备好的 your suit is ready for collection 您的西装可以取了 ready for use or to use 随时可用的 ready to do [something]; 预备好做某事的 the contract will be ready to sign in two weeks 合同两周之后可以签署 the fruit is ready to be picked 果实可以摘了 to get [something] ready 把某物预备好

④ predicative (eager) 急切的 jíqiè de I was very angry and ready for a fight 我很气愤，想打一架 I'm ready for what promises to be an interesting contest 我乐意看看想必会很有趣的比赛 ready to do [something]; 急切想做某事的 don't be so ready to find fault! 别这么爱挑刺！ to be more than ready to do [something] 非常想做某事 
⑤ predicative (quick to give) ready with [something] 迅速提供某物的 xùnsù tígōng mǒu wù de he's a bit too ready with his criticism 他有点太急于批评了 she was very ready with advice/her excuses 她很会提建议/她总是有借口 tā hěn huì tí jiànyì/tā zǒng shì yǒu jièkǒu 
⑥ predicative (on the point of) ready to do [something]; 马上要做某事的 mǎshàng yào zuò mǒu shì de I was just about ready to burst into tears 我都快要哭了 wǒ dōu kuàiyào kū le the house looked or seemed ready to collapse 这房子似乎摇摇欲坠 
⑦ predicative (in need of) 需要的 xūyào de ready for [something] 需要某物的 I really am ready for bed/a holiday! 我真的很想睡觉/度假！ you look ready for a good meal 你看起来需要好好吃一顿 I feel ready for a rest 我想休息一下

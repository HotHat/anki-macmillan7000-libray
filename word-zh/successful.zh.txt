successful 
BrE səkˈsɛsfʊl, səkˈsɛsf(ə)l, AmE səkˈsɛsfəl

② (having high status) 有成就的 yǒu chéngjiù de ‹politician, actor›
③ (popular, well regarded) 受好评的 shòu hǎopíng de (doing well) 畅销的 chàngxiāo de (profitable) 卖座的 màizuò de this film was less successful 这部电影的票房差一些 
④ (winning, getting through) 获胜的 huòshèng de ‹team, candidate›; 通过的 tōngguò de ‹application›to be successful in an exam 通过考试 
⑤ (happy) 一帆风顺的 yī fān fēng shùn de ‹relationship, partnership›

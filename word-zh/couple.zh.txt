couple 
BrE ˈkʌp(ə)l, AmE ˈkəpəl 

A.  noun 
① (married) 夫妻 fūqī ; (romantic) 情侣 qínglǚ 
② (two objects or people) 两个 liǎng gè a couple of girls 两个女孩 
③ (a few) 几个 jǐ gè a couple of times 几次 
B.  transitive verb 
① literal 连接 liánjiē the restaurant car was coupled (on) to the last coach 餐车挂在最后一节车厢上 
② figurative 把…联系起来 bǎ… liánxì qilai ‹people, things, ideas›to couple (the name of) Bill Gates with Microsoft 把比尔盖茨（的名字）和微软联系起来

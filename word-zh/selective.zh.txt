selective 
BrE sɪˈlɛktɪv, AmE səˈlɛktɪv 
adjective 
① (based on selection) 有选择的 yǒu xuǎnzé de ‹admission, recruitment›a selective school 择优录取学生的学校 
② predicative (discriminating) 精挑细选的 jīng tiāo xì xuǎn de to be selective about or in [something]; 对某事物很讲究 to be selective about what one reads 对所读的东西很挑剔 she should be more selective in the friends she makes 她挑选朋友时应该更小心些

dose 
BrE dəʊs, AmE doʊs 

A.  noun 
① (of medicine) 一次的剂量 yī cì de jìliàng to give or administer a dose of cough mixture 给服一剂咳嗽药 in small/large doses 以小/大剂量 I can only stand her in small doses 我只能忍受她一小会儿 like a dose of salts colloquial 很快地 
② colloquial a dose of [something]; (of sth. unpleasant) 一次 ‹flu, deflation, conversation› (of sth. pleasant) 一点 yīdiǎn ‹optimism, originality, good luck, fresh air›
B.  transitive verb 给…服药 gěi… fúyào to dose [somebody] (up) with painkillers 给某人服用止痛药 
C.  reflexive verb to dose oneself up 服药 fúyào to dose oneself (up) with sleeping pills 服用安眠药 fúyòng ānmiányào

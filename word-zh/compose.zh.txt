compose 
BrE kəmˈpəʊz, AmE kəmˈpoʊz 

A.  transitive verb 
① (constitute) 组成 zǔchéng ‹whole, compound, proportion, part›to be composed of …; 由…组成 
② Literature, Music 创作 chuàngzuò ‹music, literary work›
③ (write carefully) 撰写 zhuànxiě ‹letter, speech›the first sentence is so hard to compose 第一个句子很难下笔 
④ (create) 构思 gòusī ‹picture, photograph›
⑤ (order) 整理 zhěnglǐ ‹thoughts, elements of work›it was a shock, but she composed her features and restrained her tears 那件事令人震惊，但是她调整好表情，忍住了泪水 
⑥ Printing 给…排版 gěi… páibǎn ‹text, book›
B.  intransitive verb Music 创作 chuàngzuò 
C.  reflexive verb to compose oneself; 平静下来 píngjìng xialai

bounce 
BrE baʊns, AmE baʊns 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (rebound) «ball» 反弹 fǎntán ; «light, sound, signal» 反射 fǎnshè 
② (move up and down repeatedly) «ball» 弹跳 tántiào ; «vehicle» 颠簸 diānbǒ (jump) «person» 蹦跳 bèngtiào to bounce up and down on [something] 在某物上弹跳 the car bounced along the track 小轿车在小道上颠簸行进 
③ figurative (move energetically) 蹦蹦跳跳 bèngbèng-tiàotiào 
④ Finance colloquial «cheque» 被退回 bèi tuìhuí 
B.  transitive verb 
① (cause to rebound) 使反弹 shǐ fǎntán ; (reflect) 反射 fǎnshè ‹radio waves, signal›he bounced the ball off the neighbour's gate 他把球掷到邻居家大门上弹了回来 
② (repeatedly) 使跳动 shǐ tiàodòng to bounce a baby on one's knee 把婴儿放在膝上颠着玩 
③ Finance colloquial (refuse to honour) 拒付 jùfù ‹cheque›(return) 退回 tuìhuí ‹cheque›
C.  noun 
① Countable(rebound of ball) 反弹 fǎntán 
② Uncountable(of mattress, ball, hair) 弹性 tánxìng 
③ Uncountable and countablefigurative (vigour) 活力 huólì PHRASAL VERB bounce back intransitive verb 重整旗鼓 chóng zhěng qí gǔ

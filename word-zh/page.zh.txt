page

BrE peɪdʒ, AmE peɪdʒ 

A.  noun 
① (in book, newspaper) 页 yè at the top or head of the page 在页眉 at the foot or bottom of the page 在页脚 the sports/women's page 体育/女性版 
② Computing 页面 yèmiàn 
③ figurative (episode) 一段历史 yī duàn lìshǐ a page in the history of the world 世界史上的一页 
B.  transitive verb 
① (over loudspeaker) 通过广播呼叫 tōngguò guǎngbō hūjiào paging Mr Jones 呼叫琼斯先生 
② (on pager) 用寻呼机呼叫 yòng xúnhūjī hūjiào 
③ (paginate) 为…标页码 wèi… biāo yèmǎ ‹book›
C.  intransitive verb 
① (turn pages) to page backwards and forwards 前后翻阅 qiánhòu fānyuè to page through a magazine 翻阅杂志 fānyuè zázhì 
② Computing to page up/down; 向上/向下翻页 xiàng shàng/xiàng xià fān yè

interview 
BrE ˈɪntəvjuː, AmE ˈɪn(t)ərˌvju 

A.  noun 
① (for job etc.) 面试 miànshì to be called/invited for (an) interview 接到通知/受邀面试 
② (on TV, radio) 采访 cǎifǎng to hold or conduct an interview with [somebody]; 采访某人 in an interview with … 在对…的采访中 
③ (formal discussion) 面谈 miàntán a career's interview 择业咨询面谈 
④ (by police) 审问 shěnwèn 
⑤ (in survey) 访谈 fǎngtán 
B.  transitive verb 
① (for job etc.) 对…进行面试 duì… jìnxíng miànshì ‹applicant›
② (on TV, radio) 采访 cǎifǎng 
③ (question formally) «police» 问讯 wènxùn ‹suspect›
④ (in survey) 访谈 fǎngtán 
C.  intransitive verb 面试表现 miànshì biǎoxiàn to interview well/badly 面试表现好/差

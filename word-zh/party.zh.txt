party 
BrE ˈpɑːti, AmE ˈpɑrdi 

A.  noun 
① (social event) 聚会 jùhuì a Christmas/New Year's Eve party 圣诞/除夕晚会 a dinner party 宴会 to give or have a party (for [somebody]/[something]); （为某人/某事）举行聚会 a pretty party frock 漂亮的女式宴会礼服 
② (organized group) 团体 tuántǐ a party of tourists/children 旅游团/儿童团队 
③ Politics 党派 dǎngpài a political party 政党 a right-wing/left-wing/centre party 右翼/左翼/中间党派 the Conservative/Labour Party 保守党/工党 the Party (ruling party) 执政党 (Communist Party) 共产党 party policy 政党政策 
④ Law (person) 当事人 dāngshìrén a party to an agreement/a contract 协议/合同的一方 the party of the first part 甲方 a party to the action or suit 诉讼方 the innocent/guilty party 无罪/有罪的一方 to be (a) party to …; 参与… to make [somebody] (a) party to [something]; 使某人参与某事 
B.  intransitive verb colloquial 狂欢 kuánghuān they partied all night 他们寻欢作乐了一整夜 let's go party 咱们去玩玩吧

interested 
BrE ˈɪnt(ə)rɪstɪd, AmE ˈɪntrəstəd 
adjective 
① (taking or showing interest) 感兴趣的 gǎn xìngqù de to be interested in [somebody]/[something]; 对某人/某事物感兴趣 he had an interested look on his face 他脸上露出好奇的表情 he is interested in history 他喜欢历史 I shall be interested to know what happens 我想知道会发生什么事 
② (not impartial) 有利害关系的 yǒu lìhài guānxì de as an interested party, I was not allowed to vote 作为利害相关的一方，我不得投票 the interested parties Law 各当事人

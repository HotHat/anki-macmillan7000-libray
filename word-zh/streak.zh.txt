streak 
BrE striːk, AmE strik 

A.  noun 
① (of paint, substance, water) 条痕 tiáohén ; (of light) 光带 guāngdài a streak of lightning 一道闪电 
② (in character) 性格特征 xìnggé tèzhēng to have a streak of [something] in one's character 性格中有某种特点 a cruel/mean streak, a streak of cruelty/meanness 残忍/吝啬的本性 he's got a real mean streak in him 他真是卑鄙无耻 a yellow streak colloquial 懦弱的性格 
③ (run) 一阵子 yīzhènzi to be on a winning/losing streak 一切顺风顺水/在走背运 a lucky/unlucky streak 走运/不走运的时期 
④ colloquial (naked run) 裸奔 luǒbēn to do a streak 进行裸奔 
B.  transitive verb 
① (cover or mark with lines) «person, colour, light» 在…上留下条纹 zài… shang liúxia tiáowén ‹face, clothes, sky›; «tears, colour, mud» 在…上留下痕迹 zài… shang liúxia hénjì ‹face, clothes, window›to streak [something] with [something]; 在某物上留下一道道某物 to be streaked with [something] 布满一道道的某物 
② (dye) 把…条染 bǎ… tiáorǎn to streak [somebody's] hair 把某人的头发染成一绺一绺的 streaked hair 染得一绺一绺的头发 
C.  intransitive verb 
① (move fast) «person» 飞跑 fēipǎo ; «train» 疾驰 jíchí ; «aircraft, rocket» 高速飞行 gāosù fēixíng to streak across or through [something]; 飞驰过某物 to streak off 飞奔而去 
② colloquial (run naked) 裸奔 luǒbēn

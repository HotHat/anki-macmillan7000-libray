mature 
BrE məˈtʃʊə, AmE məˈtʃʊr, məˈtʊr 

A.  adjective 
① (fully grown, developed) 成熟的 chéngshú de ‹animal, plant, tree, market, piece of work›she was now physically mature 她如今身体已发育成熟 
② (sensible) 明智的 míngzhì de ‹attitude›; 懂事的 dǒngshì de ‹child›a mature and sensible attitude 一副深谙世事的态度 
③ euphemistic (middle-aged, old) 中年的 zhōngnián de a person of mature years or a mature age 中年人 
④ (adult) 成年的 chéngnián de 
⑤ formal (careful, thorough) 深思熟虑的 shēnsī shúlǜ de after or upon mature reflection/consideration 经过深思熟虑/慎重考虑 
⑥ Culinary 发酵成熟的 fājiào chéngshú de ‹cheese, wine, whisky›
B.  intransitive verb 
① (physically, psychologically) 成熟 chéngshú 
② Culinary «wine, whisky, cheese» 发酵成熟 fājiào chéngshú 
③ Finance «bill, loan, policy» 到期 dàoqī 
C.  transitive verb 使…发酵成熟 shǐ… fājiào chéngshú ‹cheese, whisky, wine›

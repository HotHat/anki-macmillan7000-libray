prohibit 
BrE prə(ʊ)ˈhɪbɪt, AmE prəˈhɪbɪt, proʊˈhɪbɪt 
transitive verb 
① (forbid) 禁止 jìnzhǐ ‹sale, weapon, drug›to prohibit [somebody] from doing [something]; 禁止某人做某事 smoking is prohibited 禁止吸烟 
② (make impossible) 阻止 zǔzhǐ ‹use, involvement, activity›to prohibit [somebody] from doing [something]; 阻止某人做某事 his poor health prohibits him from playing sports 他健康状况不佳，无法参加体育运动

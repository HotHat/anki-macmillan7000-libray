impose 
BrE ɪmˈpəʊz, AmE ɪmˈpoʊz 

A.  transitive verb 
① (enforce) 强制实行 qiángzhì shíxíng ‹restriction, penalty, sanctions›; 强行收取 qiángxíng shōuqǔ ‹charge, fine›to impose [something] on [somebody]; 把某事强加于某人 to impose a sentence of five years on [somebody] 判处某人5年徒刑 to impose silence on the class 硬让全班安静下来 to impose a tax 征税 
② (force on others) 将…强加于人 jiāng… qiángjiā yú rén to impose one's opinions on [somebody]; 勉强某人接受自己的想法 to impose one's presence on [somebody]; 让某人意识到自己在场 
B.  intransitive verb 利用 lìyòng to impose on [somebody's] kindness/hospitality 利用某人的仁慈/好客 I do hope I'm not imposing 真希望我没有添麻烦 
C.  reflexive verb to impose oneself on [somebody]; 使某人接受自己 shǐ mǒu rén jiēshòu zìjǐ

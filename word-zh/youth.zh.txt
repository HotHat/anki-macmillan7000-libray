youth 
BrE juːθ, AmE juθ 
noun 
① Uncountable(period of being young) 青年时期 qīngnián shíqī [尤指成年以前]in (the days of) my youth 在我年轻时 in early youth 在少年时期 
② Uncountable(state of being young) 年轻 niánqīng because of/despite his youth 因为/尽管他年轻 full of youth and vitality 充满青春活力的 youth will have its way or its fling proverb 年轻人不会受约束 
③ Countable(plural youths /juːðz/) (young man) 青年男子 qīngnián nánzǐ a gang of youths 一帮小伙子 
④ plus singular or plural verb (young people) 青年人 qīngniánrén the voice of youth 年轻人的呼声

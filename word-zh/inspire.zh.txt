inspire 
BrE ɪnˈspʌɪə, AmE ɪnˈspaɪ(ə)r 
transitive verb 
① (motivate) 鼓舞 gǔwǔ to inspire [somebody] to do [something]; 激励某人做某事 to inspire [somebody] to greater efforts 激励某人更加努力 
② (give rise to) 使产生 shǐ chǎnshēng ‹thought, feeling›what inspired you to suggest that? 是什么使你想到那个建议的？ 
③ (arouse) 激起 jīqǐ to inspire love/respect/trust in [somebody] 唤起某人的爱/尊敬/信任 to inspire [somebody] with hope/courage 激起某人的希望/勇气

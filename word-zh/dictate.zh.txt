dictate 
A.  /BrE dɪkˈteɪt, AmE ˈdɪkˌteɪt, ˌdɪkˈteɪt
transitive verb 
① (read out) 口述 kǒushù to dictate [something] (to [somebody]); （向某人）口授某事 to dictate a letter into a dictating machine 往口述记录机里口述一封信 
② (prescribe) 强加 qiángjiā ‹conditions›; 支配 zhīpèi ‹actions, attitude›to dictate terms (to [somebody]) （向某人）发号施令 to dictate that …; 强行规定… lifestyle is dictated by income 生活方式是由收入决定的 
B.  /BrE dɪkˈteɪt, AmE ˈdɪkˌteɪt, ˌdɪkˈteɪt
intransitive verb 
① (read aloud) 口述 kǒushù to dictate to [somebody]; 向某人口述 
② (boss sb. around) 发号施令 fāhào shīlìng to dictate to [somebody]; 向某人发号施令 
C.  /BrE ˈdɪkteɪt, AmE ˈdɪkˌteɪt, ˌdɪkˈteɪt
noun 
① (decree) 命令 mìnglìng 
② dictates plural (promptings, precepts) 支配 zhīpèi the dictates of [something]; 某物的支配 to follow the dictates of one's conscience/passion 凭良心办事/感情用事

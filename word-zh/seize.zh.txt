seize 
BrE siːz, AmE siz 

A.  transitive verb 
① literal (take hold of) 抓住 zhuāzhù to seize hold of [somebody]/[something]; 抓住某人/某物 to seize [somebody] around the waist 揽住某人的腰 to seize [somebody] by the arm/hair 揪住某人的胳膊/头发 
② figurative (make use of, grasp) 利用 lìyòng to seize an opportunity to … 抓住机会来… to seize the initiative 把握主动 
③ figurative (affect) 攫住 juézhù to be seized by fear/pain 感到一阵恐惧/疼痛 seized by or with a sudden impulse 一时冲动 I was seized by a sudden desire to tell her everything 我突然想把一切都告诉她 
④ Military (capture) 夺取 duóqǔ rebel troops seized the television station 叛军占领了电视台 
⑤ (kidnap, abduct) 绑架 bǎngjià 
⑥ (confiscate) 没收 mòshōu ; (apprehend, arrest) 拘押 jūyā 
B.  intransitive verb = seize up PHRASAL VERBS seize on transitive verb = seize upon seize up intransitive verb 
① (cease to operate) «engine, mechanism» 停止运转 tíngzhǐ yùnzhuǎn ; «piston» 卡住 qiǎzhù 
② (stop moving) 活动困难 huódòng kùnnan my back has seized up 我的背发僵 seize upon transitive verb[seize upon something] 抓住…以利用 zhuāzhù… yǐ lìyòng he immediately seized upon the weakness in the argument 他一下子抓住了论点中的漏洞

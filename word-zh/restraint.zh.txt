restraint 
BrE rɪˈstreɪnt, AmE rəˈstreɪnt 
noun 
① Uncountable(control) 控制 kòngzhì to put or place [somebody]/[something] under restraint 将某人/某物控制起来 
② Countable(restriction) 限制 xiànzhì to impose restraints on [something] 对某事物实施限制 subject to restraints 受到限制的 a restraint on the freedom of expression 对言论自由的限制 
③ Uncountable(moderation) 克制 kèzhì to show or exercise restraint 表现出克制 lack of restraint 无所顾忌 
④ Countable(rope, strap) [用于限制精神病人、囚犯等活动自由的] 束缚索带 shùfù suǒdài

yesterday 
BrE ˈjɛstədeɪ, ˈjɛstədi, AmE ˈjɛstərˌdeɪ, ˈjɛstərdi 

A.  noun 
① literal 昨天 zuótiān the day before yesterday 前天 yesterday was the third/Monday 昨天是3号/星期一 what was yesterday's date? 昨天是几号？ yesterday's Times 昨天的《泰晤士报》 yesterday's news 过时新闻 
② figurative (the past) 不久前 bùjiǔ qián yesterday's fashions 前一阵流行的款式 yesterday's man pejorative 昨日之星 all our yesterdays literary 我们的往昔岁月 tales of yesterday 旧时传说 
B.  adverb 
① literal 在昨天 zài zuótiān all (day) yesterday 昨天全天 yesterday morning/afternoon/evening/night 昨天上午/下午/傍晚/晚上 early/late yesterday 昨天早些/晚些时候 it snowed yesterday 昨天下雪了 I saw her only yesterday 我昨天才见到她 a week ago yesterday 一个星期前的昨天 it was yesterday week or a week yesterday 是在一周前的昨天 to remember [something] as if it were yesterday 记得某事就像是昨天刚发生似的 
② figurative (in the past) 在不久前 zài bùjiǔ qián everything seems to have been built only yesterday 一切似乎是昨天才建成的

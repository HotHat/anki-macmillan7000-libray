misfortune 
BrE mɪsˈfɔːtʃuːn, mɪsˈfɔːtʃ(ə)n, AmE ˌmɪsˈfɔrtʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Uncountable(bad luck) 厄运 èyùn we had the misfortune to break down halfway 我们真倒霉，半路车抛锚了 that's your misfortune 你自认倒霉吧 
② Countable(unfortunate event) 不幸的事 bùxìng de shì she had the misfortune to lose the sight of one eye 她不幸一只眼失明了

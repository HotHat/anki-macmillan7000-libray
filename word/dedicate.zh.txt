dedicate 
BrE ˈdɛdɪkeɪt, AmE ˈdɛdəˌkeɪt 

A.  transitive verb 
① (devote) 献出 xiànchū to dedicate [somebody]/[something] to [something]; 把…奉献给某事物 ‹time, energy›she dedicated her life to … 她毕生致力于… to be dedicated to [somebody]/[something]; 对某人一心一意/致力于某事物 
② (cite) 在…上题献词 zài… shang tí xiàncí to dedicate [something] to [somebody]; 把…献给某人 ‹book, record, performance, film›
③ Religion 为…举行奉献典礼 wèi… jǔxíng fèngxiàn diǎnlǐ ‹church, chapel, temple›; 为…举行落成仪式 wèi… jǔxíng luòchéng yíshì ‹monument›
B.  reflexive verb to dedicate oneself to [something]; 献身于某事 xiànshēn yú mǒu shì

transitive verb

② (inspect intellectually) 调查 diàochá ‹facts, problem, evidence›; 审查 shěnchá ‹documents, accounts›
③ School, University 对…进行考试 duì… jìnxíng kǎoshì ‹candidate, student›to examine [somebody] in or on [something]; 测试某人某课程 
④ Law 审问 shěnwèn ‹accused, suspect›; 调查 diàochá ‹case›

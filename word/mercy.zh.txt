mercy 
BrE ˈməːsi, AmE ˈmərsi 
noun 
① Uncountable(clemency) 仁慈 réncí to show mercy to or towards [somebody]; 对某人表示怜悯 to have mercy on [somebody]; 宽待某人 to beg for mercy; 乞求怜悯 in one's mercy 出于恻隐之心 for mercy's sake! colloquial dated 看在上帝的份上！ 
② Uncountable and countable(power to show mercy) 宽容力 kuānrónglì to be at the mercy of [somebody]/[something]; 任凭某人/某事摆布 to leave [somebody] to the mercy or mercies of [somebody] 听任某人受某人的摆布 to throw oneself on [somebody's] mercy; 指望得到某人的宽恕 
③ Countable(fortunate event) 幸运的事 xìngyùn de shì (relief) 解脱 jiětuō to be thankful or grateful for small mercies 庆幸不利情况略有改善

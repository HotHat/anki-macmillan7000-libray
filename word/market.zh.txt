market 
BrE ˈmɑːkɪt, AmE ˈmɑrkət 

A.  noun 
① (place to buy goods) 市场 shìchǎng an antique(s) market 古玩市场 
② (gathering for buying/selling) 集市 jíshì a monthly market 每月一度的集市 
③ (stock market) 股票市场 gǔpiào shìchǎng to play the market 炒股票 
④ (area of trade) 销售地 xiāoshòudì the tea market, the market in tea 茶叶市场 the job/property market 就业/房地产市场 to put [something] on the market; 在市场上出售某物 to be in the market for [something]; 有意购买某物 a rising market in shares 上涨的股票行情 to come on to the market; 上市 to price [something] out of the market 对某物漫天要价致使无人问津 
⑤ (customers) 消费群体 xiāofèi qúntǐ the French market 法国市场 the teenage market 青少年为主的市场 before noun market conditions/trends 市场情况/走向 
⑥ (demand) 销路 xiāolù a good market for cars 良好的汽车销路 there is no market for [something]; 某物没有销路 
B.  transitive verb 
① (sell) 销售 xiāoshòu 
② (promote) 推销 tuīxiāo to market [something] (to [somebody]) （向某人）推销某物

denial 
BrE dɪˈnʌɪ(ə)l, AmE dəˈnaɪəl 
noun 
① Countable and uncountable(of accusation) 否认 fǒurèn (of request) 拒绝 jùjué his denial that he was the culprit 他否认自己是罪犯 a denial of [something]; 对某事的否认 a flat/firm denial 断然/坚决的否认 
② Countable and uncountable(refusal to believe) 拒绝相信 jùjué xiāngxìn 
③ Countable(disowning) 脱离关系 tuōlí guānxì a traitor's denial of his native land 一个叛国者对祖国的背弃 
④ Countable and uncountable(withholding) 拒绝给予 jùjué jǐyǔ a denial of basic human rights/resources 基本人权/资源的剥夺 
⑤ UncountablePsychology 拒绝接受 jùjué jiēshòu in denial; 拒不承认 I was an addict in denial 我拒不接受我是瘾君子的事实

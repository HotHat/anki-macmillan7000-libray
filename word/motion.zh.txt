motion 
BrE ˈməʊʃ(ə)n, AmE ˈmoʊʃ(ə)n 

A.  noun 
① Uncountable(movement) 运动 yùndòng to be in motion 处于运动中 to set or put [something] in motion; literal 使某物开始运动 figurative 使某事开始运作 shǐ mǒu shì kāishǐ yùnzuò to set the wheels in motion figurative 使一切开始运转 
② Countable(gesture) (of hands) 手势 shǒushì (of head, body) 姿势 zīshì he made a motion to close the door 他做了个关门的手势 to go through the motions (of doing [something]) （做某事）走过场 
③ Countable(proposal) 动议 dòngyì a motion to do [something]; 做某事的动议 to table/second the motion 提出动议/对动议提出附议 to carry or pass/defeat or reject the motion by
10 votes to
8 以10票对8票通过/否决动议 a motion of censure 不信任动议 a Commons motion BrE 下院的提议 
④ Countable BrE formal (emptying of bowels) 排便 páibiàn to have a motion 排便 páibiàn 
⑤ Countable BrE formal (faeces) 粪便 fènbiàn 
B.  intransitive verb 做手势 zuò shǒushì to motion to or for [somebody] (to do [something]); 示意某人（做某事） he motioned for me to come over 他招呼我过去 
C.  transitive verb 向…做手势 xiàng… zuò shǒushì to motion [somebody] in/out; 示意某人进来/出去

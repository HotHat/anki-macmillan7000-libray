scout 
▶noun
1 scouts reported the enemy's position: lookout, outrider, advance guard, vanguard; spy.
2 a lengthy scout around the area: reconnaissance, reconnoiter; exploration, search, expedition; informal recon.
3 a scout for a major-league team: talent spotter, talent scout; informal bird dog.
▶verb
1 I scouted around for some logs: search, look, hunt, ferret about/around, root around/about.
2 a night patrol was sent to scout out the area: reconnoiter, explore, make a reconnaissance of, inspect, investigate, spy out, survey; examine, scan, study, observe; informal check out, case.

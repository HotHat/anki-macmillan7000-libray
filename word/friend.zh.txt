friend 
BrE frɛnd, AmE frɛnd 
noun 
① (person one likes) 朋友 péngyou to make friends; 交朋友 to make a friend of [somebody]; 把某人变成朋友 to make/be friends with [somebody]; 和某人交朋友 to be the best of friends 是最好的朋友 let's be friends! (after quarrel) 我们和好吧！ a friend of the family 亲友 with friends like him/her, who needs enemies? 有他/她这样的损友，谁还需要敌人？ a friend in need is a friend indeed proverb 患难朋友才是真朋友 
② figurative (ally) 支持者 zhīchízhě the party has many friends in industry 该党在工业界有许多支持者 to have friends in high places 有贵人相助 to be a friend of or to the poor 是穷人的同情者 
③ (fellow member) 同志 tóngzhì 
④ (patron) 赞助者 zànzhùzhě Friends of the Cathedral 大教堂之友 
⑤ (mode of address) 朋友 péngyou [用作称呼]come here, my friend, don't be shy 到这儿来，我的朋友，别不好意思

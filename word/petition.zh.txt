petition 
BrE pɪˈtɪʃ(ə)n, AmE pəˈtɪʃ(ə)n 

A.  noun 
① (document) 请愿书 qǐngyuànshū to get up a petition (for/against [something]); 组织请愿（要求/反对某事物） to present a petition (to [somebody]) （向某人）递交请愿书 
② (appeal, request) 祈求 qíqiú 
③ Law 诉状 sùzhuàng to file a petition for [something]; 提出申诉要求某事 
B.  transitive verb 请求 qǐngqiú to petition ([somebody]) for [something]; 请求（某人）做某事 they petitioned Parliament for a change in the law 他们向议会请愿，要求修改法律 to petition for divorce 要求离婚 to petition [somebody] to do [something]; 请求某人做某事

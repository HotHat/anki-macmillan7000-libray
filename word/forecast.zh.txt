forecast 
BrE ˈfɔːkɑːst, AmE ˈfɔrˌkæst 

A.  noun 
① Meteorology 天气预报 tiānqì yùbào 
② (prediction, estimation) 预测 yùcè to make forecasts about [something]; 对某事进行预测 a racing forecast 赛马结果预测 
B.  transitive verb(past tense, past participle forecast or forecasted) 
① Meteorology 预报 yùbào sunshine is forecast for tomorrow 预报明日天气晴好 
② (predict, estimate) «analyst, fortune teller» 预测 yùcè ‹profit, good luck, winner›to forecast that …; 预测… to forecast [something]; 预测某事 to be forecast to do [something]; 被预测会做某事 as forecast 如预测的一样

volunteer 
BrE ˌvɒlənˈtɪə, AmE ˌvɑlənˈtɪr 

A.  noun 
① (willing participant) 自告奋勇者 zì gào fènyǒng zhě 
② (unpaid worker) 志愿者 zhìyuànzhě a volunteer helper 自愿提供服务者 
③ Military 志愿兵 zhìyuànbīng China's national anthem is the March of the Volunteers 中国国歌是《义勇军进行曲》 a volunteer army 志愿军 
B.  transitive verb 
① (offer willingly) 自愿提供 zìyuàn tígōng ‹services, help›to volunteer to join/serve 自愿加入/服务 
② (divulge willingly) 主动提供 zhǔdòng tígōng ‹information, advice, explanation›‘it was me,’he volunteered “是我，”他主动说 
③ (nominate) [尤指擅自地] 举荐 jǔjiàn ‹person›to volunteer [somebody] for [something]; 指派某人担当某事 
C.  intransitive verb 
① (offer services, with or without payment) 自愿服务 zìyuàn fúwù to volunteer for [something]; 自愿为某事服务 
② Military 当志愿兵 dāng zhìyuànbīng to volunteer for military service/the army 志愿服兵役/参军

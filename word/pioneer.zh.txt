pioneer 
BrE pʌɪəˈnɪə, AmE ˌpaɪəˈnɪr 

A.  noun 
① (innovator in activity, field of knowledge) 先驱 xiānqū a pioneer study 开创性的研究 
② (explorer) 探索者 tànsuǒzhě 
③ (settler) 拓荒者 tuòhuāngzhě a pioneer settler/settlement 拓荒者/拓荒者居留地 
④ (soldier) 轻工兵 qīnggōngbīng 
B.  transitive verb 
① (develop, use first) 开创 kāichuàng ‹technique, process, invention›to pioneer the use/study of … 倡导…的使用/研究 
② (explore, settle) 开辟 kāipì ‹route, settlement›

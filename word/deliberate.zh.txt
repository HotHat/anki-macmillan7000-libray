deliberate 
A.  /BrE dɪˈlɪb(ə)rət, AmE dəˈlɪb(ə)rət
adjective 
① (intentional) 故意的 gùyì de ‹mistake, insult, lie, act›
② (measured) 慎重的 shènzhòng de ‹decision, judgement, reasoning›; 从容的 cóngróng de ‹manner›she has a deliberate way of talking 她说起话来不紧不慢的 
B.  /BrE dɪˈlɪbəreɪt, AmE dɪˈlɪbəˌreɪt
intransitive verb 
① (discuss) 仔细讨论 zǐxì tǎolùn to deliberate on or about or over [something]; 仔细讨论某事 
② (reflect) 认真考虑 rènzhēn kǎolǜ to deliberate on or about or over [something]; 仔细考虑某事 
C.  /BrE dɪˈlɪbəreɪt, AmE dɪˈlɪbəˌreɪt
transitive verb 
① (discuss) 仔细讨论 zǐxì tǎolùn 
② (consider) 反复思考 fǎnfù sīkǎo to deliberate what to do/whether to do [something]; 认真考虑做什么/是否做某事

bundle 
▶noun a bundle of clothes: bunch, roll, clump, wad, parcel, sheaf, bale, bolt; package; pile, stack, heap, mass; informal load.
▶verb
1 she bundled up her things: tie, pack, parcel, wrap, roll, fold, bind, bale, package.
2 she was bundled in furs: wrap, envelop, clothe, cover, muffle, swathe, swaddle, shroud, drape, enfold.
3 informal he was bundled into a van: push, shove, thrust, manhandle, hurry, rush.

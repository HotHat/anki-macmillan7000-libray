beneath 
BrE bɪˈniːθ, AmE bəˈniθ 

A.  preposition 
① (lower than, underneath) 在…下方 zài… xiàfāng (to a lower position than) 到…下方 dào… xiàfāng beneath the table 在桌子底下 her name was beneath mine on the list 名单上她的名字排在我的下面 deep beneath the earth 地下深处 
② (concealed by) 在…的掩盖下 zài… de yǎngài xia he hid his disappointment beneath a polite smile 他礼貌地微笑着以掩饰失望 
③ (inferior in rank to) 低于 dīyú the men beneath him 比他级别低的人 
④ (unworthy of) 有失…的身份 yǒushī… de shēnfèn to be beneath [somebody] to do [something]; 做某事有失某人的身份 
B.  adverb 
① (lower down, underneath) 在下面 zài xiàmian the valley/river beneath 下面的山谷/河流 the apartment beneath 楼下的公寓单元 
② (concealed) 掩藏着 yǎncángzhe the smile revealed the evil beneath 微笑中透出内心的邪恶

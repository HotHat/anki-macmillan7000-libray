orbit 
▶noun
1 the monthly orbit of the Moon: course, path, circuit, track, trajectory, rotation, revolution, circle; rare circumgyration.
2 the problem comes outside our orbit: sphere, sphere of influence, area of activity, range, scope, ambit, compass, jurisdiction, authority, domain, realm, province, territory, turf; informal bailiwick.
▶verb Mercury orbits the Sun: revolve around, circle around, go around, travel around.

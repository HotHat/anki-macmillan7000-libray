halfway 
BrE hɑːfˈweɪ, ˈhɑːfweɪ, AmE ˈhæfˌweɪ 

A.  adverb 
① (in distance) 在中间 zài zhōngjiān [something] halfway between A and B 在A和B中间的某物 halfway down the page 在下半页中间部分 
② (in journey) 在中途 zài zhōngtú to stop halfway 在半路上停下 
③ (in time, process) 到一半 dào yībàn halfway through the meal 饭吃到一半时 
④ figurative 部分地 bùfen de to be halfway there 成功了一半 to go halfway to or towards [something]/doing [something] 做某事完成了一部分 to meet [somebody] halfway 对某人让步 to meet trouble halfway 预先考虑到麻烦 
⑤ colloquial (fairly) 相当地 xiāngdāng de ‹decent, competent›
B.  adjective 中途的 zhōngtú de ‹mark›; 中间的 zhōngjiān de ‹point, stage›

balance 
BrE ˈbal(ə)ns, AmE ˈbæləns 

A.  noun 
① Uncountable(of body) 平衡 pínghéng to lose/keep one's balance 失去/保持平衡 to throw [somebody] off balance literal 使某人失去平衡 figurative 使某人不知所措 shǐ mǒu rén bù zhī suǒ cuò 
② Uncountable and countable(between different elements) 均衡 jūnhéng political balance 政治上的均势 the right balance of sweetness and spiciness 甜与辣的恰当搭配 to achieve/keep a balance (between [something] and [something]) （在两事物之间）取得/保持平衡 to strike a balance (between [something] and [something]) （在两事物之间）找到折中办法 
③ Uncountable(mental stability) 镇定 zhèndìng 
④ Uncountable(of sound) 协调 xiétiáo 
⑤ Countable(scales) 天平 tiānpíng the balance may still swing in our favour figurative 胜利的天平可能还会朝我们这边倾斜 to be or hang in the balance 悬而未决 on balance 总的说来 
⑥ Countable(in clock, watch) 平衡轮 pínghénglún 
⑦ Uncountable(preponderance) 大部分 dàbùfen the balance of opinion was … 多数人认为… 
⑧ CountableFinance, Busn (in account) 余额 yú'é balance brought forward 余额承前 balance carried forward 余额结转下期 balance in hand 现有余额 
⑨ CountableFinance, Busn (money still owed) 结欠款 jié qiànkuǎn (money still remaining) 余款 yúkuǎn 
⑩ Countable(remaining amount or part) 剩余部分 shèngyú bùfen the balance of [somebody's] annual leave 某人未休的年假 
B.  transitive verb 
① (perch) 使保持平衡 shǐ bǎochí pínghéng he balanced the bottle on top of the heap of stones 他把瓶子在石堆顶上放稳 
② (weigh same as) 和…重量相等 hé… zhòngliàng xiāngděng 
③ (create symmetry with) 与…对称 yǔ… duìchèn you need something to balance the vase on that side 你得找样东西来照应那边的花瓶 
④ balance (out) (compensate for) 和…相抵 hé… xiāngdǐ his lack of experience was balanced by his willingness to learn 他的好学弥补了经验的不足 
⑤ (adjust) 使…均衡 shǐ… jūnhéng ‹composition, diet, timetable›to balance work and family life 兼顾工作与家庭 
⑥ (weigh up, compare) 权衡 quánhéng ‹plans, suggestions, solutions›to balance [something] against [something]; 比较两事物 
⑦ Finance, Busn 使…收支平衡 shǐ… shōuzhī pínghéng ‹expenses, budget, economy›to balance the books 结平账目 
⑧ Auto 使…平衡 shǐ… pínghéng ‹wheels›
⑨ Mathematics 使…平衡 shǐ… pínghéng ‹equation›
C.  intransitive verb 
① (be perched) 保持平衡 bǎochí pínghéng to balance on [something]; 在某物上保持平衡 to balance on one leg 单腿站立 
② (weigh the same) 重量相等 zhòngliàng xiāngděng the scales balance 天平两边的秤盘平衡 
③ balance (out) (be compensated for) 相抵消 xiāng dǐxiāo the advantages and disadvantages balance out 利弊相抵 lìbì xiāngdǐ 
④ Finance «accounts, figures, budget» 收支平衡 shōuzhī pínghéng to make [something] balance, to get [something] to balance 使…收支平衡 shǐ… shōuzhī pínghéng 
⑤ Mathematics «equation, sides» 平衡 pínghéng

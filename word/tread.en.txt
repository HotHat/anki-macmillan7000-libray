tread 
▶verb

2 the snow had been trodden down by the horses: crush, flatten, press down, squash; trample on, tramp on, stamp on, stomp on.
▶noun we heard her heavy tread on the stairs: step, footstep, footfall, tramp, thump; clip-clop.

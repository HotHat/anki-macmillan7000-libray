moan 
BrE məʊn, AmE moʊn 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (cry, groan) 呻吟 shēnyín to moan in agony/with pleasure 痛苦地/快乐地呻吟 
② pejorative (complain) 抱怨 bàoyuàn to moan about [something]/[somebody]; 抱怨某事物/某人 the children moaned and groaned the entire journey 整个旅途中孩子们都在抱怨 
③ (make moaning sound) «wind» 呼啸 hūxiào the door moaned on its hinges 门在铰链上吱吱嘎嘎地响 
B.  transitive verb 
① (wail) 呻吟 shēnyín ‘help me,’he moaned “救命啊，”他呻吟道 
② (complain) 抱怨 bàoyuàn ‹dislike, dissatisfaction›to moan that …; 抱怨道… 
C.  noun 
① (wail, groan) 呻吟 shēnyín 
② often pejorative (complaint) 抱怨 bàoyuàn to have a moan about [something]/[somebody]; 抱怨某事物/某人 
③ (moaning sound) 呜咽 wūyè the moan of the wind through the trees 风掠过树丛的萧萧声

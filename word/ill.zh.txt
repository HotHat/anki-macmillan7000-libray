ill 
BrE ɪl, AmE ɪl 

A.  adjective 
① (having particular illness) 有病的 yǒubìng de to fall ill 生病 to be taken ill 病倒 she's ill with flu 她得了流感 terminally ill patients 病入膏肓的人 
② (unwell) 不舒服的 bù shūfu de to feel ill 感到不舒服 to look ill 气色不好 
③ (harmful, hostile) 坏的 huài de to suffer no ill effects 未受不良影响 it's an ill wind (that blows nobody any good) proverb 害于此者利于彼 
B.  adverb formal (scarcely) 几乎不 jīhū bù it ill becomes them to criticize us 他们无权批评我们 he ill deserves your praise 他不值得你表扬 
C.  noun 
① Uncountable(misfortune) 厄运 èyùn to wish [somebody] ill 希望某人倒霉 for good or ill 不论好坏 
② Countable(problem) 问题 wèntí the ills of city life/old age 城市生活的种种弊病/老年的种种苦恼 
D.  noun plural the ill 病人 bìngrén

reference 
BrE ˈrɛf(ə)r(ə)ns, AmE ˈrɛf(ə)rəns 

A.  noun 
① Countable(mention) 提及 tíjí the work is full of biblical references 这部作品通篇多处引用《圣经》 a reference to [somebody]/[something]; 对某人/某事物的提及 there are three references to his son in the article 这篇文章有三处提到了他儿子 to make a reference to [something] 提及某事物 a pointed reference (to [something]) （对某事物的）一针见血的谈及 
② Uncountable(allusion) 引用 yǐnyòng to make reference to [somebody]/[something] 引用某人的话/某作品 
③ Uncountable(consideration) 考虑 kǎolǜ to do [something] with/without reference to [somebody]/[something] 考虑/不考虑某人/某事物就做某事 
④ Uncountable(consultation) 参考 cānkǎo for easy reference, we recommend the pocket edition 为方便查阅，我们推荐袖珍本 for future reference 以备日后参考 for future reference, dogs are not allowed 今后请注意，禁止狗入内 a work of reference 参考书 cānkǎoshū ‘for reference only’ (on library book) “仅供查阅” to do [something] without reference to [somebody]/[something] 不征求…就做某事 ‹opinion› 不请示…就做某事 bù qǐngshì… jiù zuò mǒu shì ‹superior›the publishers reprinted and sold the work without reference to the author 出版商未征求作者意见就重印并销售这部作品 
⑤ Uncountable(connection) 关系 guānxì reference to [somebody]/[something]; 与某人/某事物的关系 this has no reference to the matter in hand 这与手头的问题没关系 
⑥ Countable(note) 引文出处 yǐnwén chūchù a reference to [somebody]/[something]; 关于某人/某事的引文 
⑦ Countable(source of information) 参考文献 cānkǎo wénxiàn what are your references for this? 你的这份东西的参考文献是哪些？ a list of references 参考书目 to cite a reference 引用文献 
⑧ CountableBusn (on letter, memo) 编号 biānhào 
⑨ Countable(testimonial) 推荐信 tuījiànxìn to give or write [somebody] a reference 给某人写推荐信 
⑩ Countable(referee) 推荐人 tuījiànrén to be/act as a reference for [somebody] 是/做某人的推荐人 
⑪ CountableGeography (on map) 标示 biāoshì the map reference is Y4 地图编号是Y4 
⑫ UncountableLinguistics 所指 suǒ zhǐ 
B.  preposition phrase formal with or in reference to …; 关于… guānyú… with reference to your letter of June 15, … 关于您6月15日的来信… with particular or specific reference to [somebody]/[something] 针对某人/某事物 zhēnduì mǒu rén/mǒu shìwù

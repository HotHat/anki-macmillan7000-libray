invite 
A.  /BrE ɪnˈvʌɪt, AmE ɪnˈvaɪt
transitive verb 
① (informally, socially) 邀请 yāoqǐng to invite [somebody] to do [something]; 邀请某人做某事 to invite [somebody] back; 回请某人 to invite [somebody] in; 邀请某人来家里做客 to invite [somebody] over or round (to one's house) 邀请某人（到家中）作客 to invite [somebody] to a party/for a drink 请某人参加聚会/喝酒 to invite [somebody] for interview; 约请某人参加面试 
② (request, elicit) 征求 zhēngqiú ‹opinions›to invite subscriptions 征订 he invited questions from the audience 他请观众提问 
③ figurative (encourage) 招致 zhāozhì why invite trouble? 为何自找麻烦？

what 
BrE wɒt, AmE (h)wət, (h)wɑt 
A. pronoun 
① (in direct and indirect questions) 什么 shénme what is happening? 出什么事了？ ask what he wants 问问他想要什么 what's up or wrong or the matter? 怎么回事？ what is to be done? 怎么办呢？ with/about what …? 用/关于什么？ and what else? 还有呢？ what's that, what did you say? 你说什么？ what do six and four add up to or make? 6加4等于几？ what's this called in Flemish?, what's the Flemish for this? 这个用佛兰芒语怎么说？ what did it cost? 这东西花了多少钱？ what's with [somebody]/[something]? colloquial 某人/某事怎么啦？ what do you think I am? colloquial 你把我看成什么人了？ what's it to you? colloquial 关你什么事？ what's the use? 这有什么用？ what's life without love? 没有爱的生活算什么生活？ he earns/did what? 他赚了/做了什么？ that's George — George what? 那是乔治──姓什么？ I asked her to marry me — you what? colloquial 我向她求婚了──你说什么？ is he stupid or what? colloquial 他真是傻透了 are we going or what? colloquial 我们走还是不走？ he's going after all — well, what do you know? colloquial ironic 他终于要走了──噢，真的吗？ 
② (asking what sb. wants) 什么事 shénme shì mummy! — what? 妈咪！──什么事？ 
③ (in clauses) (thing) …那样的事物 … nàyàng de shìwù (person) …那样的人 … nàyàng de rén and what's worse/better 更糟糕/更好的是 what we need is … 我们需要的是… what's surprising is that … 令人吃惊的是… you know what he is! 你知道他那种人！ she's not what she was 她已经不是原来的她了 it's good value for what it is 这东西物有所值 I or I'll tell you what 我来告诉你吧 to know what's what colloquial 知道轻重缓急 
④ (whatever) 无论什么 wúlùn shénme do what you want/have to 做你想做/必须做的事 and what not, and what have you colloquial 以及诸如此类的东西 toys, books, and what not 玩具、书籍等等 a hammer, a drill, and I don't know what 锤子、钻头之类的东西 
⑤ (in exclamations) 多么 duōme and what it must have cost! 它肯定花了很多钱！ 
B.  determiner 
① (which) 哪个 nǎge do you know what train he took? 你知道他乘坐的是哪班火车吗？ what magazines do you read? 你看哪些杂志？ what time is it? 现在几点钟？ 
② (in exclamations) 多么 duōme what a nice dress! 多么漂亮的连衣裙啊！ what awful weather! 天气太糟糕了！ what a strange thing 咄咄怪事 what use is that? 那有什么用？ figurative 那有什么意义？ nà yǒu shénme yìyì ？ 
③ (whatever) 无论多少 wúlùn duōshǎo what money he earns he spends 他挣多少花多少 what few friends she had 她仅有的几个朋友 
C.  adverb 
① (how much) 多少 duōshao what does it matter? 那有什么关系？ 
② colloquial (in guesses) [表示估计]it'll cost, what, £50 这要花大约50英镑 
③ BrE dated (as question tag) a good dinner, what? 很丰盛的一顿饭，是吧？ 
D.  what about phrase 
① (in question) …怎么样 … zěnmeyàng what about the children? 孩子们怎么样？ what about a meal out? 出去吃顿饭好吗？ 
② (in reply) [请求澄清问题]what about your sister? — what about her? 那么你姐姐呢？──什么意思？ 
E.  what for phrase colloquial 为什么 wèishénme what did you do that for? 你为什么做那事？ I need to see a doctor — what for? 我得去看医生──你怎么了？ what's that for? 那是干什么用的？ to give [somebody] what for BrE dated colloquial (punish) 严惩某人 (criticize) 训斥某人 
F.  what if phrase 
① (what will happen?) 要是…会怎么样 yàoshi… huì zěnmeyàng what if the train is late? 火车要是晚点会怎么样呢？ 
② (does it matter?) 即使…又有什么关系 jíshǐ… yòu yǒu shénme guānxì what if our house is a mess? 我们的房子一片狼藉又有什么关系呢？ 
③ (how about) …怎么样 … zěnmeyàng what if I bring the dessert? 我拿甜点来好吗？ 
G.  what of phrase 
① (in question) …怎么样 … zěnmeyàng what of Shakespeare? 那么莎士比亚呢？ 
② colloquial (in retort) …有什么关系 … yǒu shénme guānxì what of it! 那又怎么样！ 
H.  what with phrase 因为 yīnwèi what with one thing and another 由于这样那样的事情 what with the recession and unemployment 由于经济衰退和失业 
I.  exclamation 什么 shénme what! I don't believe it! 竟然有这种事！我不相信！

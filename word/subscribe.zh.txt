subscribe 
BrE səbˈskrʌɪb, AmE səbˈskraɪb 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (pay to receive or view sth.) 订购 dìnggòu to subscribe to; 订阅 ‹magazine› 预付费收看 yùfùfèi shōukàn ‹TV, channel›
② (support) 定期捐助 dìngqī juānzhù to subscribe to; 定期向…捐助 ‹fund, charity›
③ Finance (apply) 认购 rèngòu to subscribe to or for [something]; 认购 rèngòu ‹issue, shares›
④ (agree with) to subscribe to an idea/theory; 赞同一个观点/认同一个理论 zàntóng yī gè guāndiǎn/rèntóng yī gè lǐlùn 
B.  transitive verb 
① (contribute) 定期捐助 dìngqī juānzhù ‹sum›
② usually passive (apply for) 报名参加 bàomíng cānjiā ‹course, tour›; 认购 rèngòu ‹issue, shares›the class was fully subscribed 这个班名额已满

combination 
BrE kɒmbɪˈneɪʃ(ə)n, AmE ˌkɑmbəˈneɪʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Countable(two or more things together, mixture) 混合体 hùnhétǐ the weather was a combination of bitter cold and sun 那天天气既有阳光又很寒冷 for a combination of reasons 由于种种原因 
② Uncountable(linking, mixing) 混合 hùnhé in combination with … 与…结合地 the producer, in combination with the designer, has achieved a miracle 生产商与设计师联手创造了奇迹 
③ Countable(sequence) 组合 zǔhé the canvases may be arranged in any number of combinations 这些船帆可以进行多种组合 
④ CountableSport 组合动作 zǔhé dòngzuò a good uppercut/hook combination 漂亮的上钩拳/勾拳连击 
⑤ Countable(for lock) 密码组合 mìmǎ zǔhé 
⑥ Countable(motorcycle and sidecar) 跨斗摩托车 kuàdǒu mótuōchē

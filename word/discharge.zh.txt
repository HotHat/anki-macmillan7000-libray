discharge 
A.  /BrE dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒ, AmE dɪsˈtʃɑrdʒ
transitive verb 
① (release) 允许…出院 yǔnxǔ… chūyuàn ‹patient›; 允许…退伍 yǔnxǔ… tuìwǔ ‹serviceman, soldier›
② (dismiss) 解雇 jiěgù ‹employee›to discharge [somebody] from their duties 撤某人的职 
③ Law 释放 shìfàng ‹prisoner, the accused›; 解散 jiěsàn ‹jury, juror›
④ (emit) 排放 páifàng ‹smoke, water, waste›
⑤ Medicine 流出 liúchū ‹blood, pus, mucus›
⑥ Finance 清偿 qīngcháng ‹debt›
⑦ (perform) 履行 lǚxíng ‹responsibility, obligation, duty›
⑧ (unload) 卸下 xièxia ‹cargo, load›; 让…下来 ràng… xiàlai ‹passengers›
⑨ (fire) 击发 jīfā ‹gun, firearms›; 使用 shǐyòng ‹weapon›; 发射 fāshè ‹arrow›
B.  /BrE dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒ, AmE dɪsˈtʃɑrdʒ
intransitive verb 
① (flow) 流出 liúchū the river discharges into the sea five miles down the coast 那条河在海岸以南5英里处流入大海 
② Medicine «wound» 流脓 liúnóng 
③ Electricity 放电 fàngdiàn 
C.  /BrE ˈdɪstʃɑːdʒ, dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒ, AmE ˈdɪsˌtʃɑrdʒ
noun 
① Uncountable(release) (from hospital) 出院 chūyuàn (from employment) 解雇 jiěgù (from military service) 退伍 tuìwǔ to get one's discharge 被解雇 
② Countable and uncountable(of liquid, gas) 排放 páifàng 
③ Countable(substance released) 排放物 páifàngwù 
④ Countable and uncountableMedicine 分泌物 fēnmìwù bodily discharges 身体分泌物 
⑤ UncountableFinance 清偿 qīngcháng in discharge of [something] 作为对某项债务的清偿 
⑥ Uncountable(performance) 履行 lǚxíng the discharge of his obligations 他的义务的履行 
⑦ Uncountable(firing) 发射 fāshè 
⑧ UncountableElectricity (electricity from charged object) 放电 fàngdiàn (flow of electricity) 电流 diànliú

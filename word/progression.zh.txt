progression 
BrE prəˈɡrɛʃ(ə)n, AmE prəˈɡrɛʃən 
noun 
① Uncountable and countable(development) 发展 fāzhǎn (improvement) 进步 jìnbù good opportunities for career progression 事业发展的好机遇 a natural/logical progression 自然/必然的过渡 
② Uncountable and countable(forward movement) 前进 qiánjìn a steady progression towards your goals 朝着目标的稳步前进 
③ Countable(series) 系列 xìliè an endless progression of meetings 没完没了的会议 a long progression of sunny days 持续很久的晴天 
④ Uncountable and countableMathematics 级数 jíshù arithmetic/geometric progression 算术/几何级数

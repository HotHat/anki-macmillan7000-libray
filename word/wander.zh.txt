wander 
BrE ˈwɒndə, AmE ˈwɑndər 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (stroll aimlessly) 游荡 yóudàng to wander around town/in the park 在城里/公园里闲逛 they wandered in and out of the shops 他们在商店里逛进逛出 to wander through; 漫步穿过 ‹forest, corridor›
② (move nonchalantly) 漫不经心地走 màn bù jīngxīn de zǒu he wandered into work two hours late 他磨磨蹭蹭地晚了两个小时来干活 
③ (move away) 走散 zǒusàn 
④ (be distracted) «thoughts, attention» 走神 zǒushén 
⑤ (be confused through age, illness) «person» 神志恍惚 shénzhì huǎnghū ; «mind» 混乱 hùnluàn the old man is wandering again 那位老人又在东拉西扯了 
⑥ (digress) «speaker, speech» 离题 lítí to wander from the topic; 偏离话题 
⑦ (look away) 游离不定 yóulí bùdìng her gaze wandered along the row of books 她的目光扫过那一排书 his eyes wandered towards the photograph on the desk 他的目光慢慢地移向书桌上的照片 
⑧ (touch sb. inappropriately) «hands» 乱摸 luàn mō 
⑨ (meander) 蜿蜒 wānyán the road wanders through the hills 公路蜿蜒穿过丘陵 
B.  transitive verb 在…游荡 zài… yóudàng ‹streets, hills›to wander the world 周游世界 
C.  noun 闲逛 xiánguàng to go for or have or take a wander colloquial 去逛一圈 I like going for a wander around the shops 我喜欢逛商店 PHRASAL VERBS wander around, wander about

patent 
BrE ˈpat(ə)nt, ˈpeɪt(ə)nt, AmE ˈpætnt 

A.  adjective 
① (obvious) 明显的 míngxiǎn de a patent lie 赤裸裸的谎言 her honesty was patent to everyone 她的诚实是有目共睹的 
② (patented) 受专利保护的 shòu zhuānlì bǎohù de 
③ (proprietary) 专利生产经销的 zhuānlì shēngchǎn jīngxiāo de patent drugs 专利药物 
B.  noun 
① Uncountable and countable(authority) 专利权 zhuānlìquán to take out/hold a patent on [something] 取得/拥有某物的专利 the device was protected by patent 该设备受专利保护 
② Countable(document) 专利证书 zhuānlì zhèngshū 
③ Countable(patented invention) 专利发明 zhuānlì fāmíng 
C.  transitive verb 获得…的专利权 huòdé… de zhuānlìquán

silver 
▶noun
1 freshly polished silver: silverware, (silver) plate; cutlery, 'knives, forks, and spoons'.
2 a handful of silver: coins, coinage, specie; (small) change, loose change.
3 she won three silvers: silver medal, second prize.
▶adjective
1 silver hair: gray, grayish, white.
2 the silver water: silvery, shining, lustrous, gleaming; literary argent.

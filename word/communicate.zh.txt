communicate 
BrE kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt, AmE kəˈmjunəˌkeɪt 

A.  transitive verb 
① (convey) «person» 传达 chuándá ‹news, order›; «work of art, gesture, expression» 表达 biǎodá ‹idea, emotion›
② Medicine (transmit) 传染 chuánrǎn ‹disease, virus›
B.  reflexive verb to communicate itself; «feeling» 显露 xiǎnlù her anxiety communicates itself to others 她表现得焦虑不安 
C.  intransitive verb 
① (share information) 交流 jiāoliú to communicate with [somebody]; 与某人交流 to communicate by phone/radio 用电话/无线电交流 to communicate through dance/by gestures 通过舞蹈/手势交流 
② (express oneself) 表达想法 biǎodá xiǎngfǎ to communicate well/badly 表达力强/差 
③ (connect) 相连 xiānglián to communicate with [something]; 与…相通 ‹room, apartment›

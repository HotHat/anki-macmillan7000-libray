anywhere 
BrE ˈɛnɪwɛː, AmE ˈɛniˌ(h)wɛr 
A. adverb 
① (in any place) 在任何地方 zài rènhé dìfang (to any place) 去任何地方 qù rènhé dìfang we didn't go anywhere special/interesting 我们没去什么特别/有趣的地方 I'll go anywhere where there's sun 只要有阳光的地方我都想去 have you seen Andrew anywhere? 你在什么地方见过安德鲁吗？ I didn't come anywhere (in contests) 我没有取得名次 we're going to Spain, if anywhere 我们要去就去西班牙 she's not anywhere near ready/as clever 她远没有准备好/那么聪明 
② (within a range) 介于…之间 jièyú… zhījiān anywhere between 50 and 100 people 介于50到100人之间 
B.  pronoun 任何地方 rènhé dìfang I don't have anywhere to stay 我没有地方可待

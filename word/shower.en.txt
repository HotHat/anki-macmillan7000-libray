shower 
▶noun
1 a shower of rain: (light) fall, drizzle, sprinkling, misting.
2 a shower of arrows: volley, hail, salvo, bombardment, barrage, fusillade, cannonade.
3 a shower of awards: avalanche, deluge, flood, spate, flurry; profusion, abundance.
▶verb
1 confetti showered down on us: rain, fall, hail.
2 she showered them with gifts: deluge, flood, inundate, swamp, engulf; overwhelm, overload, snow under.
3 showering praise on his cronies: lavish, heap, bestow freely.

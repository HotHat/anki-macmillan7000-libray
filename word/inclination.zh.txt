② Countable(disposition) 性格 xìnggé to be lazy by inclination 生性懒惰 
③ Countable and uncountable(degree of slope) 坡度 pōdù an inclination of 45° 45度的坡度 
④ Countable(bow) 弯腰 wānyāo (nod) 点头 diǎntóu an inclination of one's head 点头 diǎntóu

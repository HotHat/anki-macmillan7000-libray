helpless 
BrE ˈhɛlpləs, AmE ˈhɛlpləs 
adjective 
① (powerless) 无助的 wúzhù de to feel helpless 感到无助 she was helpless in this matter 这件事她帮不上忙 I was helpless to prevent his leaving 我没能阻止他离开 
② (incapacitated) 抑制不住的 yìzhì bùzhù de they were helpless with laughter colloquial 他们情不自禁地大笑起来 
③ (incapable, dependent) 不能自立的 bù néng zìlì de a helpless invalid 生活不能自理的病人 as helpless as a child/baby/new-born babe 像小孩/婴儿/新生儿一样无助 
④ (defenceless) 不能自卫的 bù néng zìwèi de the helpless victims of war 手无寸铁的战争受害者

bare 
BrE bɛː, AmE bɛr 

A.  adjective 
① (naked) 裸露的 luǒlù de ‹body, blade›to walk with bare feet 赤脚走路 to sit in the sun with one's head bare 不戴帽坐在太阳底下 bare to the waist 赤膊的 with one's bare hands 赤手空拳地 
② (exposed) 无遮盖的 wúzhēgài de to strip [something] down to the bare bones 去除某事物的枝节 bare walls 没有装饰的墙壁 he laid bare their evil plan 他揭露了他们的恶毒计划 to lay one's soul or heart bare 袒露心扉 
③ (empty) 空的 kōng de ‹cupboard, room›the burglars stripped the house bare 窃贼将房子洗劫一空 
④ (stark) 光秃秃的 guāngtūtū de ‹mountain, tree›a barren landscape which was bare of almost every sign of life 几乎毫无生命迹象的不毛之地 
⑤ (mere) 仅仅的 jǐnjǐn de a bare 3%/20 dollars 仅仅3％/区区20美元 to last a bare 30 seconds 持续了只不过30秒钟 
⑥ (just sufficient) 刚够的 gāng gòu de the bare minimum 最低限度 the bare essentials or necessities 最低限度的必需品 
⑦ (unembellished) 不加掩饰的 bù jiā yǎnshì de ‹facts›

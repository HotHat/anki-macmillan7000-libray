lodge 
BrE lɒdʒ, AmE lɑdʒ 

A.  noun 
① (for gatekeeper, caretaker) 门房 ménfáng entrance lodge 门房 ménfáng 
② (for country sports) [供参加户外运动者暂住的] 乡间小屋 xiāngjiān xiǎowū 
③ (in Freemasonry) (building) 地方分会集会处 dìfāng fēnhuì jíhuìchù (group) 地方分会 dìfāng fēnhuì 
④ (of beaver) 穴 xué 
B.  transitive verb 
① (accommodate) 为…提供住宿 wèi… tígōng zhùsù ‹guests, friends›
② (file) 正式提出 zhèngshì tíchū ‹appeal, protest›to lodge a complaint 正式提出投诉 
③ (deposit) 存放 cúnfàng to lodge the money with the court 把钱交法庭保管 
C.  intransitive verb 
① (reside) 寄宿 jìsù to lodge with [somebody]; 在某人家寄宿 
② (stick) 嵌入 qiànrù the bullet has lodged in his shoulder 子弹射入了他的肩膀 a bone lodged in his throat 一根骨头卡在他的喉咙里 it lodged in her memory figurative 这事留存在她的记忆中

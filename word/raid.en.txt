raid 
▶noun
1 the raid on Dieppe: attack, assault, descent, blitz, incursion, sortie; onslaught, storming, charge, offensive, invasion, blitzkrieg.
2 a police raid: search; informal bust, takedown, shakedown.
▶verb
1 they raided shipping in the harbor: attack, assault, set upon, descend on, swoop on, blitz, assail, storm, rush.
2 armed men raided the store: rob, hold up, break into; plunder, steal from, pillage, loot, ransack, sack; informal stick up, heist.

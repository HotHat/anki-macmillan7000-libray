recommendation 
BrE ˌrɛkəmɛnˈdeɪʃ(ə)n, AmE ˌrɛkəmənˈdeɪʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Uncountable(commendation, suggestion) 推荐 tuījiàn on the recommendation of … 经…的推荐 to speak in recommendation of [somebody]/[something] 口头推荐某人/某事物 
② Countable(official suggestion) 建议 jiànyì to make a recommendation 提出建议 he was sentenced to life imprisonment with a recommendation that he serve at least 30 years 他被判无期徒刑，并建议至少服刑30年 
③ Countable(letter, statement) 推荐信 tuījiànxìn to write [somebody] a recommendation 给某人写推荐信 
④ Countable(good quality, advantage) 可取之处 kě qǔ zhī chù

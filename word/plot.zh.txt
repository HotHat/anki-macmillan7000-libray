plot 
BrE plɒt, AmE plɑt 

A.  noun 
① (piece of ground) 小块土地 xiǎokuài tǔdì a plot of land 一块地 a vegetable plot 一块菜地 a cemetery plot 一块墓地 
② (conspiracy) 密谋 mìmóu an assassination plot 暗杀阴谋 
③ (narrative of novel, film, play) 情节 qíngjié I couldn't follow the plot 我搞不明白故事情节 the plot thickens 情况变得复杂起来 
B.  transitive verb(present participle etc. -tt-) 
① (secretly plan) 密谋 mìmóu to plot to do [something]; 暗中策划做某事 
② (make plan of) 设计…的方案 shèjì… de fāng'àn ; (map) 绘制…的平面图 huìzhì… de píngmiàntú they have plotted a way forward for company expansion 他们为公司设计好了一条扩张之路 
③ (chart) 在图上标绘出 zài tú shang biāo huì chū ‹course, position, progress›
④ (on graph) 绘制 huìzhì ‹curve›; 标定 biāodìng ‹point, figure›
C.  intransitive verb (present participle etc. -tt-) 密谋 mìmóu to plot against the government 密谋反政府

affect 
BrE əˈfɛkt, AmE əˈfɛkt 
transitive verb 
① (influence) 影响 yǐngxiǎng ‹decision, event, result, person, career›
② (emotionally disturb) 打动 dǎdòng the pictures of the disaster affected her badly 有关那场灾难的图片令她唏嘘不已 
③ Medicine (afflict) «medical condition» 侵袭 qīnxí ; «habit» 损害 sǔnhài a high percentage of people are affected by cancer 大部分人患有癌症 the disease often affects people's sight 这种病常会损害人的视力 
④ formal (feign) 假装 jiǎzhuāng ‹ignorance, astonishment, mannerism›to affect to do [something]; 假装做某事 
⑤ formal (like to wear) 喜欢穿 xǐhuan chuān ‹clothing, style of dress›

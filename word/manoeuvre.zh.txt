manoeuvre 
BrE məˈnuːvə, AmE məˈnuvər
BrE 
A.  transitive verb 
① (move) 熟练谨慎地移动 shúliàn jǐnshèn de yídòng she manoeuvred the car into the garage 她小心翼翼地把车开进车库 
② (manipulate) 操纵 cāozòng to manoeuvre the conversation round to the subject of money 巧妙地把谈话引到金钱问题上 she manoeuvred her way to the top of the company 她施展手腕进入了公司的高层 
B.  intransitive verb 
① (move skilfully) 熟练地移动 shúliàn de yídòng ; (move carefully) 谨慎地移动 jǐnshèn de yídòng 
② (plot, scheme) 用策略 yòng cèlüè to manoeuvre for power 施展策略谋取权力 the new laws have left us little room to manoeuvre 新法律没给我们留下多少回旋的余地 
C.  noun 
① Countable(movement) 机动动作 jīdòng dòngzuò a skilful manoeuvre 熟练的移动 
② Countable(plan, scheme) 策略 cèlüè 
③ Uncountableroom for manoeuvre 回旋余地 huíxuán yúdì 
D.  manoeuvres noun plural Military 演习 yǎnxí to be on manoeuvres 进行演习

oblige 
BrE əˈblʌɪdʒ, AmE əˈblaɪdʒ 

A.  transitive verb 
① (compel) 强迫 qiǎngpò to be obliged to do [something]; 被迫做某事 to feel obliged to do [something]; 觉得有义务做某事 you are not obliged/they cannot oblige you to answer 你不一定要回答/他们不能强迫你回答 
② (be helpful to) 帮助 bāngzhù she did it to oblige them 她做这事来帮助他们 
B.  intransitive verb 有帮助 yǒu bāngzhù anything to oblige! 乐于效劳！

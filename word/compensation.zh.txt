compensation 
BrE kɒmpɛnˈseɪʃ(ə)n, kɒmp(ə)nˈseɪʃ(ə)n, AmE ˌkɑmpənˈseɪʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Uncountable(recompense) 补偿 bǔcháng in or as or by way of compensation (for [something]); 作为（对某事物的）补偿 to award/claim/pay/receive/seek compensation 给予/索取/支付/得到/寻求补偿 he was awarded £3,000 compensation for his injuries 他得到了3,000英镑的受伤赔偿 a compensation claim 索赔 
② Uncountable and countable(counterbalance) 弥补 míbǔ the rise in salary was no compensation for the long hours 加薪弥补不了长时间工作 getting older has its compensations 上年纪有上年纪的好处

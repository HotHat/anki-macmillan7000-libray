warrant 
BrE ˈwɒr(ə)nt, AmE ˈwɔrənt 

A.  transitive verb 
① (justify) «fact, situation» 证明…正当 zhèngmíng… zhèngdàng ‹behaviour, measure›
② Busn «company» 为…作担保 wèi… zuò dānbǎo ‹product, accuracy›
③ dated (assure) 打保票 dǎ bǎopiào he'll be back, I'll warrant you 他会回来的，我向你保证 
B.  noun 
① CountableLaw 授权令 shòuquánlìng an arrest/a search warrant 逮捕/搜查令 
② Uncountable(justification) 正当理由 zhèngdàng lǐyóu to be no/sufficient warrant for [something]/for doing [something]/to do [something]; 某事/做某事没有依据/有足够依据 
③ Countable(voucher) 许可证 xǔkězhèng 
④ CountableFinance 认股权证 rèngǔ quánzhèng 
⑤ Uncountableformal (legal authority) 授权 shòuquán 
⑥ CountableMilitary 准尉委任状 zhǔnwèi wěirènzhuàng

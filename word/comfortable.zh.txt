comfortable 
BrE ˈkʌmf(ə)təb(ə)l, AmE ˈkəmfərdəb(ə)l, ˈkəmftərbəl 
adjective 
① (promoting well-being) 舒适的 shūshì de ‹house, clothes, temperature›the patient had a comfortable night 病人晚上未受病痛折磨 
② (relaxed) 舒服的 shūfu de to make oneself comfortable (in chair) 坐得舒服 (at ease) 不拘束 
③ (reassuring) 给人以安慰的 gěi rén yǐ ānwèi de ‹idea, belief›; 容易相处的 róngyì xiāngchǔ de ‹person›; 轻松愉快的 qīngsōng yúkuài de ‹job, lifestyle, routine›
④ (considerable) 相当大的 xiāngdāng dà de ‹majority, victory, margin›their lead in the polls is still comfortable 他们在民意调查中仍处于明显的领先地位 a comfortable winner 以较大优势获胜的选手 
⑤ (well-off) 宽裕的 kuānyù de she's not rich, but she's quite comfortable 她不算富有，但还是相当宽裕 
⑥ (happy) 内心舒坦的 nèixīn shūtan de I don't feel comfortable doing that 我觉得做那事心里不踏实 I would feel more comfortable about leaving if … 如果…，我离开会觉得更心安理得 
⑦ (confident) 自信的 zìxìn de he is more comfortable with computers than with people 比起与人相处，他和电脑打交道时更能应付自如

acknowledge 
BrE əkˈnɒlɪdʒ, AmE əkˈnɑlədʒ 
transitive verb 
① (admit) 承认 chéngrèn ‹mistake, fact, debt›I acknowledge myself to be in error 我承认自己错了 to acknowledge doing [something]; 承认做过某事 
② (accept legitimacy of, recognize) 认可 rènkě to acknowledge an illegitimate child 承认私生子 to be acknowledged to be an excellent lawyer/doctor 被公认为是优秀的律师/医生 she acknowledged him as her heir 她认他为继承人 
③ (express thanks for) 感谢 gǎnxiè to acknowledge one's sources (in book) 注明引文出处并致谢 
④ (confirm receipt of) 告知收悉 gàozhī shōuxī we will of course acknowledge all letters 来信必复 
⑤ (show recognition of) 向…致意 xiàng… zhìyì he acknowledged them with a wave 他挥手向他们致意

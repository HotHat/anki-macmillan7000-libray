shower 
BrE ˈʃaʊə, AmE ˈʃaʊ(ə)r 

A.  noun 
① Meteorology 阵雨 zhènyǔ a heavy/light shower 一阵大雨/小雨 to get caught in a shower 遇上阵雨 a snow shower 雨夹雪 
② (of leaves, confetti, insults) 一阵 yī zhèn (of gifts) 一大批 yī dà pī we were suddenly covered in a shower of petals 突然花瓣雨点般落在我们身上 a shower of sparks flew out from the fire 炉火中迸发出一串火星 
③ (apparatus) 淋浴器 línyùqì (unit) 淋浴间 línyùjiān I won't be long, I'm in the shower 我用不了多久，我在淋浴 an electric shower 电淋浴器 
④ (wash) 淋浴 línyù to have or take a shower 冲淋浴 
⑤ BrE colloquial pejorative (group) 乌合之众 wū hé zhī zhòng they're just a shower of druggies 他们只是一伙瘾君子 what a shower! 真是一帮废物！ 
⑥ AmE (party) [为即将结婚或分娩的妇女举办的] 送礼聚会 sònglǐ jùhuì to give a shower for [somebody]; 为某人举办送礼会 a baby shower 新生儿送礼会 
B.  transitive verb 
① (spray) 使纷纷降落 shǐ fēnfēn jiàngluò to shower [something] on or over [somebody]; 把某物抛撒在某人身上 the tree was showering petals everywhere 这棵树掉落了一地的花瓣 the fire showered us with sparks 火星四溅，落到我们身上 
② figurative (lavish) 大量给予 dàliàng jǐyǔ to shower [somebody] with [something]; 慷慨给予某人 ‹gifts, praise, affection›to shower [something] on [somebody]; 送给某人许多某物 
C.  intransitive verb 
① (have a shower) 洗淋浴 xǐ línyù 
② (fall in a shower) «leaves, sparks, stones» 纷纷落下 fēnfēn luòxià to shower on or down on …; 雨点般地落到…上

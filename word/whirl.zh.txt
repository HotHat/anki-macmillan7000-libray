whirl 
BrE wəːl, AmE (h)wərl 

A.  noun 
① (spinning or swirling movement) 旋转 xuánzhuǎn 
② (spiral motif) 旋涡式图案 xuánwōshì tú'àn 
③ figurative (frantic activity) 繁忙 fánmáng the social whirl 纷繁的社交活动 to be all in a whirl about [something] 为某事都忙得晕头转向 her head is in a whirl 她的脑子乱成一团 to give [something] a whirl colloquial 试一试某事 
B.  intransitive verb 
① (spin, swirl) «person, leaves, water, propeller» 旋转 xuánzhuǎn my mind or head is or my thoughts are whirling 我的脑子里乱糟糟的 
② (rush) to whirl past/along «vehicle» 急驶过/向前疾驰 «person» 飞跑过/向前飞跑 fēipǎoguo/xiàng qián fēipǎo 
C.  transitive verb 
① (spin, swirl) «wind, water» 使…旋转 shǐ… xuánzhuǎn ‹leaves, sand›

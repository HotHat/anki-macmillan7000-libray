transmit 
BrE tranzˈmɪt, trɑːnz-, -ns-, AmE trænzˈmɪt, trænsˈmɪt
(present participle etc. -tt-) 
A.  transitive verb 
① (transfer) 传播 chuánbō to transmit a report/message 传送报告/消息 to transmit [something] to [somebody]; 把某物传给某人 to transmit disease 传播疾病 to transmit a skill 传授技能 to transmit light/an image 传播光/传送图像 
② (broadcast) 播送 bōsòng ‹news, signal›to transmit [something] live 现场直播某事件 the programme will be transmitted at a later date 节目将在日后播放 
③ Physics 传 chuán ‹heat, sound›; 传递 chuándì ‹vibration›; 输送 shūsòng ‹electricity›
B.  intransitive verb 广播 guǎngbō

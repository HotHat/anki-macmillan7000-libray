fuse 
BrE fjuːz, AmE fjuz 

A.  noun 
① Electricity 保险丝 bǎoxiǎnsī to blow a fuse; literal 熔断保险丝 figurative colloquial 勃然大怒 bórán dà nù 
② (for explosive device) 导火线 dǎohuǒxiàn to light a fuse; 点燃导火索 to arm or set a fuse; 安导火索 to be on a short fuse figurative 动辄发怒 
③ (detonator) 引信 yǐnxìn 
B.  transitive verb 
① (join) «person, machine, heat» 使…熔接 shǐ… róngjiē ‹metal, wires, ends›to fuse [something] with/to [something]; 把某物同某物熔接在一起 
② (unite, blend) 使…混合 shǐ… hùnhé 
③ Electricity (provide with fuse) 给…接上保险丝 gěi… jiēshang bǎoxiǎnsī 
④ BrE Electricity (cause to stop functioning) 把…的保险丝熔断 bǎ… de bǎoxiǎnsī róngduàn 
C.  intransitive verb 
① Chemistry, Technology «metals, chemicals» 熔接 róngjiē 
② (merge) «companies, parties» 合并 hébìng 
③ (blend) «images, flavours» 结合 jiéhé to fuse together; 结合在一起 
④ BrE Electricity (stop working) «lights, television, plug» 保险丝熔断 bǎoxiǎnsī róngduàn 
⑤ Physics 核聚变 héjùbiàn

pace 
BrE peɪs, AmE peɪs 

A.  noun 
① (step) 一步 yī bù to take a pace forward/back 向前迈/后退一步 
② (distance) 步幅 bù fú twenty paces (away) from … 离…二十步远 at (a distance of) thirty paces 在（相距）三十步远处 
③ (speed) 速度 sùdù at a fast pace 快速地 at a brisk or cracking pace 迅速地 at a slow/steady pace 以缓慢/平稳的速度 to keep pace with [somebody]/[something] 与某人/某物并驾齐驱 to set the pace (fix the speed) 确定速度 (establish the standard) 确立标准 (be the leader) 领先 
④ (rate) 节奏 jiézòu at a steady pace 以平稳的步速 at one's own pace 按自己的节奏 the leisurely pace of life in the country 乡间悠闲的生活节奏 
B.  paces noun plural (routine) 步态 bùtài to put a horse through its paces 让马展示步态 to put [somebody] through their paces 考查某人的能力 to show one's paces «horse» 展示步态 «person» 展示才能 zhǎnshì cáinéng 
C.  intransitive verb 踱步 duó bù to pace up and down 走来走去 
D.  transitive verb 
① (walk up and down) 在…上走来走去 zài… shang zǒulái zǒuqù ‹floor›; 在…里走来走去 zài… li zǒulái zǒuqù ‹corridor, room›I spent hours pacing the carpet in the waiting room 我在等候室的地毯上来回走了好几个小时

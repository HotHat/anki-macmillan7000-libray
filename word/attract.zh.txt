attract 
BrE əˈtrakt, AmE əˈtrækt 
transitive verb 
① (draw, appeal) 吸引 xīyǐn to be attracted to [somebody]/[something]; 受到某人/某事物的吸引 a magnet will not attract aluminium 磁石不吸铝 
② (be cause of attraction) 引起…的好感 yǐnqǐ… de hǎogǎn his sense of humour attracted me to him 他的幽默感使我对他产生好感 
③ (evoke) 引起 yǐnqǐ to attract attention/criticism 引起注意/招致批评

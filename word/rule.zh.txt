rule 
BrE ruːl, AmE rul 

A.  noun 
① Countable(regulation) (of game, sport, system) 规则 guīzé (of religion) 教规 jiàoguī (of school, company, organization) 规章 guīzhāng rules and regulations 规章制度 school/EU rules 校规/欧盟法规 to break the rules 违规 to obey/follow/observe the rules 服从/遵循/遵守规则 to be against the rules (to do [something]) 违反规定（做某事） under this rule 按照这条规定 it is a rule that … …是规定 the rules of the game literal 游戏规则 figurative 行为准则 xíngwéi zhǔnzé to play by the rules literal 按规则玩游戏 figurative 处事公正诚实 chǔshì gōngzhèng chéngshí to play by one's own/[somebody's] rules 按自己的原则/某人的规矩行事 to bend or stretch the rules 通融 
② Countable(piece of advice) 建议 jiànyì hard and fast rules 一定之规 there are two vital rules to remember 有两条极其重要的原则要记住 
③ Countable(usual occurrence) 常规 chángguī I make it a rule always/never to do … 我的习惯是一向/从不做… hot summers are the rule here 这里的夏天通常很炎热 as a rule 通常 as a general rule 一般说来 
④ Uncountable(authority) 统治 tǒngzhì colonial/civilian/military rule 殖民/文官/军人统治 majority/minority rule 多数派政党/少数派政党执政 under the rule of a tyrant 在暴君的统治下 
⑤ Countable(for measuring) 直尺 zhíchǐ a metre rule 米尺

① (govern) «monarch, dictator» 统治 tǒngzhì ‹country›to rule with a rod of iron or with an iron hand 实行铁腕统治

③ (influence) «money, appetite» 支配 zhīpèi ‹person, life, character›; «person, consideration» 控制 kòngzhì ‹behaviour›; «factor» 决定 juédìng ‹strategy›to be ruled by; 受…支配 ‹emotion, greed, need›to let one's heart rule one's head 感情用事 
④ (decide) 裁定 cáidìng to rule [somebody]/[something] (to be) [something]; «judge, court» 裁定某人/某事为某情况 to rule [something] unlawful or illegal 判定某事非法 to rule that …; 裁定…

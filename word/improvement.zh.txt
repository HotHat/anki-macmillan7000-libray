improvement 
BrE ɪmˈpruːvm(ə)nt, AmE ɪmˈpruvmənt 
nounCountable and uncountable
① (in quality) 改善 gǎishàn an improvement in East-West relations 东西方关系的改善 she has shown some improvement in maths 她的数学成绩有所提高 there has been no further improvement in her condition 她的病情没有进一步好转 there's room for improvement in your work 你的工作仍有改进的余地 
② (in quantity) (increase) 增加 zēngjiā (decrease) 减少 jiǎnshǎo a significant improvement in the sales figures 销售数字的大幅增长 there has been an improvement in the infant mortality rate 婴儿死亡率降低了 
③ (better condition) 改进之处 gǎijìn zhī chù (sth. better) 改善的事物 gǎishàn de shìwù what do you think of my new hairstyle? — it's an improvement 你觉得我的新发型怎么样？──比原来的好 the new edition is a considerable improvement on the old one 新版本较旧版本有了很大的改进 
④ (to building, road) 整修 zhěngxiū to make/carry out improvements to the house 对房屋进行整修 a road improvement scheme 道路修缮计划

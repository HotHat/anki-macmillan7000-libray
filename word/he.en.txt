he 
BrE hiː, AmE hi 
A. pronoun 
① (referring to man, boy) 他 tā 
② (referring to male animal) 它 tā 
③ dated (referring to person without specifying sex) 他 tā [现代英语中这一表达方式常以he/she或they代替]; (referring to animal) 它 tā everyone may do what he likes 每个人都可以做自己想做的事 
④ dated (referring to any person) 他 tā [指任何人]he who hesitates is lost proverb 当断不断，必受其患 
⑤ He (referring to God) 他 tā [指上帝]
B.  noun 
① (man) 男子 nánzǐ ; (boy) 男孩 nánhái is the baby a he or a she? 婴儿是男的还是女的？ 
② (male animal) 雄性动物 xióngxìng dòngwù the dog's definitely a he 这条狗绝对是公狗

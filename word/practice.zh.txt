practice 
BrE ˈpraktɪs, AmE ˈpræktəs 

A.  noun 
① Uncountable(action, operation) 实践 shíjiàn to put [something] into practice 把某事付诸实践 in practice 实际上 the principles and practice of teaching 教学理论与实践 
② Uncountable(repetition, exercises) 练习 liànxí to be/keep in practice 在/保持练习 to be or get out of practice 疏于练习 two hours' practice a day 每天练习两小时 practice makes perfect proverb 熟能生巧 
③ Uncountable(procedure) 常规 chángguī to be good/common/accepted/standard practice to do [something] 是做某事的优良/一般/公认/标准做法 
④ Countable(habit, custom) 习惯 xíguàn the practice of doing [something] 做某事的习惯 as is my (usual) practice, I got up at
6 a.m. 我一如既往地在早上6点钟起床了 
⑤ Uncountable and countable(of doctor, lawyer) 工作 gōngzuò to set up in or go into practice (as a doctor/lawyer) 开始执业（行医/做律师） to work in general/private practice 从事普通诊疗/开设私人诊所行医 she has a profitable medical/legal practice 她的诊所/律师事务所很赚钱 a large/efficiently-run practice 规模很大的/高效运营的执业机构 
B.  adjectivebefore noun 训练的 xùnliàn de ‹exam, game, flight, session›
C.  transitive verb, intransitive verb AmE = practise

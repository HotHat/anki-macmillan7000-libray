half 
BrE hɑːf, AmE hæf 

A.  noun(plural halves) 
① (equal part) 一半 yībàn the top/left half 上/左半部分 to cut/tear/break [something] in half 将某物切/撕/破成两半 to increase/cut by half 增加/削减一半 to go halves (with [somebody]) (on [something]) (share food) （与某人）平分（某物） (share expense) （与某人）平摊（某）费用 a … and a half colloquial (great) 棒极了 (big) 极大的… a meal/job and a half 丰盛可口的一餐/一件费时费力的工作 too clever/friendly etc. by half 过于聪明/友善等 not to do things or anything by halves 做任何事都不半途而废 she doesn't know the half of it colloquial 她不懂得最重要的方面 [somebody's] better or other half colloquial humorous 某人的另一半 ［指配偶］how the other half lives colloquial (richer) 富人如何生活 (poorer) 穷人如何生活 to give [somebody]/[something] half a chance 给某人/某事一点点机会 to have half a mind to do [something] 可能会做某事 
② Mathematics 零点五 líng diǎn wǔ twelve and a half per cent 百分之十二点五 
③ (in age) 半 bàn two and a half 两岁半 
④ Sport (period, area) 半场 bànchǎng the first/second half 上/下半场 
⑤ Sport = half back 
⑥ BrE colloquial (bus fare) 半价票 bànjiàpiào (a) half to the station, please 请给我一张到车站的半价票 
⑦ BrE colloquial (half pint) 半品脱 bàn pǐntuō two halves of bitter 两份半品脱的苦啤酒 
B.  adjective 
① (50%) 一半的 yībàn de half the price/the size 一半的价格/尺寸 half a minute or second or moment colloquial 一会儿 
② (most of) 很大部分 hěn dà bùfen a woman half [somebody's] age 一个小某人很多岁的女人 half the time/fun/trouble 大部分时间/主要乐趣/最大的麻烦 two and a half years old 两岁半 
C.  pronoun 
① (50%) 一半 yībàn out of 30 students, only half passed 在30个学生之中，只有一半人及格 
② (a large number) 很大部分 hěn dà bùfen half of them can't even spell their names properly 他们当中很多人甚至不能正确拼写出自己的名字 
D.  adverb 
① (50%) 有一半 yǒu yībàn to be half full/empty/over 满/空/过了一半 half as much/many 一半那么多 half as big/old 一半大/老的 half as much/big etc. again BrE, half again as much/big etc. AmE 原来数量/大小等的一倍半 to be half Italian 有一半意大利血统 
② (partly) 部分地 bùfen de half awake/asleep 睡眼惺忪/半睡半醒 to be half drowned/drunk 淹得半死/喝得半醉 half-cooked/-digested/-remembered 半熟的/未完全消化的/记忆不全的 to be only half listening 只是漫不经心地听着 
③ (in time expressions) 半小时 bàn xiǎoshí half past four, colloquial half four 4点半 half past colloquial 半点 to run at half past the hour «buses» 每隔半点发车 
E.  not half adverb phrase 
① (not nearly) 远非 yuǎn fēi the hole's not half deep enough 洞远远不够深 
② colloquial (not at all) 一点也不 yīdiǎn yě bù that hat doesn't look half bad 那顶帽子很漂亮 
③ BrE colloquial (extremely) 非常 fēicháng it doesn't half stink! 它奇臭无比！

sustain 
BrE səˈsteɪn, AmE səˈsteɪn 
transitive verb 
① (maintain) 维持 wéichí ‹interest, friendship, life, economy›; 使…持续 shǐ… chíxù ‹sound, interest, campaign›a sustained effort 不断的努力 a sustained note 持续音 he cannot sustain a normal conversation 他无法进行正常的谈话 
② (provide strength) «hope, belief, food» 支撑 zhīchēng ‹person›they were sustained by the knowledge that eventually truth would prevail 他们知道真理必胜，这给了他们以力量 
③ (suffer) 遭受 zāoshòu ‹injury, defeat, damage›to sustain a blow to the head 头上挨一拳 
④ (bear) 承受 chéngshòu ‹weight›
⑤ Law (confirm) 认可 rènkě ‹claim, allegation›(affirm, uphold) 确认 quèrèn ‹decision, verdict›objection sustained! 反对有效！

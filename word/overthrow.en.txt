overthrow 
▶verb
1 the president was overthrown: remove (from office/power), bring down, topple, depose, oust, displace, unseat, dethrone.
2 an attempt to overthrow military rule: put an end to, defeat, conquer.
▶noun
1 the overthrow of the general: removal (from office/power), downfall, fall, toppling, deposition, ousting, displacement, supplanting, unseating.
2 the overthrow of capitalism: ending, defeat, displacement, fall, collapse, downfall, demise.

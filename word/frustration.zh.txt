frustration 
BrE frʌˈstreɪʃn, AmE frəˈstreɪʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Uncountable(irritated feeling) 失望 shīwàng to suffer from frustration 感到失望 frustration at or with [something]; 对某事感到的失望 to vent one's frustration (on [somebody]/[something]); （在某人/某物身上）出气 
② Countable(irritating thing) 令人沮丧的事物 lìng rén jǔsàng de shìwù the frustrations of doing [something]; 做某事的不如意 
③ (of attempt, effort) 沮丧 jǔsàng 
④ Uncountable(in job, role) 不满意 bù mǎnyì 
⑤ Uncountable(of plans, ambition) 受挫 shòucuò the frustration of [somebody's] hopes 某人希望的落空 
⑥ (sexual) 性方面的不满足 xìng fāngmiàn de bù mǎnzú

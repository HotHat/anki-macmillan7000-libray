contrast 
A.  /BrE ˈkɒntrɑːst, AmE ˈkɑnˌtræst
noun 
① Uncountable and countable(difference) 差异 chāyì by or in contrast 相比之下 a contrast with [somebody]/[something]; 与某人/某事物的对照 the contrast between [somebody]/[something] and [somebody]/[something]; …与…之间的差异 
② Countable(unlike thing) 明显不同的事物 míngxiǎn bùtóng de shìwù (unlike person) 明显不同的人 míngxiǎn bùtóng de rén to be a contrast with or to [somebody]/[something]; 与某人/某事物截然不同 
③ Uncountable(opposition) 反面 fǎnmiàn in contrast to [somebody]/[something] 与某人/某事物相反地 
④ UncountableArt, Photography (of colour) 反差 fǎnchā (of light) 明暗对比 míng àn duìbǐ ; Television 对比度 duìbǐdù 
B.  /BrE kənˈtrɑːst, AmE ˈkɑnˌtræst, kənˈtræst
intransitive verb 形成对比 xíngchéng duìbǐ to contrast with [somebody]/[something]; 与某人/某事物形成对比 to contrast in style 在风格上形成对比 to contrast sharply 形成鲜明对照 
C.  /BrE kənˈtrɑːst, AmE ˈkɑnˌtræst, kənˈtræst
transitive verb 对比 duìbǐ to contrast [somebody]/[something] with [somebody]/[something]; 将…与…作对比

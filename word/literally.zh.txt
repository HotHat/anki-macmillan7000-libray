literally 
BrE ˈlɪt(ə)rəli, AmE ˈlɪdərəli, ˈlɪtrəli 
adverb 
① (exactly as written or said) 逐字地 zhúzì de to take [something]/[somebody] literally; 照字面意义理解某事物/某人说的话 
② (without exaggeration) 确确实实地 quèquèshíshí de they quite literally danced all night 他们真的整晚都在跳舞 
③ colloquial (emphatic) 简直 jiǎnzhí he literally exploded with rage 他简直气炸了

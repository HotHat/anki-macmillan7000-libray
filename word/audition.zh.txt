audition 
BrE ɔːˈdɪʃ(ə)n, AmE ɔˈdɪʃ(ə)n 

A.  noun (for acting role) 试演 shìyǎn ; (for singing role) 试唱 shìchàng ; (as musician) 试奏 shìzòu 
B.  intransitive verb to audition for [something]; «actor» 试演某角色 «singer» 试唱某曲目 shìchàng mǒu qǔmù «musician» 试奏某曲目 shìzòu mǒu qǔmù 
C.  transitive verb 对…进行面试 duì… jìnxíng miànshì to be auditioned for [something] 为某事接受面试

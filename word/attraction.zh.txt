attraction 
BrE əˈtrakʃ(ə)n, AmE əˈtrækʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Uncountable(appealing quality) 吸引力 xīyǐnlì an irresistible attraction to [something] 某事物不可抗拒的吸引力 attraction between two people 两人之间的爱慕 
② Countable(favourable feature) 优点 yōudiǎn I can't see the attraction of … 我看不出…有什么好处 
③ Countable(entertainment, sight) 有吸引力的地方 yǒu xīyǐnlì de dìfang a great tourist attraction 旅游胜地 
④ UncountablePhysics 引力 yǐnlì gravitational/magnetic attraction 重力/磁力

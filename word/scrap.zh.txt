scrap 
BrE skrap, AmE skræp 

A.  noun 
① Countable(of paper, cloth) 碎片 suìpiàn (of verse, conversation) 片段 piànduàn (of news, information) 点滴 diǎndī a scrap of paper 一小片纸 scraps of material 碎布料 the occasional scrap of the conversation 谈话的片言只语 scraps of news/information 零星消息/信息 
② Countable(of meat) 下脚料 xiàjiǎoliào (of bread) 面包屑 miànbāoxiè (of cake) 蛋糕渣 dàngāo zhā (of leftover food) 残羹剩饭 cán gēng shèng fàn the scraps are fed to the pigs 剩饭拿去喂猪 
③ Countable(small amount) 少量 shǎoliàng not a scrap 丝毫没有 he never does a scrap of work! 他一点儿活儿都不干！ it doesn't make a scrap of difference! 这毫无差别！ there isn't a scrap of truth in what he says 他说的话没有丝毫事实根据 
④ Uncountable(waste articles or materials for reprocessing) (metal) 废金属 fèi jīnshǔ ; (other materials) 废料 fèiliào there's a thriving trade in scrap 废金属生意很兴旺 to sell [something] for scrap 把某物当废金属出售 the scrap value 废金属价值 
⑤ Countablecolloquial (fight) 打架 dǎjià ; figurative (quarrel) 争吵 zhēngchǎo to get into a scrap with [somebody] 和某人打架 a fierce scrap 激烈争吵 
B.  transitive verb(present participle etc. -pp-) 
① (dispose of) 报废 bàofèi ‹car, equipment, missile›
② colloquial (do away with) 取消 qǔxiāo ‹project, plan›; 废除 fèichú ‹system, policy, tax›the two governments scrapped the peace talks 两国政府停止了和谈 
C.  intransitive verb(present participle etc. -pp-) colloquial (fight) 打架 dǎjià ; figurative (quarrel) 争吵 zhēngchǎo

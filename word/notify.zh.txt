notify 
BrE ˈnəʊtɪfʌɪ, AmE ˈnoʊdəˌfaɪ 
transitive verb 
① (inform) 通知 tōngzhī ‹police, authorities›to notify [somebody] of or about [something]; 通知某人某事 to notify [somebody] that …; 通知某人… 
② (report) 报告 bàogào ‹theft, crime, result›to notify [something] to [somebody]; 向某人报告某事 
③ (give formal notice to) 正式通知 zhèngshì tōngzhī ‹family, nation›to notify [somebody] of [something]; 通知某人 ‹accident, result›

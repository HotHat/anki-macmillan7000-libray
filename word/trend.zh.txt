trend 
BrE trɛnd, AmE trɛnd 

A.  noun 
① (tendency) (in events, prices) 趋势 qūshì (in allegiance, public opinion) 倾向 qīngxiàng an upward/downward trend 上升/下降的趋势 if the present trend continues 如果当前的趋势持续下去 a trend in medicine/education; 医学/教育方面的趋势 a trend towards doing [something]; 做某事的趋势 the trend is towards democracy 趋势是倾向于民主化 a trend away from [something]; 背离某事物的倾向 
② (fashion) 时新款式 shíxīn kuǎnshì a fashion trend 流行款式 to set a new trend 创新风 to follow the trend 赶时髦 
B.  intransitive verb 趋向 qūxiàng to trend higher/lower 趋向升高/下降 house prices are trending rapidly downwards 房价呈迅速下行趋势

front 
BrE frʌnt, AmE frənt 

A.  noun 
① Countable(forward-facing part) 前面 qiánmian ; (main flat side) 正面 zhèngmiàn in front of [something]; 在某物前面 there is a dent in the front of the car 车子前面有一个凹痕 the skirt fastens at the front 这条裙子在前面系扣 the book had a picture of an elephant on the front 书的封面有一张大象的图片 from the front, it looks like … 从正面看，它似乎… the eyes are at the front of the head (of animal) 眼睛在头的正面 I'd like it a bit shorter at the front, please (at hairdresser's) 我想要前面短一些 
② Countable(of person's body) 胸部 xiōngbù he spilt soup down his front 他把汤洒在了前胸上 I was lying on my front 我正趴着 
③ Countable(furthest forward interior part) 前部 qiánbù the teacher stood at the front of the room 老师站在房间前部 the pages at the front of the book 书的前几页 at the front of the plane 在飞机的前部 face the front! 向前看！ out front 观众席 
④ Countable(furthest forward part of procession, crowd) 前端 qiánduān I was at the front of the queue or line 我在队列的最前面 
⑤ Uncountable(position ahead of sb./sth.) 领先地位 lǐngxiān dìwèi to be in front (of [something]/[somebody]); 领先（某物/某人） if you want to stay in front of your competitors, you must … 如果想保持领先你的对手，你必须… 
⑥ Countable(initial position) 开始位置 kāishǐ wèizhi at the front 在开始位置 
⑦ Uncountable(presence) 面前 miànqián in front of [somebody]; 在某人面前 not in front of the children! 不要当着孩子的面！ 
⑧ Countableespecially BrE (promenade) 海滨人行道 hǎibīn rénxíngdào we went for a stroll along/on the front 我们沿着海滨路/在海滨路上漫步 on the river front 在滨河人行道上 
⑨ CountableMeteorology 锋 fēng a cold/warm/occluded front 冷锋/暖锋/锢囚锋 
⑩ CountableMilitary 前线 qiánxiàn to open up a front 开辟一条战线 
⑪ Countable(area of activity) 方面 fāngmiàn on the … front; 在…范围内 on the political/fashion front 在政治/时尚领域 
⑫ Countable(in names) 阵线 zhènxiàn the worker's/people's front 工人/人民阵线 
⑬ Countable(outer appearance) 外表 wàibiǎo to put on/show/present a bold/brave front 装出/显示出/呈现出大胆/勇敢的外表 to present a united front 表现出团结一致 
⑭ Countable(cover) 掩护 yǎnhù a front for [something]; 某物的掩护 the jewellery business was a front for a diamond-smuggling operation 珠宝生意被用作走私钻石的幌子 
⑮ Uncountable(boldness) 放肆 fàngsì he's got a bit of talent and a lot of front 他小有才华，但狂妄自大 he had the front to tell me I was wrong! 他竟厚颜无耻地对我说我错了！ 
B.  adjectiveattributive 
① (facing street) 前面的 qiánmian de ‹window, wall, gate, garden›(in the front) 前部的 qiánbù de ‹wheel, wing›(furthest from rear) 前端的 qiánduān de ‹carriage, seat, row›the piano is in the front room 钢琴在前厅 the front leg/foot/paw 前腿/足/爪 we sat in the front row 我们坐在前排 the front section of the book 书的第一部分 front teeth 门牙 
② Sport 领先的 lǐngxiān de ‹athlete, car, horse›
③ (head-on) 正面的 zhèngmiàn de ‹view, elevation›
C.  intransitive verb 
① (face) «house, window, garden» 朝向 cháoxiàng to front on to [something] BrE to front on [something] AmE 面向某物 his office fronts on to the car park 他的办公室面对着停车场 
② (act as cover) 作掩护 zuò yǎnhù to front for [somebody]/[something]; 掩护某人/某物 
D.  transitive verb 
① (face) «house, garden» 面向 miànxiàng ‹beach, river, street›
② (be in front of) «car park, wall, hedge» 位于…前面 wèiyú… qiánmian ‹building›the house is fronted by an attractive garden 房子前面是一个漂亮的花园 
③ (provide with front) 为…装正面 wèi… zhuāng zhèngmiàn ‹building, monument, cupboard›to be fronted with or by [something]; 正面覆盖某物 
④ colloquial (lead) 领导 lǐngdǎo ‹organization, group›

extension 
BrE ɪkˈstɛnʃ(ə)n, ɛkˈstɛnʃ(ə)n, AmE ˌɪkˈstɛn(t)ʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Countable(extra section) (of cable, table, road, track) 延长部分 yáncháng bùfen (to building) 扩建部分 kuòjiàn bùfen 
② Countable(prolongation) (of visa, permit, opening hours) 延期 yánqī (of deadline) 宽限期 kuānxiànqī 
③ Countable and uncountable(expansion) (of vocabulary, influence, power, authority) 扩大 kuòdà (of law) 适用范围扩大 shìyòng fànwéi kuòdà (of knowledge, meaning, idea, theory, story, business, market) 扩展 kuòzhǎn (of services, demand) 增加 zēngjiā 
④ Countable(telephone line) 分机 fēnjī (number) 分机号 fēnjīhào 
extension 
▶noun
1 they are planning a new extension: addition, adjunct, annex, wing, supplementary building, ell, add-on, bump-out.
2 an extension of knowledge: expansion, increase, enlargement, widening, broadening, deepening; augmentation, enhancement, development, growth, continuation.
3 an extension of opening hours: prolongation, lengthening, increase.
4 I need an extension on my essay: postponement, more/extra time, deferral, delay.

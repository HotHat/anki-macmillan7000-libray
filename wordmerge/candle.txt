candle 
ˈkandl

▶noun a cylinder or block of wax or tallow with a central wick that is lit to produce light as it burns. 
• (also international candle) Physics a unit of luminous intensity, superseded by the candela. 
▶verb [with object] (of a poultry breeder) test (an egg) for freshness or fertility by holding it to the light.

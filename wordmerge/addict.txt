addict 
ˈadikt

▶noun a person who is addicted to a particular substance, typically an illegal drug: a former heroin addict. 
• [with modifier] informal an enthusiastic devotee of a specified thing or activity: a must-buy book for the crossword-puzzle addict 
a self-confessed chocolate addict. ORIGIN early 20th century: from the obsolete verb addict, which was a back-formation from addicted .

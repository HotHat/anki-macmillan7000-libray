tonight 
təˈnīt

▶adverb on the present or approaching evening or night: are you doing anything tonight? 
▶noun the evening or night of the present day: tonight is a night to remember. ORIGIN Old English tō niht, from the preposition to + night. Compare with today and tomorrow .

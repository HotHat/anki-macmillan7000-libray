enjoyment 
BrE ɪnˈdʒɔɪmənt, AmE ɪnˈdʒɔɪmənt, ɛnˈdʒɔɪmənt 
noun 
① Uncountable(pleasure) 愉快 yúkuài to get enjoyment from [something]/doing [something]; 从某事/做某事中获得乐趣 to do [something] for enjoyment; 出于兴趣做某事 to spoil [somebody's] enjoyment of [something] 破坏某人对某事物的兴致 the pianist gave an encore, much to the enjoyment of the audience 令观众十分愉快的是，钢琴家加演了一曲 
② Countable(enjoyable activity) 乐事 lèshì gardening is one of her chief enjoyments in life 园艺是她生活中的主要乐趣之一 
③ Uncountableformal (entitlement) 享有 xiǎngyǒu enjoyment of rights/privileges/advantages 权利/特权/有利条件的享有

honestly 
ˈänəstlē

▶adverb
1 in a truthful, fair, or honorable way: he'd come by the money honestly.
2 used to emphasize the sincerity of an opinion, belief, or feeling: she honestly believed that she was making life easier for Jack. 
• [sentence adverb] used to emphasize the sincerity or truthfulness of a statement: honestly, darling, I'm not upset. 
• [sentence adverb] used to indicate the speaker's disapproval, annoyance, or impatience: honestly, that man is the absolute limit!

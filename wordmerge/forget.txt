forget 
▶verb
1 he forgot where he was: fail to remember, fail to recall, fail to think of; informal disremember. ANTONYMS remember.
2 I never forget my briefcase: leave behind, fail to take/bring.
3 I forgot to close the door: neglect, fail, omit.
4 you can forget that idea: stop thinking about, put out of one's mind, shut out, blank out, pay no heed to, not worry about, ignore, overlook, take no notice of; abandon, say goodbye to, deep-six.

behind 
BrE bɪˈhʌɪnd, AmE bəˈhaɪnd 

A.  adverb 
① (at the rear of sb./sth.) 在背后 zài bèihòu (towards the back of sb./sth.) 向后 xiàng hòu ‹look, glance›from behind 从后面 he grabbed her from behind 他从背后抓住了她 
② (remaining after departure) 留在原处 liú zài yuánchù to stay or remain behind 留下来 to keep [somebody] behind 把某人留下 
③ (further back) 在后面 zài hòumian the car behind 后面的汽车 winter is not far behind 冬天即将到来 
④ (in competition) 落后 luòhòu two goals behind 落后两个球 to be far or a long way behind 远远落后 
⑤ (late) 滞后 zhìhòu to be weeks/years behind 滞后好几周/好几年 to be behind with or in [something]; 积压 ‹work, studies›
⑥ (in arrears) 拖欠 tuōqiàn to be behind with [something]; 拖欠 tuōqiàn ‹rent, payments›
B.  preposition 
① (at the rear of) 在…的后面 zài… de hòumian (towards the back of) 向…的后面 xiàng… de hòumian look behind you 回头看看你背后 the mountains behind the town 城镇后面的山峦 the sun came out from behind a cloud 太阳拨云而出 they were miles behind us 他们落在我们后面好几英里 she shut the gate behind her 她随手关上大门 
② (underlying) 隐藏在…后面 yǐncáng zài… hòumian the hatred behind his apparent friendliness 藏在他友善外表下面的仇恨 the real story behind the news 新闻背后的实情 
③ (responsible for) 作为…的原因 zuòwéi… de yuányīn the man behind the plan 制订计划的人 what is behind his actions? 他的动机是什么？ who is behind this proposal? 谁提出的这个议案？ 
④ (in support of) 支持 zhīchí (giving guidance) 指引 zhǐyǐn to be (solidly) behind [somebody] （坚决）支持某人 he has no family behind him 他没有家人的支持 
⑤ (less advanced than) 落后于 luòhòu yú to be behind the others «pupil» 落后于其他人 we're
3 points behind the other team 我们比另一支队伍落后了3分 
⑥ (in one's past) 成为…的过去 chéngwéi… de guòqù those days are behind me now 对我来说，那些日子已经过去了 he has three years' experience behind him 他已经有3年的经验了 to put [something] behind one 忘掉某事 
C.  noun colloquial 屁股 pìgu he needs a kick in the behind 他需要鞭策 
behind 
▶preposition
1 he hid behind a tree: at the back/rear of, beyond, on the far/other side of, in back of. ANTONYMS in front of.
2 a guard ran behind him: after, following, at the back/rear of, (hard) on the heels of, in the wake of. ANTONYMS ahead of.
3 he was behind the bombings: responsible for, at the bottom of, the cause of, the source of, the organizer of; to blame for, culpable of, guilty of.
4 we're behind you all the way: supporting, backing, for, on the side of, in agreement with; financing; informal rooting for.
▶adverb
1 a man followed behind: after, afterward, at the back/end, in the rear. ANTONYMS in front, ahead.
2 I looked behind: over one's shoulder, to/toward the back, to/toward the rear, backward. ANTONYMS ahead.
3 we're behind, so don't stop: late, running late, behind schedule, behindhand, not on time, behind time.
4 he was behind with his subscription: in arrears, overdue; late, unpunctual, behindhand.
▶noun informal he sat on his behind. See buttocks. 
PHRASES
put something behind one they put last night's loss to the Orioles behind them: consign to the past, put down to experience, regard as water under the bridge, forget about, ignore.

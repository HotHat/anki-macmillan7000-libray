USAGE
barbecue Barbecue is often misspelled as barbeque. This form arises understandably from the word's pronunciation and from the informal abbreviations BBQ and Bar-B-
Q.  Although almost a quarter of citations in the Oxford English Corpus are for the -que spelling, it is not accepted in standard English.Usage notes show additional guidance on finer points of English usage.

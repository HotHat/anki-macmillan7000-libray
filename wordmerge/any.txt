any 
▶adjective
1 is there any cake left? some, a piece of, a part of, a bit of.
2 it doesn't make any difference: the slightest bit of, a scrap of, a shred of, a whit of, a particle of, an iota of, a jot of.
3 any job will do: whichever, no matter which, never mind which; informal any old.
▶pronoun
1 you don't know any of my friends: a single one, one, even one.
2 we no longer give to any, unless they represent one of our top five charities: anyone, anybody, any individual/person; any group.
▶adverb is your father any better? at all, in the least, to any extent, in/to any degree.

muddy 
▶adjective
1 muddy ground: waterlogged, boggy, marshy, swampy, squishy, mucky, slimy, spongy, wet, soft, heavy; archaic quaggy.
2 muddy shoes: mud-caked, muddied, dirty, filthy, mucky, grimy, soiled; literary begrimed. ANTONYMS clean.
3 muddy water: murky, cloudy, muddied, turbid, riled. ANTONYMS clear.
4 a muddy pink: dingy, dirty, drab, dull, sludgy.
▶verb
1 don't muddy your boots: make muddy, dirty, soil, spatter, bespatter; literary besmirch, begrime.
2 these results muddy the situation: make unclear, obscure, confuse, obfuscate, blur, cloud, befog. ANTONYMS clarify.

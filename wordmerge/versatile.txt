▶adjective

2 archaic changeable; inconstant. DERIVATIVES versatilely adverbORIGIN early 17th century (in the sense ‘inconstant, fluctuating’): from French, or from Latin versatilis, from versat- ‘turned around, revolved,’ from the verb versare, frequentative of vertere ‘to turn.’

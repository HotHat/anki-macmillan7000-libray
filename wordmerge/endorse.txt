endorse 
▶verb
1 endorse a product: support, back, agree with, approve (of), favor, subscribe to, recommend, champion, stick up for, uphold, affirm, sanction; informal throw one's weight behind, okay. ANTONYMS oppose.
2 endorse a check: countersign, sign, autograph, authenticate; rare chirographate.

trailer 
ˈtrālər

▶noun
1 an unpowered vehicle towed by another, in particular: 
• the rear section of a tractor-trailer. 
• an open cart. 
• a platform for transporting a boat. 
• North American an unpowered vehicle equipped for living in, typically used during vacations.
2 an excerpt or series of excerpts from a movie or program used to advertise it in advance; a preview.
3 a thing that trails, especially a trailing plant. 
▶verb [with object]
1 advertise (a movie or program) in advance by broadcasting excerpts or details.
2 transport (something) by trailer.

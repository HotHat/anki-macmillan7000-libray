▶noun a destructive insect or other animal that attacks crops, food, livestock, etc. 
• informal an annoying person or thing; a nuisance. 
• (the pest) archaic bubonic plague. ORIGIN late 15th century (denoting the bubonic plague): from French peste or Latin pestis ‘plague.’

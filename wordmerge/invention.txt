invention 
▶noun
1 the invention of the telescope: origination, creation, innovation, devising, development, design.
2 medieval inventions: innovation, creation, design, contraption, contrivance, construction, device, gadget; informal brainchild.
3 she played with taste and invention: inventiveness, originality, creativity, imagination, inspiration.
4 the story was a total invention: fabrication, concoction, (piece of) fiction, story, tale; lie, untruth, falsehood, fib, myth, fantasy, make-believe; informal tall tale, cock-and-bull story.

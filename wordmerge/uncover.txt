uncover 
ˌənˈkəvər

▶verb [with object] remove a cover or covering from: he uncovered the face of the dead man. 
• discover (something previously secret or unknown): further evidence has been uncovered. 
• [no object] archaic remove one's hat, especially as a mark of respect.

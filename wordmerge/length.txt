length 
BrE lɛŋ(k)θ, lɛn(t)θ, AmE lɛŋ(k)θ, lɛnth 

A.  noun 
① Countable and uncountable(linear measurement) 长度 chángdù a river 500 kilometres in length 一条500公里长的河流 this room is twice the length of the other 这个房间的长度是另一个的两倍 
② Uncountablethe length of … (all the way along) 全长 quáncháng the whole length of the street was planted with trees 整条街上都种了树 ice had formed along the length of the plane's wing 整个机翼都结了冰 
③ Countable(of swimming pool) 游泳池长度 yóuyǒngchí chángdù to swim/do a length (of the pool) 游一个游泳池长度的距离 he does
20 lengths every morning 他每天早晨游10个来回 
④ Countable(in race) 自身长度 zìshēn chángdù the horse is two lengths ahead 那匹马领先两个马身的距离 I was two car lengths behind the other vehicle 我在另一辆车后面两个车身远 
⑤ Countable(piece, section) 一段 yī duàn a length of rope 一根绳子 to be sold in lengths of five metres 5米一段出售 a dress length 一块够做一条连衣裙的布料 
⑥ Countable and uncountable(size, extent) 篇幅 piānfú a book 200 pages in length 一本200页的书 a book the length of War and Peace 一本篇幅像《战争与和平》一样长的书 
⑦ Countable and uncountable(duration) 时间的长短 shíjiān de chángduǎn the film is three hours in length 这部影片长3小时 the whole length of [something] 某事的全部时间 a considerable length of time 很长一段时间 the length of time between two events 两件事之间的间隔 
⑧ Uncountable(quality of being long) 长 cháng I was struck by the length of his fingernails 他的指甲那么长，让我很吃惊 a war of such length 一场如此旷日持久的战争 
⑨ at length (for a long time) 长时间地 cháng shíjiān de (at last) 最后 zuìhòu he went on at tedious length about his favourite hobby 他啰啰唆唆地谈论他最大的爱好 
⑩ Countable and uncountable(of vowel, syllable) 音长 yīncháng 
B.  lengths noun plural (extent, measures) 程度 chéngdù to go to great/any/such lengths (to do [something]) （为做某事）不遗余力 they went to absurd lengths to keep the affair secret 他们为了对这件事保密无所不用其极 to go to the lengths of doing [something] 到做某事的程度 he even went to the lengths of writing to the Queen 他甚至给女王写了信 
length 
▶noun
1 a length of three or four yards 
the whole length of the valley: extent, distance, linear measure, span, reach; area, expanse, stretch, range, scope.
2 a considerable length of time: period, duration, stretch, span.
3 a length of blue silk: piece, swatch, measure.
4 the press criticized the length of her speech: lengthiness, extent, prolixity, wordiness, verbosity, long-windedness.

2 Everett was questioned at length: thoroughly, fully, in detail, in depth, comprehensively, exhaustively, extensively.

bungalow 
ˈbəNGɡəˌlō

▶noun a low house, with a broad front porch, having either no upper floor or upper rooms set in the roof, typically with dormer windows. ORIGIN late 17th century: from Hindi baṅglā ‘belonging to Bengal.’

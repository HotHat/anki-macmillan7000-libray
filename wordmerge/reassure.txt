reassure 
ˌrēəˈSHo͝or

▶verb [with object] say or do something to remove the doubts and fears of someone: he understood her feelings and tried to reassure her 
[with object and clause] : Joachim reassured him that he was needed 
(as adjective reassuring) : Gina gave her a reassuring smile.

accomplish 
əˈkämpliSH

▶verb [with object] achieve or complete successfully: the planes accomplished their mission. ORIGIN late Middle English: from Old French acompliss-, lengthened stem of acomplir, based on Latin ad- ‘to’ + complere ‘to complete.’

chain 
▶noun
1 he was held in chains: fetters, shackles, irons, leg irons, manacles, handcuffs; informal cuffs, bracelets; historical bilboes.
2 a chain of events: series, succession, string, sequence, train, course.
▶verb she chained her bicycle to the railing: secure, fasten, tie, tether, hitch; restrain, shackle, fetter, manacle, handcuff.

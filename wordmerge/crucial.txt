1 negotiations were at a crucial stage: pivotal, critical, key, climacteric, decisive, deciding; life-and-death. ANTONYMS minor, unimportant.

USAGE
crucial Crucial is used in formal contexts to mean ‘decisive, critical’: the testimony of the only eyewitness was crucial to the case.

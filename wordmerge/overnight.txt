overnight 
A.  /BrE əʊvəˈnʌɪt, ˈəʊvənʌɪt, AmE ˌoʊvərˈnaɪt
adverb 
① (for the night) 一晚上 yī wǎnshang ‹stop›; (through the night) 整晚 zhěng wǎn ‹drive›to stay overnight at a friend's house 在朋友家住一晚 
② figurative (suddenly) 一夜之间 yī yè zhījiān ‹change›
B.  /BrE əʊvəˈnʌɪt, ˈəʊvənʌɪt, AmE ˈoʊvərˌnaɪt
adjective 
① (night-time) 夜间的 yèjiān de ‹journey, train›; 过夜的 guòyè de ‹guest›; 持续一晚的 chíxù yī wǎn de ‹stay, party, rain›
② attributive figurative (sudden) 一夜之间的 yī yè zhījiān de ‹success, change›

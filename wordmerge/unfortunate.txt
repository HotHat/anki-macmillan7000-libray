unfortunate 
▶adjective
1 unfortunate people: unlucky, hapless, jinxed, out of luck, luckless, wretched, miserable, forlorn, poor, pitiful; informal down on one's luck. ANTONYMS lucky.
2 an unfortunate start to our vacation: adverse, disadvantageous, unfavorable, unlucky, unwelcome, unpromising, inauspicious, unpropitious, bad; formal grievous. ANTONYMS auspicious.
3 an unfortunate remark: regrettable, inappropriate, unsuitable, infelicitous, unbecoming, inopportune, tactless, injudicious.

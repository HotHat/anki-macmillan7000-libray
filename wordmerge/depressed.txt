depressed 
dəˈprest

▶adjective (of a person) in a state of general unhappiness or despondency. 
• (of a person) suffering from clinical depression. 
• (of a place or economic activity) suffering the damaging effects of a lack of demand or employment: depressed urban areas. 
• (of an object or part of an object) in a physically lower position, having been pushed or forced down: a depressed fracture of the skull.

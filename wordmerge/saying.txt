saying 
ˈsāiNG

▶noun a short, pithy expression that generally contains advice or wisdom. 
• (sayings) a collection of short, pithy expressions identified with a particular person, especially a political or religious leader. 
PHRASES
as (or so) the saying goes used to introduce or append an expression, drawing attention to its status as a saying or as not part of one's normal language: I am, as the saying goes, burned out.

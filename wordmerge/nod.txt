nod 
▶verb
1 she nodded her head: incline, bob, bow, dip.
2 he nodded to me to start: signal, gesture, gesticulate, motion, sign, indicate.
▶noun
1 she gave a nod to the manager: signal, indication, sign, cue; gesture.
2 a quick nod of his head: inclination, bob, bow, dip.
3 Halifax will get the nod as host city: approval, selection, sanction, endorsement; informal OK, A-OK, green light, thumbs up.
PHRASES
nod off give me a pillow and I'll nod off right this second: fall asleep, go to sleep, doze off, drop off; informal drift off, go out like a light, sack out, drift into the arms of Morpheus.

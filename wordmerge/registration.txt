registration 
ˌrejəˈstrāSH(ə)n

▶noun
1 the action or process of registering or of being registered: the registration of births, marriages, and deaths 
the number of new private car registrations has increased. 
• a certificate that attests to the registering of a person, automobile, etc.
2 Music a combination of stops used when playing the organ. ORIGIN mid 16th century: from medieval Latin registratio(n-), based on Latin regerere ‘enter, record’ (see register) .

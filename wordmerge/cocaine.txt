cocaine 
kōˈkān ˈkōˌkān

▶noun an addictive drug derived from coca or prepared synthetically, used as an illegal stimulant and sometimes medicinally as a local anesthetic. [An alkaloid; chemical formula: C 17H 21NO 4.] ORIGIN mid 19th century: from coca + -ine4 .

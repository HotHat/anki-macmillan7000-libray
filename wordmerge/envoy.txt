envoy 
ˈenˌvoi ˈänˌvoi

▶noun
1 a messenger or representative, especially one on a diplomatic mission.
2 short for envoy extraordinary. ORIGIN mid 17th century: from French envoyé, past participle of envoyer ‘send,’ from en voie ‘on the way,’ based on Latin via ‘way.’

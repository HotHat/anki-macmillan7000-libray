obstacle 
ˈäbstək(ə)l

▶noun a thing that blocks one's way or prevents or hinders progress: the major obstacle to achieving that goal is money. ORIGIN Middle English: via Old French from Latin obstaculum, from obstare ‘impede,’ from ob- ‘against’ + stare ‘stand.’

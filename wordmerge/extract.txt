extract 
A.  /BrE ɪkˈstrakt, ɛkˈstrakt, AmE ɪkˈstrækt
transitive verb 
① (pull out) 取出 qǔchū ‹bullet›; 挑出 tiāochū ‹splinter›; 拔出 báchū ‹tooth›
② (obtain) 设法得到 shèfǎ dédào ‹confession, promise, payment, information›to extract [something] from [somebody] (object) 设法从某人处取得某物 (information) 设法从某人处知悉某事情 shèfǎ cóng mǒu rén chù zhīxī mǒu shìqing 
③ (derive) 获得 huòdé ‹pleasure, benefit›; 设法实现 shèfǎ shíxiàn ‹publicity›; 引申出 yǐnshēn chū ‹idea, meaning, moral›the following ideas are extracted from a variety of theories 以下构思取自多个不同理论 
④ (obtain by chemical, physical process) 提取 tíqǔ ‹essence, dye, oil›; 榨取 zhàqǔ ‹juice›; 采掘 cǎijué ‹minerals, coal›copper is extracted from copper ore by smelting 铜是利用熔炼的方式从铜矿中提炼出来的 
⑤ (select, excerpt) 摘录 zhāilù ‹article, passage, quotation›
B.  /BrE ˈɛkstrakt, AmE ˈɛkˌstrækt
noun 
① Countable(excerpt) 摘录 zhāilù 
② Countable and uncountable(preparation, solution) 萃取物 cuìqǔwù 
extract 
▶verb
1 he extracted the videocassette: take out, draw out, pull out, remove, withdraw; free, release, extricate. ANTONYMS insert.
2 extracting money: wrest, exact, wring, screw, squeeze, obtain by force, obtain by threat(s), extort, blackmail someone for; informal put the bite on someone for.
3 the roots are crushed to extract the juice: squeeze out, express, press out, obtain. ANTONYMS add, infuse.
4 the figures are extracted from the report: excerpt, select, reproduce, copy, take. ANTONYMS insert.
5 ideas extracted from a variety of theories: derive, develop, evolve, deduce, infer, obtain; formal educe.
▶noun
1 an extract from his article: excerpt, passage, citation, quotation; (excerpts) analects.
2 an extract of the ginseng root: decoction, distillation, distillate, abstraction, concentrate, essence, juice.

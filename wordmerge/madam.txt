madam 
ˈmadəm

▶noun used to address or refer to a woman in a polite or respectful way: Can I help you, madam? 
• (Madam) used to address a woman at the start of a formal or business letter: Dear Madam, …. 
• (Madam) used before a title to address or refer to a female holder of that position: Madam President. 
• a woman who runs a brothel. ORIGIN Middle English: from Old French ma dame ‘my lady.’

eventual 
əˈven(t)SH(o͞o)əl

▶adjective [attributive] occurring at the end of or as a result of a series of events; final; ultimate: it's impossible to predict the eventual outcome of the competition. ORIGIN early 17th century (in the sense ‘relating to an event or events’): from Latin eventus (see event), on the pattern of actual.

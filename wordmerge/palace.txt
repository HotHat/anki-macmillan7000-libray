palace 
ˈpaləs

▶noun the official residence of a sovereign, archbishop, bishop, or other exalted person: the royal palace. 
• informal a large, splendid house. ORIGIN Middle English: from Old French paleis, from Latin Palatium, the name of the Palatine hill in Rome, where the house of the emperor was situated.

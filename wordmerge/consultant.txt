consultant 
kənˈsəltnt

▶noun
1 a person who provides expert advice professionally.
2 [usually as modifier] British a hospital doctor of senior rank within a specific field: a consultant pediatrician. ORIGIN late 17th century (in the sense ‘a person who consults’): probably from French, from Latin consultare (see consult) .

equilibrium 
▶noun
1 the equilibrium of the economy: balance, symmetry, equipoise, parity, equality; stability. ANTONYMS imbalance.
2 his equilibrium was never shaken: composure, calm, equanimity, sangfroid; level-headedness, coolheadedness, imperturbability, poise, presence of mind; self-possession, self-command; impassivity, placidity, tranquility, serenity; informal cool. ANTONYMS nervousness, agitation.

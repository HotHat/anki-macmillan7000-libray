summary 
ˈsəmərē

▶noun (plural summaries) a brief statement or account of the main points of something: a summary of Chapter Three. 
▶adjective
1 dispensing with needless details or formalities; brief: summary financial statements.
2 Law (of a judicial process) conducted without the customary legal formalities: summary arrest. 
• (of a conviction) made by a judge or magistrate without a jury. 
PHRASES
in summary in short: in summary, there is no clear case for one tax system compared to another.DERIVATIVES summariness

soar 
▶verb
1 the bird soared into the air: fly, wing, ascend, climb, rise; take off, take flight. ANTONYMS plummet.
2 the gulls soared on the winds: glide, plane, float, drift, wheel, hover.
3 the cost of living soared: increase, escalate, shoot up, rise, spiral; informal go through the roof, skyrocket.

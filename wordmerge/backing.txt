backing 
ˈbakiNG

▶noun
1 support or help: he accepted the backing of the police group 
they had financial backing from local firms. 
• a layer of material that forms, protects, or strengthens the back of something: the fabric has a special backing for durability. 
• (especially in popular music) the music or singing that accompanies the main singer or soloist: the trio provided backing to some of the most popular vocalists of the day 
[as modifier] : backing vocals.
2 Phonetics the movement of the place of formation of a sound toward the back of the mouth.

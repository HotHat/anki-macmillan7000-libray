recreation
1 
ˌrekrēˈāSH(ə)n

▶noun activity done for enjoyment when one is not working: areas used for recreation such as hiking or biking 
[as modifier] : athletic and recreation facilities. ORIGIN late Middle English (also in the sense ‘mental or spiritual consolation’): via Old French from Latin recreatio(n-), from recreare ‘create again, renew.’

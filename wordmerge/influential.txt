adjective

influential 
ˌinflo͞oˈen(t)SH(ə)l

▶adjective having great influence on someone or something: her work is influential in feminist psychology. 
▶noun (usually influentials) an influential person. DERIVATIVES influentially adverbORIGIN late 16th century (referring to astral influence): from medieval Latin influentia (see influence) .

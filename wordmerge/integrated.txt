integrated 
ˈin(t)əˌɡrādəd

▶adjective having been integrated, in particular: 
• (of an institution, body, etc.) desegregated, especially racially: integrated education. 
• with various parts or aspects linked or coordinated: an integrated and high-quality public transportation system. 
• chiefly Physics indicating the mean value or total sum of (temperature, an area, etc.): integrated electron density along the line of sight.

perfectly 
ˈpərfək(t)lē

▶adverb in a manner or way that could not be better: the ring fitted perfectly 
[as submodifier] : perfectly clean glass bottles. 
• [as submodifier] used for emphasis, especially in order to assert something that has been challenged or doubted: you know perfectly well I can't stay.

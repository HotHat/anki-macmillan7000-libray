draught 
BrE drɑːft, AmE dræft/ BrE A. noun 
① Countable(current of air) 通风气流 tōngfēng qìliú there's a terrible draught in the room 房间里有一股很大的穿堂风 to sit in a draught 坐在风口上 to feel the draught figurative colloquial 手头拮据 
② Countable(to fire, furnace) 抽风 chōufēng 
③ Uncountable(of beer) on draught 散装的 sǎnzhuāng de 
④ Countable(act of drinking) 一饮 yī yǐn (act of inhaling) 吸入 xīrù (amount swallowed) 一饮之量 yī yǐn zhī liàng (amount inhaled) 一口吸入的量 yī kǒu xīrù de liàng she downed it in a single draught 她一口把它喝光了 to take long draughts of cool air 大口大口地吸入凉爽的空气 
⑤ Countable(of ship) 吃水深度 chīshuǐ shēndù a vessel with a shallow draught 吃水浅的船 
⑥ Countable BrE (piece in draughts) 国际跳棋棋子 guójì tiàoqí qízǐ 
B.  adjective 
① (on draught) 散装的 sǎnzhuāng de ‹beer›
② (used to pull loads) 拖曳重载的 tuōyè zhòngzài de a draught animal 役畜

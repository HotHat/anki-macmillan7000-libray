banana 
BrE bəˈnɑːnə, AmE bəˈnænə 
noun 
① (fruit) 香蕉 xiāngjiāo a bunch of bananas 一串香蕉 
② (tree) 香蕉树 xiāngjiāo shù 
③ to go bananas colloquial (go mad or crazy) 发疯 fāfēng (become angry) 发怒 fānù

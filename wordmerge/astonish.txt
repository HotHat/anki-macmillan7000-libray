transitive verb 使大为惊奇 shǐ dàwéi jīngqí it astonishes me that … 我真没料到… you astonish me! literal 真想不到！ ironic 一点都不奇怪！ yīdiǎn dōu bù qíguài ！ 
astonish 
▶verb he astonished many when he dismounted from his horse in full armor and led his men on foot: amaze, astound, stagger, surprise, startle, stun, confound, dumbfound, strike dumb, boggle, stupefy, daze, shock, take aback, leave open-mouthed, leave aghast; informal flabbergast, blow away, bowl over, floor.

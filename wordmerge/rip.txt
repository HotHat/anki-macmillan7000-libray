RIP abbreviation = rest in peace 安息 ānxī [书于墓碑上]
rip 
▶verb
1 he ripped the posters down: tear, wrench, wrest, pull, snatch, tug, pry, heave, drag, peel, pluck; informal yank.
2 she ripped Leo's note into pieces: tear, claw, hack, slit, cut; literary rend.
▶noun a rip in my sleeve: tear, slit, split, rent, laceration, cut, gash, slash.

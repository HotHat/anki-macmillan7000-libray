possibly 
ˈpäsəblē

▶adverb
1 [sentence adverb] perhaps (used to indicate doubt or hesitancy): he found himself alone, possibly the only surviving officer. 
• [with modal] used in polite requests: could you possibly pour me another cup of coffee?
2 [usually with modal] in accordance with what is likely or achievable, in particular: 
• used to emphasize that something is difficult, surprising, or bewildering: what can you possibly mean? 
• used to emphasize that someone has or will put all their effort into something: be as noisy as you possibly can.

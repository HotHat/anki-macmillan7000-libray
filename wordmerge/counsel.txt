counsel 
▶noun
1 his wise counsel: advice, guidance, counseling, direction, information; hints, recommendations, suggestions, guidelines, tips, pointers, warnings.
2 the counsel for the defense: lawyer, advocate, attorney, attorney-at-law, counselor; chiefly British solicitor, barrister.
▶verb he counseled the team to withdraw from the deal: advise, recommend, direct, advocate, encourage, urge, warn, caution; guide, give guidance.

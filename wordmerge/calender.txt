calender 
ˈkaləndər

▶noun a machine in which cloth or paper is pressed by rollers to glaze or smooth it. 
▶verb [with object] press in a calender. ORIGIN late 15th century (as a verb): from French calendre (noun), calendrer (verb), of unknown origin.

revolve 
BrE rɪˈvɒlv, AmE rəˈvɑlv 
A. intransitive verb 
① literal (rotate) «wheel, fan» 旋转 xuánzhuǎn the earth revolves on its axis/around the sun 地球绕地轴自转/绕太阳公转 
② figurative to revolve around [somebody]/[something] (focus on) 以某人/某事物为中心 yǐ mǒu rén/mǒu shìwù wéi zhōngxīn his life revolves around his work 他的生活以工作为中心 
B.  transitive verb 反复掂量 fǎnfù diānliang she revolved the possibilities in her mind 她在心中反复思考了各种可能性 
revolve 
▶verb
1 a fan revolved slowly: go around, turn around, rotate, spin.
2 the moon revolves around the earth: circle, travel, orbit.
3 his life revolves around cars: be concerned with, be preoccupied with, focus on, center on/around.

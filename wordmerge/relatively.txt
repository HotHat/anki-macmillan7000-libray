relatively 
ˈrelədivlē

▶adverb [sentence adverb] in relation, comparison, or proportion to something else: it is perfectly simple, relatively speaking, to store a full catalog entry on magnetic tape. 
• [as submodifier] viewed in comparison with something else rather than absolutely: relatively affluent people 
the site was cheap and relatively clean.

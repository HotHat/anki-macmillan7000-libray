supervise 
BrE ˈsuːpəvʌɪz, AmE ˈsupərˌvaɪz 

A.  transitive verb 
① (oversee activity of) 管理 guǎnlǐ ‹worker, team, student›; 照料 zhàoliào ‹child, patient›
② (oversee execution of) 监督 jiāndū ‹job, activity, operation›; 指导 zhǐdǎo ‹thesis, research›to supervise an exam 监考 
③ (oversee activity in) 监管 jiānguǎn ‹building site, department›a supervised playground 受监管的游乐场 
B.  intransitive verb «manager, supervisor» 监督 jiāndū ; «doctor, parent» 照料 zhàoliào 
supervise 
▶verb
1 he had to supervise the loading: oversee, superintend, be in charge of, preside over, direct, manage, run, look after, be responsible for, govern, organize, handle, micromanage.
2 you may need to supervise the patient: watch, oversee, keep an eye on, observe, monitor, mind; invigilate.

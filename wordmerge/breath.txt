breath 
▶noun
1 I took a deep breath: inhalation, inspiration, gulp of air; exhalation, expiration; Medicine respiration.
2 a breath of wind: puff, waft, faint breeze.
3 a breath of scandal: hint, suggestion, trace, touch, whisper, murmur, suspicion, whiff, undertone.
4 there was no breath left in him: life, life force.

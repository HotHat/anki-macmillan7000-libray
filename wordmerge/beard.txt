▶noun
1 a growth of hair on the chin and lower cheeks of a man's face: he had a black beard 
three days' growth of beard. 
• a tuft of hair on the chin of certain mammals, for example a lion or goat. 
• an animal's growth or marking that is likened to a beard, e.g., the gills of an oyster, or the beak bristles of certain birds. 
• a tuft of hairs or bristles on certain plants, especially the awn of a grass.
2 US informal a person who carries out a transaction, typically a bet, for someone else in order to conceal the other's identity. 
• North American informal a person who pretends to have a romantic or sexual relationship with someone else in order to conceal the other's true sexual orientation. 
▶verb [with object] boldly confront or challenge (someone formidable).

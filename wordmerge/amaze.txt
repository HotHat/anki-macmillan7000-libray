amaze 
əˈmāz

▶verb [with object] surprise (someone) greatly; fill with astonishment: he was amazed at how modern everything was 
[with object and clause] : she was amazed that Paul should notice her 
(as adjective amazed) : she shook her head in amazed disbelief. ORIGIN Old English āmasian, of unknown origin.

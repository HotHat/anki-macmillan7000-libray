surf 
sərf

▶noun the mass or line of foam formed by waves breaking on a seashore or reef: the roar of the surf. 
• [in singular] a spell of surfing: he went for an early surf. 
▶verb [no object] stand or lie on a surfboard and ride on a wave toward the shore: learning to surf. 
• [with object] ride (a wave) toward the shore on a surfboard: he has built a career out of surfing big waves. 
• informal ride on the roof or outside of a fast-moving vehicle, typically a train, for excitement: he fell to his death while surfing on a 70 mph train. 
• short for channel-surf. 
• [with object] move from site to site on (the Internet). DERIVATIVES surfy

silk 
BrE sɪlk, AmE sɪlk 
noun 
① Uncountable(fibre) 丝 sī 
② Uncountable(fabric) 丝绸 sīchóu a silk shirt/tie/flower 真丝衬衫/真丝领带/绢花 silk production/industry 丝织品生产/丝织业 as smooth as silk 如丝绸般光滑的 
③ Uncountable(thread) 丝线 sīxiàn 
④ Countable(clothing) 丝绸服装 sīchóu fúzhuāng 
⑤ Uncountable(of spider) 蛛丝 zhūsī 
⑥ Uncountable(of sweetcorn) 须 xū 
⑦ Uncountable BrE Law colloquial 王室律师 wángshì lǜshī to take silk; 担任王室律师

supposed adjective 
① /BrE səˈpəʊzd, AmE səˈpoʊzd, səˈpoʊzəd/; attributive (alleged) 误以为的 wù yǐwéi de when did this supposed accident happen? 这场所谓的事故发生在什么时候？ 
② /BrE səˈpəʊzd, AmE səˈpoʊzd, səˈpoʊzəd/; predicative (expected, required) 应当的 yīngdāng de to be supposed to do [something]; 应当做某事 you’re supposed to buy a ticket, but not many people do 按说应当买票，不过买的人不多 I thought we were supposed to be paid today 我原以为我们今天会领到薪水呢 the engine doesn’t sound like it’s supposed to 发动机听起来不对劲 
③ /BrE səˈpəʊzd, AmE səˈpoʊzd, səˈpoʊzəd/; predicative (believed) 普遍认为的 pǔbiàn rènwéi de to be supposed to be [something]/to do [something]; 普遍认为是某物/做某事 it's supposed to be a good hotel 这家旅馆口碑不错 
④ /BrE səˈpəʊzd, AmE səˈpoʊzd, səˈpoʊzəd/; predicative (allowed) 允许的 yǔnxǔ de to be not supposed to do [something]; 不允许做某事 you’re not supposed to walk on the grass 不准践踏草地

pollution 
pəˈlo͞oSH(ə)n

▶noun the presence in or introduction into the environment of a substance or thing that has harmful or poisonous effects: the level of pollution in the air is rising. ORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin pollutio(n-), from the verb polluere (see pollute) .

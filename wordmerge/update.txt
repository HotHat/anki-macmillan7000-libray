update 
▶verb 
ˌəpˈdāt ˈəpˌdāt 
[with object] make (something) more modern or up to date: security measures are continually updated and improved 
(as adjective updated) : an updated list of subscribers. 
• give (someone) the latest information about something: the reporter promised to keep the viewers updated. 
▶noun 
ˈəpˌdāt 
an act of bringing something or someone up to date, or an updated version of something: an update on recently published crime figures. DERIVATIVES updatable adjective (Computing)

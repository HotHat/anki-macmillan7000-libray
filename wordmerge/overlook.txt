overlook 
▶verb
1 he overlooked the mistake: fail to notice, fail to spot, miss.
2 his work has been overlooked: disregard, neglect, ignore, pay no attention/heed to, pass over, forget.
3 she was almost willing to overlook his faults: ignore, not take into consideration, disregard, take no notice of, make allowances for, turn a blind eye to, excuse, pardon, forgive.

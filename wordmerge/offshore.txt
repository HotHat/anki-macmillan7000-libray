offshore 
BrE ˈɒfʃɔː, ɒfˈʃɔː, AmE ˌɔfˈʃɔr, ˌɑfˈʃɔr 

A.  adjective 
① (away from the coast) 近海的 jìnhǎi de ‹island, anchorage›; 向海的 xiàng hǎi de ‹wind›
② (in oil industry) 海上的 hǎi shang de ‹drilling, oilfield, platform›
③ Finance 境外的 jìngwài de ‹banking›an offshore fund/tax haven 境外基金/避税港 
B.  adverb 
① (away from the coast) 离岸地 lí'àn de 
② (in oil industry) 在海上 zài hǎi shang ‹drill, prospect›
③ Finance 在境外 zài jìngwài to invest/bank offshore 在海外投资/存钱

she 
BrE ʃiː, AmE ʃi 

A.  pronoun (referring to woman, girl) 她 tā (referring to female animal) 它 tā (referring to ship, vehicle, country) 她 tā 
B.  noun 
① (woman) 女人 nǚrén ; (girl) 女孩 nǚhái is the baby a he or a she? 这个婴儿是男孩还是女孩？ 
② (female animal) 雌性动物 cíxìng dòngwù what a sweet little dog! is it a he or a she? 多么可爱的小狗！是公的还是母的？

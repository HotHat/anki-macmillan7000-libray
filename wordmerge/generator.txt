generator 
ˈjenəˌrādər

▶noun a thing that generates something, in particular: 
• a dynamo or similar machine for converting mechanical energy into electricity. 
• an apparatus for producing gas, steam, or another product. 
• [with modifier] Computing a routine that constructs other routines or subroutines using given parameters, for specific applications: a report generator. 
• Mathematics a point, line, or surface regarded as moving and so notionally forming a line, surface, or solid.

differentiate 
BrE ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃɪeɪt, AmE ˌdɪfəˈrɛn(t)ʃiˌeɪt

① (tell the difference between) 区分 qūfēn (make the difference between) 使不同 shǐ bùtóng differentiate [something] from [something]; 把某物和某物区分开 the officers are differentiated from the men by their uniform 军官和士兵通过军服进行区别 
② Mathematics 求…的微分 qiú… de wēifēn ‹quantity, expression›
B.  intransitive verb 
① (tell the difference) 区分 qūfēn to differentiate between A and B; 区分A和B 
② (show the difference) 表明差别 biǎomíng chābié to differentiate between A and B; 表明A和B之间的差别 
③ (discriminate) 区别对待 qūbié duìdài to differentiate between A and B; 区别对待A和B

coast

A.  noun 
① (part of land beside sea) 海岸 hǎi'àn off the coast 在近海岸的海上 from coast to coast 在整个大陆 the coast is clear figurative 四下无人 
② (region) 沿海地区 yánhǎi dìqū the Atlantic coast 大西洋沿岸地区 
③ (seaside) 海边 hǎibiān 
④ (glide) 滑行 huáxíng 
B.  intransitive verb 
① (freewheel) 靠惯性滑行 kào guànxìng huáxíng to coast along at 50 mph 以每小时50英里的速度滑行 
② (glide) 平稳行进 píngwěn xíngjìn ; figurative 不费力地前进 bù fèilì de qiánjìn to coast along; (glide) 平稳前行 figurative 顺利进展 shùnlì jìnzhǎn to coast through an exam 顺利通过考试 to coast to victory 轻松取胜

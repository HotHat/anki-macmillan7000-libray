yawn 
BrE jɔːn, AmE jɔn 
A. intransitive verb 
① (open mouth) 打哈欠 dǎ hāqian 
② figurative (gape) «chasm, abyss, crater, gap» 裂开 lièkāi 
B.  transitive verb 打着哈欠说 dǎzhe hāqian shuō 
C.  noun 
① (action) 哈欠 hāqian to give a yawn 打哈欠 dǎ hāqian 
② figurative (bore) 乏味的事情 fáwèi de shìqing the meeting was a yawn 这个会议无聊透顶

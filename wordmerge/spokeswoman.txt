spokeswoman 
ˈspōksˌwo͝omən

▶noun (plural spokeswomen) a woman who makes statements on behalf of a group or individual: a spokeswoman for the agency said it would not be investigating the case. ORIGIN mid 17th century: from spoke2 + woman, after spokesman.

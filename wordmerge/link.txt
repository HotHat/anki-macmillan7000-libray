link 
▶noun
1 a chain of steel links: loop, ring, connection, connector, coupling, joint.
2 the links between transport and the environment: connection, relationship, association, linkage, tie-up.
3 their links with the labor movement: bond, tie, attachment, connection, relationship, association, affiliation.
4 he was an important link in our operation: component, constituent, element, part, piece.
▶verb
1 four boxes were linked together: join, connect, fasten, attach, bind, unite, combine, amalgamate; clamp, secure, fix, tie, couple, yoke, hitch.
2 the evidence linking him with the murder: associate, connect, relate, join, bracket.

sheet 
BrE ʃiːt, AmE ʃit 
A. noun 
① (for bed) 床单 chuángdān single/double sheets 单人/双人床单 linen/cotton sheets 亚麻/棉布床单 to be as white as a sheet 面无血色 
② (of paper, metal) 薄片 báopiàn a sheet of plastic 一块塑料布 a blank sheet of paper 一张白纸 a sheet of stamps 一版邮票 
③ (of water, ice) 大片 dàpiàn the fields were covered in a solid sheet of water 田野被一大片水淹没了 the oil tank exploded in a sheet of flames 油罐在一片火海中爆炸了 the rain came down in sheets 大雨倾盆而下 
④ (sail) 帆 fān two or three sheets to the wind colloquial 醉得东倒西歪 
B.  intransitive verb to sheet (down) «rain» 倾盆而下 qīng pén ér xià to sheet against [something]; 对着某物倾泻 duìzhe mǒu wù qīngxiè the water was sheeting down off the steep roof 水从尖屋顶上倾泻而下 
sheet 
▶noun
1 (often sheets) she changed the sheets: bed linen, linen, bedclothes.
2 a sheet of ice: layer, stratum, covering, blanket, coating, coat, film, skin.
3 a sheet of glass: pane, panel, piece, plate; slab.
4 she put a fresh sheet in the typewriter: piece of paper, leaf, page, folio.
5 a sheet of water: expanse, area, stretch, sweep.

anyhow 
▶adverb
1 anyhow, it doesn't really matter: anyway, in any case, in any event, at any rate; however, be that as it may, regardless; informal still and all, anyways.
2 her clothes were strewn about anyhow: haphazardly, carelessly, heedlessly, negligently, in a muddle; informal all over the place, every which way.

graphics 
ˈɡrafiks

▶pl. noun [usually treated as singular]
1 the products of the graphic arts, especially commercial design or illustration.
2 the use of diagrams in calculation and design.
3 (also computer graphics) [treated as plural] visual images produced by computer processing. 
• [treated as singular] the use of computers linked to display screens to generate and manipulate visual images.

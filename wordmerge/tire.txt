tire 
▶verb
1 he began to tire as the ascent grew steeper: weaken, grow weak, flag, wilt, droop; deteriorate.
2 the journey had tired her: fatigue, tire out, exhaust, wear out, drain, weary, frazzle, overtire, enervate; informal knock out, do in, wear to a frazzle.
3 we are tired of your difficult behavior: weary of, get fed up with, get sick of, get bored with, get impatient with; informal have had it up to here with, have had enough of.

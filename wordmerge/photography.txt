photography 
fəˈtäɡrəfē

▶noun the art or practice of taking and processing photographs. Modern photography is based on the property of silver compounds decomposing to metallic silver when exposed to light. The light-sensitive salts are held in an emulsion (in color film, layers of emulsion) usually mounted on transparent roll film.

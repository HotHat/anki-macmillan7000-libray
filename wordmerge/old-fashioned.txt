old-fashioned 
ˌōldˈfaSHənd

▶adjective in or according to styles or types no longer current or common; not modern: an old-fashioned kitchen range. 
• (of a person or their views) favoring traditional and usually restrictive styles, ideas, or customs: she's stuffy and old-fashioned. 
▶noun North American a cocktail consisting chiefly of whiskey, bitters, water, and sugar. DERIVATIVES old-fashionedness noun

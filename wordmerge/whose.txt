whose 
BrE huːz, AmE huz 
A. pronoun 谁的 shuí de whose is that? 那个是谁的？ I'm not sure whose he'll prefer 我拿不准他会喜欢谁的 
B.  adjective 
① (in direct or indirect questions) 谁的 shuí de whose side are you on? 你赞成谁的观点？ it doesn't matter whose fault it is 这是谁的错无关紧要 
② (of that person) 那个人的 nàge rén de (of those people) 那些人的 nàxiē rén de (of that thing) 那一个的 nà yī gè de (of those things) 那一些的 nà yīxiē de the ones whose names are drawn first 最先抽到名字的那些人 the house whose door is painted red 那所门被刷成红色的房子 Internet sites whose content is not suitable for children 那些含儿童不宜内容的互联网站 
③ (adding to preceding noun) [进一步提供先行词的信息]Isobel, whose brother he was, … 伊索贝尔，就是他的兄弟，…

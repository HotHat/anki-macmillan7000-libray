duck 
▶verb
1 he ducked behind the wall: bob down, bend (down), stoop (down), crouch (down), squat (down), hunch down, hunker down; cower, cringe.
2 she was ducked in the river: dip, dunk, plunge, immerse, submerge, lower, sink.
3 informal they cannot duck the issue forever: shirk, dodge, evade, avoid, elude, escape, back out of, shun, eschew, sidestep, bypass, circumvent; informal cop out of, get out of, wriggle out of, dipsy-doodle around.

defend

① (protect, guard) «armed forces» 保卫 bǎowèi ‹country, territory›; «person, animal» 保护 bǎohù ‹person, young›to defend against or from [something]; 抵御某事物 
② (support) 捍卫 hànwèi ‹freedom, interests, rights›; 为…辩解 wèi… biànjiě ‹action, decision›

④ Sport (protect) «player, goalkeeper» 防守 fángshǒu ‹goal›
⑤ (in contest) 保住 bǎozhù ‹championship, record›to successfully defend one's title 成功卫冕 the government is fighting to defend its majority 内阁力争保住自己多数党的地位

defend 
▶verb
1 a fort built to defend the border: protect, guard, safeguard, secure, shield; fortify, garrison, barricade; uphold, support, watch over. ANTONYMS attack.
2 he defended his policy: justify, vindicate, argue for, support, make a case for, plead for; excuse, explain. ANTONYMS attack, criticize.
3 the manager defended his players: support, back, stand by, stick up for, stand up for, argue for, champion, endorse; informal throw one's weight behind. ANTONYMS criticize.

▶noun

2 sympathy with a fellow journalist: rapport, fellow feeling, affinity, empathy, harmony, accord, compatibility; fellowship, camaraderie. ANTONYMS hostility.
3 their sympathy with the rebels: agreement, favor, approval, approbation, support, encouragement, partiality; association, alignment, affiliation. ANTONYMS disapproval.

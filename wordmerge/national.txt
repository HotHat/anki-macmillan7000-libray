national 
ˈnaSH(ə)n(ə)l

▶adjective relating to a nation; common to or characteristic of a whole nation: this policy may have been in the national interest 
a national newspaper. 
• owned, controlled, or financially supported by the federal government: plans for a national art library. 
▶noun
1 a citizen of a particular country, typically entitled to hold that country's passport: a German national 
the new law on foreign nationals.
2 (usually nationals) a nationwide competition or tournament: she finished 16th at the nationals that year. ORIGIN late 16th century: from French, from Latin natio(n-) ‘birth, race of people’ (see nation) .

giggle 
BrE ˈɡɪɡ(ə)l, AmE ˈɡɪɡəl 

A.  intransitive verb 吃吃傻笑 chīchī shǎxiào to giggle at [somebody]/[something]; 对某人/某事物傻笑 
B.  noun 
① (laugh) 吃吃傻笑 chīchī shǎxiào to give a nervous giggle 发出紧张的傻笑 
② BrE colloquial (joke) 玩笑 wánxiào to do [something] for a giggle; 为了搞笑而做某事 we had a good giggle 我们玩得很开心 
③ the giggles (continuous giggling) 咯咯的笑 gēgē de xiào to get the giggles; 发出咯咯的笑声 fāchū gēgē de xiàoshēng to have a fit of the giggles 咯咯笑个不停 gēgē xiào gè bù tíng

helmet 
ˈhelmət

▶noun
1 a hard or padded protective hat, various types of which are worn by soldiers, police officers, firefighters, motorcyclists, athletes, and others.firefighter's helmet
2 Botany the arched upper part (galea) of the corolla in some flowers, especially those of the mint and orchid families.
3 (also helmet shell) a predatory mollusk with a squat heavy shell, living in tropical and temperate seas and preying chiefly on sea urchins. [Family Cassidae, class Gastropoda.] DERIVATIVES helmeted 
ˈhelmədəd 
adjectiveORIGIN late Middle English: from Old French, diminutive of helme, of Germanic origin; related to helm2 .

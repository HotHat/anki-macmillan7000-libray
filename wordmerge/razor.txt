razor 
ˈrāzər

▶noun an instrument with a sharp blade or combination of blades, used to remove unwanted hair from the face or body. 
▶verb [with object] cut with a razor. ORIGIN Middle English: from Old French rasor, from raser ‘shave closely’ (see raze) .

sharply 
BrE ˈʃɑːpli, AmE ˈʃɑrpli 
adverb 
① (abruptly) 突然 tūrán ‹turn, stop, rise, fall›; (by large degree) 突然大幅度地 tūrán dàfúdù de ‹rise, fall, fluctuating›
② (harshly) 尖刻地 jiānkè de ‹speak, retort›
③ (distinctly) 鲜明地 xiānmíng de ‹stand out, contrast, differing›to bring [something] sharply into focus literal 使某事物变清晰 figurative 使某事物成为焦点 shǐ mǒu shìwù chéngwéi jiāodiǎn 
④ (perceptively) 机敏地 jīmǐn de ‹say, look at, aware›to be sharply intelligent 绝顶聪明 
⑤ (quickly and suddenly) 猛地 měng de ‹move, look up, knock›

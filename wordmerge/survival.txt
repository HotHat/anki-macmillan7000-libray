survival 
BrE səˈvʌɪv(ə)l, AmE sərˈvaɪvəl 
noun 
① Uncountable(continued existence) (of person, animal, plant, project, government) 存留 cúnliú (of custom, belief) 遗存 yícún the survival of the fittest 适者生存 what are his chances of survival? 他存活的可能性有多大？ before noun 救生的 jiùshēng de ‹equipment, training, expert›
② Countable(relic) (of custom, practice) 残存物 cáncúnwù (of object) 幸存物 xìngcúnwù (person) 幸存者 xìngcúnzhě to be a survival from [something]; 是…的幸存者

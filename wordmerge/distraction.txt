distraction 
▶noun
1 a distraction from the real issues: diversion, interruption, disturbance, interference, hindrance.
2 frivolous distractions: amusement, entertainment, diversion, recreation, leisure pursuit, divertissement.
3 he was driven to distraction: frenzy, hysteria, mental distress, madness, insanity, mania; agitation, perturbation.

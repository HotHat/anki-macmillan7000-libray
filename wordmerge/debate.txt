debate 
BrE dɪˈbeɪt, AmE dəˈbeɪt 

A.  noun 
① (formal discussion) 辩论 biànlùn to hold a debate on or about [something]; 就某事物进行辩论 
② (argument) 讨论 tǎolùn to be under debate; 在讨论中 to be open to debate 有待讨论 a debate on or about [something]; 有关某事物的争论 
B.  transitive verb 
① (discuss formally) «government, committee» 辩论 biànlùn ‹policy, proposal›
② (discuss informally) 讨论 tǎolùn to debate the pros and cons of a situation 讨论某情况的利弊 
C.  intransitive verb 考虑 kǎolǜ to debate about [something]/whether to do [something]; 盘算某事/是否做某事 
debate 
▶noun a debate on the reforms: discussion, discourse, parley, dialogue; argument, counterargument, dispute, wrangle, war of words; argumentation, disputation, dissension, disagreement, contention, conflict; negotiations, talks; informal confab, powwow.
▶verb
1 they will debate the future of rail transport: discuss, talk over/through, talk about, thrash out, hash out, argue, dispute; informal kick around, bat around.
2 he debated whether to call her: consider, think over/about, chew over, mull over, ponder, revolve, deliberate, contemplate, muse, meditate; formal cogitate.

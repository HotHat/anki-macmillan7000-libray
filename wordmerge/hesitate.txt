hesitate 
ˈhezəˌtāt

▶verb [no object] pause before saying or doing something, especially through uncertainty: she hesitated, unsure of what to say. 
• [with infinitive] be reluctant to do something: he hesitated to spoil the mood by being inquisitive. 
PHRASES
he who hesitates is lost proverb delay or vacillation may have unfortunate or disastrous consequences.DERIVATIVES hesitater

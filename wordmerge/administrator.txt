administrator 
ədˈminəˌstrādər

▶noun a person responsible for running a business, organization, etc. 
• Law a person legally appointed to manage and dispose of the estate of an intestate, deceased person, debtor, or other individual, or of an insolvent company. 
• a person who performs official duties in some sphere, especially dealing out punishment or giving a religious sacrament: administrators of justice.

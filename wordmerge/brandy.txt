brandy 
ˈbrandē

▶noun (plural brandies) a strong alcoholic spirit distilled from wine or fermented fruit juice. ORIGIN mid 17th century: from earlier brandwine, brandewine, from Dutch brandewijn, from branden ‘burn, distill’ + wijn ‘wine.’

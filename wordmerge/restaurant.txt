restaurant 
ˈrest(ə)rənt ˈrest(ə)ˌränt

▶noun a place where people pay to sit and eat meals that are cooked and served on the premises. ORIGIN early 19th century: from French, from restaurer ‘provide food for’ (literally ‘restore to a former state’).

clumsy 
▶adjective
1 she was terribly clumsy: awkward, uncoordinated, ungainly, graceless, inelegant; inept, maladroit, unskillful, unhandy, accident-prone, like a bull in a china shop, all thumbs; informal ham-fisted, butterfingered, having two left feet, klutzy. ANTONYMS graceful.
2 a clumsy contraption: unwieldy, cumbersome, bulky, awkward. ANTONYMS elegant.
3 a clumsy remark: gauche, awkward, graceless; unsubtle, uncouth, boorish, crass; tactless, insensitive, thoughtless, undiplomatic, indelicate, ill-judged. ANTONYMS tactful.

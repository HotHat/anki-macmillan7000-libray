motivation 
BrE məʊtɪˈveɪʃ(ə)n, AmE ˌmoʊdəˈveɪʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Countable(motive) 动机 dòngjī the motivation for/behind [something]; 某事/某事背后的动机 
② Uncountable(enthusiasm, drive) 积极性 jījíxìng the motivation to do [something]; 做某事的积极性 
motivation 
ˌmōdəˈvāSH(ə)n

▶noun the reason or reasons one has for acting or behaving in a particular way: escape can be a strong motivation for travel. 
• the general desire or willingness of someone to do something: keep staff up to date and maintain interest and motivation. ORIGIN late 19th century: from motive, reinforced by motivate .

bus 
BrE bʌs, AmE bəs 

A.  noun(plural buses; AmE also busses) 
① (passenger vehicle) 公共汽车 gōnggòng qìchē by bus 乘公共汽车 on the bus 在公共汽车上 to get on/off a bus 上/下公共汽车 to catch or take a bus (to somewhere) 搭乘公共汽车（去某地） to miss the bus figurative 错过机会 
② colloquial dated (old vehicle) 老爷车 lǎoyechē 
③ Computing 总线 zǒngxiàn 
B.  transitive verb (present participle etc. -ss- or -s-) BrE (transport by bus) 用公共汽车运送 yòng gōnggòng qìchē yùnsòng ; AmE (transport to school) 用校车送 yòng xiàochē sòng we were bussed from the airport to our hotel 公共汽车把我们从机场拉到了旅馆 
C.  intransitive verb(present participle etc. -ss- or -s-) 
① colloquial (travel by bus) 乘公共汽车旅行 chéng gōnggòng qìchē lǚxíng to bus back/to work 乘公共汽车回去/上班 
② AmE (clear tables in restaurant) 收盘子 shōu pánzi

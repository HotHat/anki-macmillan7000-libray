surround 
BrE səˈraʊnd, AmE səˈraʊnd 

A.  transitive verb 
① (be all round) «fence, hills» 环绕 huánrào to be surrounded by or with [something]; 被某物环绕 I'm surrounded by books at home 我家里到处都是书 
② (move into position round) «police, troops» 包围 bāowéi 
③ (be associated with) «rumour, doubt» 围绕 wéirào ‹plan, future›to be surrounded by [something]; 充满某物 
B.  reflexive verb to surround oneself with [something]; 喜欢身边总有某物 xǐhuan shēnbiān zǒng yǒu mǒu wù 
C.  noun (of fireplace, door, window) 边 biān ; (of painting, quilt) 饰边 shìbiān 
D.  surrounds noun plural 周围地区 zhōuwéi dìqū

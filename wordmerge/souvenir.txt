souvenir 
BrE ˌsuːvəˈnɪə, AmE ˌsuvəˈnɪr 
noun 纪念品 jìniànpǐn a souvenir of [something] 某事物的纪念 
souvenir 
ˌso͞ovəˈnir

▶noun a thing that is kept as a reminder of a person, place, or event. 
▶verb [with object] informal take as a memento: many parts of the aircraft have been souvenired. ORIGIN late 18th century: from French, from souvenir ‘remember,’ from Latin subvenire ‘occur to the mind.’

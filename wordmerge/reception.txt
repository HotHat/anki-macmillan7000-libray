reception 
BrE rɪˈsɛpʃ(ə)n, AmE rəˈsɛpʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Uncountable(formal welcoming) 欢迎 huānyíng 
② Uncountable(admission) 接纳 jiēnà her reception into the Church 她的加入基督教 
③ Countable(response) 接待 jiēdài to get or be given a favourable/hostile reception 受到友好的/怀有敌意的接待 the film got a favourable reception 影片收到良好的反响 they gave us a great reception 他们热情地接待了我们 
④ Countable(area in hotel, office) 接待处 jiēdàichù 
⑤ Countable(gathering, social occasion) 招待会 zhāodàihuì an official/a wedding reception 正式招待会/婚宴 to attend a reception 出席招待会 to give/hold/host a reception 举行/举办/主持招待会 a reception for [somebody] 为某人举办的招待会 
⑥ Uncountable(of signal) 接收 jiēshōu good/strong/poor/weak reception 接收信号好/强/差/弱 
reception 
▶noun
1 the reception of the goods: receipt, receiving, getting.
2 the reception of foreign diplomats: greeting, welcoming, entertaining.
3 a chilly reception: response, reaction, treatment.
4 a wedding reception: (formal) party, function, social occasion, after-party, soirée, fête, levee; informal do, bash.

resemblance 
rəˈzembləns

▶noun the state of resembling or being alike: they bear some resemblance to Italian figurines. 
• a way in which two or more things are alike: the physical resemblances between humans and apes. ORIGIN Middle English: from Anglo-Norman French, from the verb resembler (see resemble) .

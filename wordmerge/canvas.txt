canvas 
BrE ˈkanvəs, AmE ˈkænvəs 
noun 
① Uncountable(cloth) 帆布 fānbù under canvas (in a tent) 在帐篷里 (under sail) 扬着帆 
② Uncountable and countable(for art) 油画布 yóuhuàbù 
③ Countable(oil painting) 油画 yóuhuà 
④ Uncountable(in boxing) the canvas 地板 dìbǎn to put one's opponent on the canvas 将对手击倒在地 jiāng duìshǒu jīdǎo zài dì

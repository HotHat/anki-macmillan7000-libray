criticism 
▶noun
1 she was stung by his criticism: censure, condemnation, denunciation, disapproval, disparagement, opprobrium, fault-finding, attack, broadside, stricture, recrimination; informal flak, bad press, panning, put down, knock, slam, brickbats, potshot(s); formal excoriation.
2 literary criticism: evaluation, assessment, appraisal, analysis, judgment; commentary, interpretation, explanation, explication, elucidation.QUOTE People ask you for criticism, but they only want praise.
W.  Somerset Maugham Of Human Bondage (1915)

oneself 
ˌwənˈself 
(also one's self) 
▶pronoun [third person singular]
1 [reflexive] a person's own self: it is difficult to wrest oneself away 
resolves that one makes to oneself.
2 [emphatic] used to emphasize that one does something individually or unaided: the idea of publishing a book oneself.
3 in one's normal and individual state of body or mind; not influenced by others: freedom to be oneself.

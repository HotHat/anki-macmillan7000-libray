ball 
▶noun
1 a ball of dough: sphere, globe, orb, globule, spherule, spheroid, ovoid.
2 a musket ball: bullet, pellet, slug, projectile.
3 a costume ball: dance, dinner dance, masked ball, masquerade, formal, prom, baile; informal hop, bop.
4 everyone had a ball: good time, blast, riot.

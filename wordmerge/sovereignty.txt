sovereignty 
ˈsäv(ə)rən(t)ē

▶noun (plural sovereignties) supreme power or authority: how can we hope to wrest sovereignty away from the oligarchy and back to the people? 
• the authority of a state to govern itself or another state: national sovereignty. 
• a self-governing state. ORIGIN late Middle English: from Old French sovereinete, from soverain (see sovereign) .

pause 
BrE pɔːz, AmE pɔz 

A.  noun 
① (brief silence, break, stoppage) 暂停 zàntíng a pause in the conversation 谈话中的停顿 she spoke for an hour without a pause 她一直不停地讲了一个小时 there was a ten-minute pause in production 生产停顿了10分钟 
② to give [somebody] pause (for thought) (make sb. think) 使某人认真考虑 shǐ mǒu rén rènzhēn kǎolǜ (make sb. hesitate) 使某人犹豫 shǐ mǒu rén yóuyù 
B.  intransitive verb 
① (stop) 暂停 zàntíng she paused and then went on in a quiet voice 她停顿了一下，然后平静地继续说 to pause in [something]; 暂停做某事 to pause for breath 停下来歇口气 
② (hesitate, wait) 犹豫 yóuyù to pause over [something]; 在某物前驻足 
pause 
▶noun a pause in the conversation: stop, cessation, break, halt, interruption, check, lull, respite, breathing space, discontinuation, hiatus, gap, interlude; adjournment, suspension, rest, wait, hesitation; informal letup, breather.
▶verb Hannah paused for a moment: stop, cease, halt, discontinue, break off, take a break; adjourn, rest, wait, hesitate, falter, waver; informal take a breather, take five.

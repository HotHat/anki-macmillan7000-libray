cradle

A.  noun 
① (for baby) 摇篮 yáolán from the cradle to the grave 一生 the cradle figurative 婴儿时期 the hand that rocks the cradle rules the world 晃动摇篮之手掌握未来世界 ［指母亲对孩子的成长影响巨大］
② figurative (origin) 发源地 fāyuándì the cradle of [something]; 某事物的发源地 the cradle of civilization 文明的发祥地 
③ (framework) Nautical [修造船只用的] 托架 tuōjià Medicine [使床单不碰到受伤肢体的] 支架 zhījià 
④ (telephone rest) 听筒架 tīngtǒngjià 
⑤ (platform) [空中作业时用的] 吊篮 diàolán 
B.  transitive verb 轻轻抱着 qīngqīng bàozhe to cradle [somebody]/[something] in one's arms 把某人/某物抱在怀里

sticky 
▶adjective
1 sticky tape: (self-)adhesive, gummed, self-stick; technical adherent.
2 sticky clay: glutinous, viscous, viscid, gluey, tacky, gummy, treacly, syrupy; mucilaginous; informal gooey, icky, gloppy. ANTONYMS dry.
3 sticky weather: humid, muggy, close, sultry, steamy, sweaty, oppressive, heavy. ANTONYMS fresh, cool.
4 a sticky situation: awkward, difficult, tricky, ticklish, problematic, delicate, touch-and-go, touchy, embarrassing, sensitive, uncomfortable; informal hairy. ANTONYMS easy.

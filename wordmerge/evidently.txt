evidently 
ˈevədəntlē

▶adverb
1 plainly or obviously; in a way that is clearly seen or understood: a work so evidently laden with significance.
2 [sentence adverb] it is plain that; it would seem that: evidently Mrs. Smith thought differently. 
• used as an affirmative response or reply: “Were they old pals or something?” “Evidently.”

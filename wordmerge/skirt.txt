skirt 
▶verb
1 he skirted the city: go around, walk around, circle.
2 the fields that skirt the highway: border, edge, flank, line, lie alongside.
3 he carefully skirted the subject: avoid, evade, sidestep, dodge, pass over, gloss over; informal duck.

capital
1 
BrE ˈkapɪt(ə)l, AmE ˈkæpədl 

A.  noun 
① Countable(letter) 大写字母 dàxiě zìmǔ with a capital (A, B etc.) figurative 确确实实 she's crazy with a capital C 她确实发疯了 
② Countable(of country) 首都 shǒudū (of state) 首府 shǒufǔ (of province) 省会 shěnghuì 
③ Countable(leader) 重要都市 zhòngyào dūshì the fashion capital of the world 世界时装之都 
④ Uncountable and countable(money, wealth) 资本 zīběn a starting capital of £50,000 5万英镑的启动资金 
⑤ Uncountable(capitalist interests) 资方 zīfāng capital and labour 劳资双方 
⑥ Uncountable(profit, advantage) 好处 hǎochu to make capital out of [something] 利用某事物谋私利 to make political capital out of [something] 从某事物中捞取政治资本 
B.  adjective 
① Law 可处死刑的 kě chǔ sǐxíng de a capital crime or offence 死罪 to carry a capital sentence 可判处死刑 
② (essential) 主要的 zhǔyào de to be of capital importance 至关重要

darling 
ˈdärliNG

▶noun used as an affectionate form of address to a beloved person: good night, my darling. 
• a lovable or endearing person: he's such a darling. 
• a person who is particularly popular with a certain group: she is the darling of the media. 
▶adjective [attributive] beloved: his darling wife. 
• (especially in affected use) pretty; charming: a darling little pillbox hat. ORIGIN Old English dēorling (see dear, -ling) .

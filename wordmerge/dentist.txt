dentist 
ˈden(t)əst

▶noun a person qualified to treat the diseases and conditions that affect the teeth and gums, especially the repair and extraction of teeth and the insertion of artificial ones. ORIGIN mid 18th century: from French dentiste, from dent ‘tooth,’ from Latin dens, dent- .

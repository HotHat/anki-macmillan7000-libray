zoo 
zo͞o

▶noun an establishment that maintains a collection of wild animals, typically in a park or gardens, for study, conservation, or display to the public. 
• informal a situation characterized by confusion and disorder: it's a zoo in the lobby. ORIGIN mid 19th century: abbreviation of zoological garden, originally applied specifically to that of Regent's Park, London.

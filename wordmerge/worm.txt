worm 
BrE wəːm, AmE wərm 
A. noun 
① (invertebrate) 蠕虫 rúchóng [尤指蚯蚓]the worm will turn figurative 老实人被逼急了也会反抗 
② (insect larva) 幼虫 yòuchóng this apple is full of worms 这个苹果里满是蛀虫 
③ colloquial (wretch) 懦夫 nuòfū 
④ usually singular Technology (endless screw) 蜗杆 wōgǎn (coiled pipe) 螺旋管 luóxuánguǎn 
⑤ usually singular Computing (virus) 蠕虫病毒 rúchóng bìngdú 
B.  worms noun plural Veterinary medicine, Medicine 肠虫 chángchóng to have/get worms 有/生肠虫 
C.  transitive verb 
① (wriggle) to worm one's way 蠕动 rúdòng to worm one's way into [somebody's] heart/confidence figurative 慢慢赢得某人的心/信任 mànmàn yíngdé mǒu rén de xīn/xìnrèn 
② (extract) 刺探 cìtàn to worm a secret/the truth out of [somebody]; 从某人嘴里套出秘密/实情 
③ Veterinary medicine 给…除肠虫 gěi… chú chángchóng ‹animal›

arithmetic 
▶noun 
əˈriTHməˌtik 
the branch of mathematics dealing with the properties and manipulation of numbers: the laws of arithmetic. 
• the use of numbers in counting and calculation: he could do arithmetic in his head. 
▶adjective 
ˌeriTHˈmedik

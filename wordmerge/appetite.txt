appetite 
BrE ˈapɪtʌɪt, AmE ˈæpəˌtaɪt 
noun 
① literal 食欲 shíyù he has a good/poor appetite 他食欲旺盛/不振 to work up an appetite 激起食欲 it'll spoil or take away your appetite 那会让你倒胃口 
② figurative 欲望 yùwàng he has no appetite for that sort of work 他对那样的工作没兴趣 
appetite 
▶noun
1 a walk sharpens the appetite: hunger, ravenousness, hungriness; taste, palate.
2 my appetite for learning: craving, longing, yearning, hankering, hunger, thirst, passion; enthusiasm, keenness, eagerness, desire; informal yen.

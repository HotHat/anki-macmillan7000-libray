noun

② Countable(quarrel) 争吵 zhēngchǎo a disagreement between two people (on or about [something]); 两个人之间（为某事）的争吵 
③ Uncountable(inconsistency) 不符 bùfú disagreement about [something]/between two things; 在某事上/两件事之间的不一致

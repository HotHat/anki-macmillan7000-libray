remark 
▶verb
1 “You're quiet,” he remarked: comment, say, observe, mention, reflect, state, declare, announce, pronounce, assert; formal opine.
2 many critics remarked on their rapport: comment on, mention, refer to, speak of, pass comment on.
3 she remarked the absence of policemen: note, notice, observe, take note of, perceive, discern.
▶noun
1 his remarks have been misinterpreted: comment, statement, utterance, observation, declaration, pronouncement.
2 worthy of remark: attention, notice, comment, mention, observation, acknowledgment.

businessman 
ˈbiznisˌman ˈbiznisˌmən

▶noun (plural businessmen) a man who works in business or commerce, especially at an executive level. 
• [with adjective] a person with a specified level of skill in financial matters: his knowledge and talent were never in question, but he was a poor businessman.

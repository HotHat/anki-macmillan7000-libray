sharpen 
BrE ˈʃɑːp(ə)n, AmE ˈʃɑrpən

② (make clearer) 使…更清晰 shǐ… gèng qīngxī ‹image, outline›we need to sharpen the focus of the discussion 我们要使讨论的重点更明确

④ (raise pitch) 使…升高 shǐ… shēnggāo ‹note, pitch›
B.  intransitive verb 
① (become piercing) «voice, tone» 变尖 biànjiān 
② (intensify) «pain, tension» 加剧 jiājù PHRASAL VERB sharpen up

cargo 
ˈkärɡō

▶noun (plural cargoes or cargos) goods carried on a ship, aircraft, or motor vehicle: transportation of bulk cargo 
a cargo of oil. ORIGIN mid 17th century: from Spanish cargo, carga, from late Latin carricare, carcare ‘to load,’ from Latin carrus ‘wheeled vehicle.’

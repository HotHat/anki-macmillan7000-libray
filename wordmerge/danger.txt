danger 
▶noun
1 an element of danger: peril, hazard, risk, jeopardy; perilousness, riskiness, precariousness, uncertainty, instability, insecurity. ANTONYMS safety.
2 that car is a danger on the roads: menace, hazard, threat, risk; informal death trap, widow-maker.
3 a serious danger of fire: possibility, chance, risk, probability, likelihood, fear, prospect.

loop 
BrE luːp, AmE lup 

A.  noun 
① (circular shape) 环形 huánxíng he attached a loop of wire to the post 他把导线绕在柱子上 to be in/out of the loop figurative colloquial 属圈内/圈外人士 to keep somebody out of/in the loop figurative colloquial 不让/让某人知情 
② (of plane) 筋斗 jīndǒu to loop the loop 翻筋斗特技飞行 
③ (repeat of film) 循环电影胶片 xúnhuán diànyǐng jiāopiàn ; (repeat of tape) 循环音像磁带 xúnhuán yīnxiàng cídài 
④ (closed circuit) 回路 huílù 
⑤ (in computer programming) 循环 xúnhuán 
⑥ (contraceptive) 节育环 jiéyùhuán 
B.  transitive verb 使成环 shǐ chénghuán to loop [something] around [something] 用某物环绕某物 
C.  intransitive verb 成环形 chénghuánxíng the river loops across the plain 那条河盘绕着流过平原 
loop 
▶noun a loop of rope: coil, hoop, ring, circle, noose, oval, spiral, curl, bend, curve, arc, twirl, whorl, twist, hook, helix, convolution.
▶verb
1 Dave looped rope around their hands: coil, wind, twist, snake, wreathe, spiral, curve, bend, turn.
2 he looped the cables together: fasten, tie, join, connect, knot, bind.

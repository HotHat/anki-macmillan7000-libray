overseas 
AmE ˌoʊvərˈsiz 
A. adverb 在国外 zài guówài ‹work, retire›from overseas 来自海外 
B.  adjectiveattributive 
① (from abroad) 外国的 wàiguó de ‹visitor, investor, company›
② (in other countries) 在国外的 zài guówài de ‹investment›; (to other countries) 去国外的 qù guówài de ‹travel›to get an overseas posting 得到一个去海外工作的职务

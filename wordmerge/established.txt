established 
əˈstabliSHt

▶adjective
1 having been in existence for a long time and therefore recognized and generally accepted: the ceremony was an established event in the annual calendar

• (of a plant) having taken root; growing well.
2 (of a church or religion) recognized by the government as the national church or religion.

sparkle 
BrE ˈspɑːk(ə)l, AmE ˈspɑrk(ə)l 

A.  intransitive verb 
① (flash, shine brightly) «flame, firework, eyes» 发光 fāguāng ; «jewellery, tinsel, snow» 闪耀 shǎnyào to sparkle with … figurative 因…而闪闪发光 ‹happiness, wit›
② (be vivacious) 生动活泼 shēngdòng huópo ; (be witty) 富于机智 fùyú jīzhì 
③ (effervesce) 发泡 fāpào (fizz) 发嘶嘶声 fā sīsī shēng the champagne sparkled in the glasses 香槟酒在玻璃杯里泛着气泡 
B.  noun 
① Uncountable and countable(flash of light) 闪光 shǎnguāng to add sparkle to [something] 使某物发光 there was a sparkle of mischief/excitement in his eyes 他的眼睛里闪着调皮/激动的光芒 
② Uncountable(vivacity) 活力 huólì (wit) 才智 cáizhì to lose one's sparkle 失去活力 to add some sparkle to one's life 给生活增添一些激情 
sparkle 
▶verb
1 her earrings sparkled: glitter, glint, glisten, twinkle, flash, blink, wink, shimmer, shine, gleam; literary coruscate, glister.
2 she sparkled as the hostess: be lively, be vivacious, be animated, be ebullient, be exuberant, be bubbly, be effervescent, be witty, be full of life.
▶noun the sparkle of the pool: glitter, glint, twinkle, flicker, shimmer, flash, shine, gleam; literary coruscation.

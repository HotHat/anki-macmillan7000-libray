A.  noun 
① (idea) 模式化观念 móshìhuà guānniàn (person) 模式化的人 móshìhuà de rén to perpetuate or conform to a stereotype 墨守陈规 sexual/racial stereotypes 有关性/种族的旧框框 
② Printing 铅版 qiānbǎn

stereotype 
▶noun the stereotype of the rancher: standard/conventional image, received idea, cliché, hackneyed idea, formula.
▶verb women in detective novels are often stereotyped as femmes fatales: typecast, pigeonhole, conventionalize, categorize, label, tag.

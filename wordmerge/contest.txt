② (struggle) 争夺 zhēngduó (in election) 竞争 jìngzhēng a leadership contest 领导权的争夺 the presidential contest 总统竞选 
B.  /BrE kənˈtɛst, AmE kənˈtɛst, ˈkɑnˌtɛst/ transitive verb 
① (object to) 辩驳 biànbó ‹position›; 质疑 zhìyí ‹decision, result›to contest a will 对遗嘱提出质疑

contest 
▶noun
1 a boxing contest: competition, match, tournament, game, meet, event, trial, bout, heat, race; deathmatch.
2 the contest for the party leadership: fight, battle, tussle, struggle, competition, race.
▶verb
1 he intended to contest the seat: compete for, contend for, vie for, fight for, try to win, go for.
2 we contested the decision: oppose, object to, challenge, take a stand against, take issue with, question, call into question.
3 the issues have been hotly contested: debate, argue about, dispute, quarrel over.

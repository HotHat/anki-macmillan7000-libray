poke 
▶verb
1 she poked him in the ribs: prod, jab, dig, nudge, butt, shove, jolt, stab, stick.
2 leave the cable poking out : stick out, jut out, protrude, project, extend.
▶noun give him a poke: prod, jab, dig, elbow, nudge, shove, stab.

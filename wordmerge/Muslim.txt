Muslim 
ˈməzləm ˈmo͝ozləm 
(also Moslem) 
▶noun a follower of the religion of Islam. 
▶adjective relating to the Muslims or their religion. ORIGIN early 17th century: from Arabic, active participle of 'aslama (see Islam) . usage: Muslim is the preferred term for ‘follower of Islam,’ although Moslem is also widely used. The archaic term Muhammadan (or Mohammedan) should be avoided.

appliance 
əˈplīəns

▶noun
1 a device or piece of equipment designed to perform a specific task, typically a domestic one: electrical and gas appliances. 
• an apparatus fitted by a surgeon or a dentist for corrective or therapeutic purpose.
2 British the action or process of bringing something into operation: the appliance of science could increase crop yields.

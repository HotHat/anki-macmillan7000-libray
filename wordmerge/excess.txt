excess 
▶noun
1 an excess of calcium: surplus, surfeit, overabundance, superabundance, superfluity, glut. ANTONYMS lack, dearth.
2 the excess is turned into fat: remainder, rest, residue; leftovers, remnants; surplus, extra, difference.
3 a life of excess: overindulgence, intemperance, immoderation, profligacy, lavishness, extravagance, decadence, self-indulgence, overconsumption. ANTONYMS moderation, restraint.
▶adjective excess skin oils: surplus, superfluous, redundant, unwanted, unneeded, excessive; extra.

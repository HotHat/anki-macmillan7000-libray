rigorous 
ˈriɡ(ə)rəs

▶adjective extremely thorough, exhaustive, or accurate: the rigorous testing of consumer products. 
• (of a rule, system, etc.) strictly applied or adhered to: rigorous controls on mergers. 
• (of a person) adhering strictly or inflexibly to a belief, opinion, or way of doing something: a rigorous teetotaler. 
• harsh and demanding: my exercise regime is a little more rigorous than most 
the rigorous climate in the regions of perpetual snow high in the Himalayas. DERIVATIVES rigorousness

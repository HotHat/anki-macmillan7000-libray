practically 
BrE ˈpraktɪkli, AmE ˈpræktək(ə)li 
adverb 
① (virtually) 几乎 jīhū 
② (rationally, sensibly) 实事求是地 shí shì qiú shì de 
practically 
▶adverb
1 the theater was practically empty: almost, (very) nearly, virtually, just about, all but, more or less, as good as, to all intents and purposes, verging on, bordering on; informal pretty near, pretty well; literary well-nigh.
2 “You can't afford it,” he pointed out practically: realistically, sensibly, reasonably.

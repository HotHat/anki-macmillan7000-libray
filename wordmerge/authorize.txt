authorize 
BrE ˈɔːθərʌɪz, AmE ˈɔθəˌraɪz 
transitive verb 批准 pīzhǔn to authorize [somebody] to do [something]; 授权某人做某事 
authorize 
▶verb
1 they authorized further action: sanction, permit, allow, approve, consent to, assent to; ratify, endorse, validate; informal give the green light to, give the go-ahead to, OK, give the thumbs up to. ANTONYMS forbid.
2 the troops were authorized to fire: empower, mandate, commission; entitle.

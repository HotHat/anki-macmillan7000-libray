aboard 
BrE əˈbɔːd, AmE əˈbɔrd 
A. adverb 在船上/车上/飞机上 zài chuán shang/chē shang/fēijī shang to come or go or step aboard 上船/上车/登机 
B.  preposition 在…上 zài… shang ‹aircraft, train, bus, ship›all aboard! 请上船/上车/登机！

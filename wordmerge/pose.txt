pose 
BrE pəʊz, AmE poʊz 
A. intransitive verb 
① (for photograph, painting) 摆姿势 bǎi zīshì to pose for one's portrait 为画像摆好姿势 to pose for [somebody]; (for photograph) 摆好姿势让某人照相 (for painting) 摆好姿势让某人画像 
② (masquerade) 假装 jiǎzhuāng to pose as [somebody]; 冒充某人 
③ pejorative (posture) 装腔作势 zhuāng qiāng zuò shì 
B.  transitive verb 
① (present) 构成 gòuchéng ‹problem, challenge, threat›
② (ask, put forward) 提出 tíchū ‹question, concept›
③ Art 使摆好姿势 shǐ bǎihǎo zīshì 
C.  noun 
① (position, manner) 姿势 zīshì to adopt a pose 摆出一种姿势 
② pejorative (affected or deceiving behaviour) 故作姿态 gù zuò zītài it's all a pose colloquial 这完全是装腔作势 to strike a pose 装模作样

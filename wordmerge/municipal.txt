municipal 
myo͞oˈnisəpəl

▶adjective relating to a city or town or its governing body: national and municipal elections 
municipal offices. DERIVATIVES municipally adverbORIGIN mid 16th century (originally relating to the internal affairs of a state as distinct from its foreign relations): from Latin municipalis, from municipium ‘free city,’ from municeps, municip- ‘citizen with privileges,’ from munia ‘civic offices’ + capere ‘take.’

floor 
▶noun
1 he sat on the floor: ground, flooring.
2 the second floor: story, level, deck, tier.
▶verb
1 he floored his attacker: knock down, knock over, bring down, fell, prostrate; informal lay out.
2 informal the question floored him: baffle, defeat, confound, perplex, puzzle, mystify; informal beat, flummox, stump, fox.

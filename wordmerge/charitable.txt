charitable 
ˈCHerədəb(ə)l

▶adjective
1 relating to the assistance of those in need: charitable works such as care of the sick. 
• (of an organization or activity) officially recognized as devoted to the assistance of those in need. 
• generous in giving to those in need.
2 apt to judge others leniently or favorably: those who were less charitable called for his resignation. DERIVATIVES charitableness nounORIGIN Middle English (in the sense ‘showing Christian love to God and man’): from Old French, from charite (see charity) .

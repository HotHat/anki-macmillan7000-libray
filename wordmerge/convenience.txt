noun

convenience 
▶noun
1 the convenience of the arrangement: expedience, advantage, propitiousness, timeliness; suitability, appropriateness.
2 for convenience, the handset is wall-mounted: ease of use, usability, usefulness, utility, serviceability, practicality.
3 the kitchen has all the modern conveniences: appliance, device, labor-saving device, gadget; amenity; informal gizmo.

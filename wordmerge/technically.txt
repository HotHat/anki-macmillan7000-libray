technically 
BrE ˈtɛknɪkli, AmE ˈtɛknək(ə)li 
adverb 
① (technologically) 在技术上 zài jìshù shang ‹advanced, backward, feasible›
② (strictly) 严格地 yángé de technically speaking 严格说来 
③ (in technique) 在技巧上 zài jìqiǎo shang ‹bad, brilliant›
technically 
ˈteknək(ə)lē

▶adverb
1 [usually sentence adverb] according to the facts or exact meaning of something; strictly: technically, a nut is a single-seeded fruit.
2 with reference to the technique displayed: a technically brilliant boxing contest.
3 involving or regarding the technology available: technically advanced tools.

ending 
ˈendiNG

▶noun an end or final part of something, especially a period of time, an activity, or a book or movie: the ending of the Cold War. 
• the furthest part or point of something: a nerve ending. 
• the final part of a word, constituting a grammatical inflection or formative element. ORIGIN Old English endung ‘termination, completion’ (see end, -ing1) .

refusal 
BrE rɪˈfjuːz(ə)l, AmE rəˈfjuzəl 
noun 
① Countable and uncountable(in response to order) 拒绝 jùjué his refusal to move/cooperate/hand over the keys 他拒绝搬家/合作/交出钥匙 
② Uncountable and countable(in response to request) 不答应 bù dāying the doctor's refusal to see me 医生拒绝给我看病 
③ Uncountable and countable(in response to offer) 回绝 huíjué our offer was met with a firm refusal 我们的建议被断然回绝了 
④ Uncountable(opportunity to acquire) 优先取舍权 yōuxiān qǔshě quán to give [somebody] first refusal on [something]; 给予某人对某物的优先取舍权 
⑤ CountableRiding 拒跳障碍物 jù tiào zhàng'àiwù

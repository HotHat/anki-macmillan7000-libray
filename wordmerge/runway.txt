runway |ˈrənˌwā|
noun
1 a leveled strip of smooth ground along which aircraft take off and land.
2 North American a raised aisle extending into the audience from a stage, especially as used for fashion shows.
3 an animal run, especially one made by small mammals in grass, under snow, etc.
4 an incline or chute down which something slides or runs.

squadron 
BrE ˈskwɒdrən, AmE ˈskwɑdrən 
noun 
① BrE (air force unit) 空军中队 kōngjūn zhōngduì a fighter squadron 战斗机中队 
② (in cavalry regiment) 骑兵中队 qíbīng zhōngduì ; (in armoured regiment) 装甲兵连 zhuāngjiǎbīnglián 
③ Nautical 海军中队 hǎijūn zhōngduì 
④ figurative colloquial (large group) 一大群 yī dà qún

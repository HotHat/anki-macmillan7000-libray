yacht 
yät

▶noun a medium-sized sailboat equipped for cruising or racing. 
• [with modifier] a powered boat or small ship equipped for cruising, typically for private or official use: a steam yacht. 
▶verb [no object] race or cruise in a yacht. ORIGIN mid 16th century: from early modern Dutch jaghte, from jaghtschip ‘fast pirate ship,’ from jag(h)t ‘hunting’ + schip ‘ship.’

screening 
ˈskrēniNG

▶noun
1 a showing of a movie, video, or television program.
2 the evaluation or investigation of something as part of a methodical survey, to assess suitability for a particular role or purpose. 
• the testing of a person or group of people for the presence of a disease or other condition: prenatal screening for Down syndrome.
3 (screenings) refuse separated by sieving grain.

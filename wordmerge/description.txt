description 
dəˈskripSH(ə)n

▶noun
1 a spoken or written representation or account of a person, object, or event: people who had seen him were able to give a description. 
• the action of giving a spoken or written representation or account: teaching by demonstration and description.
2 a sort, kind, or class of people or things: ships of every description. 
PHRASES
beyond description to a great and astonishing extent: his face was swollen beyond description. defy description be so unusual or remarkable as to be impossible to describe: the sheer scale of the Requiem defies description. answer (to) (or fit) the description have the qualities specified.ORIGIN late Middle English: via Old French from Latin descriptio(n-), from describere ‘write down.’

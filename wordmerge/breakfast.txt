breakfast 
BrE ˈbrɛkfəst, AmE ˈbrɛkfəst 
A. noun 早饭 zǎofàn to have or eat breakfast 吃早饭 chī zǎofàn to have or eat [somebody] for breakfast colloquial 对付某人是小菜一碟 
B.  intransitive verb 吃早饭 chī zǎofàn to breakfast on [something] 早餐吃某物

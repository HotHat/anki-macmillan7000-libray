swap 
BrE swɒp, AmE swɑp 

A.  transitive verb(present participle etc. -pp-) 
① (exchange) 交换 jiāohuàn to swap [something] (with [somebody]) for [something] else; 用某物（和某人）交换另一物 will you swap me a chocolate for this piece of cake? 给我把这块蛋糕换成巧克力，好吗？ I wouldn't swap you for anyone else! 我舍不得把你换成别人！ 
② colloquial (make exchange with) 和…交换 hé… jiāohuàn if you like this one better, I'll swap you 如果你更喜欢这个，我和你换 
③ (reverse position of) 对调…的位置 duìdiào… de wèizhi to swap things over or around 把东西对调 
④ (take part in exchange of) 交流 jiāoliú ‹news, information›; 对调 duìdiào ‹jobs, places›to swap phone numbers 交换电话号码 
B.  intransitive verb(present participle etc. -pp-) 交换 jiāohuàn 
C.  noun 
① (exchange) 交换 jiāohuàn 
② colloquial (item exchanged) 交换品 jiāohuànpǐn 
swap 
▶verb
1 I swapped my stereo for some hockey equipment: exchange, trade, barter, interchange, bargain; switch, change, replace.
2 we swapped jokes: bandy, exchange, trade, reciprocate.
▶noun a job swap: exchange, interchange, trade, switch, trade-off, substitution; informal switcheroo.

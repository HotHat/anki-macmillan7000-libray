curl 
▶verb
1 smoke curled up from his cigarette: spiral, coil, wreathe, twirl, swirl; wind, curve, bend, twist, twist and turn, loop, meander, snake, corkscrew, zigzag.
2 Ruth curled her arms around his neck: wind, twine, entwine, wrap.
3 she washed and curled my hair: crimp, perm, wave.
4 they curled up together on the sofa: nestle, snuggle, cuddle.
▶noun
1 the tangled curls of her hair: ringlet, corkscrew, kink, wave.
2 a curl of smoke: spiral, coil, twirl, swirl, twist, corkscrew, curlicue, helix.

simplify 
ˈsimpləˌfī

▶verb (simplifies, simplifying, simplified) [with object] make (something) simpler or easier to do or understand: an overhaul of court procedure to simplify litigation. ORIGIN mid 17th century: from French simplifier, from medieval Latin simplificare, from Latin simplus (see simple) .

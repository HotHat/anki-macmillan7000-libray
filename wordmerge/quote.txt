quote 
BrE kwəʊt, AmE kwoʊt 

A.  transitive verb 
① (cite) 引用 yǐnyòng he quoted a passage from the minister's speech 他引用了部长的一段讲话 to quote Shakespeare 引用莎士比亚的话 don't quote me (on this), but she's pregnant 别说（这话）是我说的──她怀孕了 to quote [somebody] as saying that … 引用某人的话说… 
② (mention) 援引 yuányǐn ‹reference, fact, law›please quote this number in all correspondence 请在所有函件中标明这个编号 to quote [somebody]/[something] as an example 引某人/某事物为例 can you quote me an instance of this happening? 你能否给我举例说明这种事件？ 
③ Busn (give) 报 bào ‹figure, price›they quoted us £600 for repairing or to repair the car 修这辆车他们向我们开价600英镑 
④ Finance (state price of) 报…的价 bào… de jià yesterday the pound was quoted at $1.8285 昨天英镑的报价为1.8285美元 
⑤ Finance (list on stock exchange) 给…挂牌 gěi… guàpái several football clubs are now quoted on the Stock Exchange 已有好几家足球俱乐部在证券交易所挂牌上市 
⑥ (in betting) 报出…的投注赔率 bàochū… de tóuzhù péilǜ they are quoted (at or as) 6-1 它们的投注赔率为6赔1 to quote odds of 3-1 开出3赔1的赔率 
B.  intransitive verb 
① (recite from text) 引用 yǐnyòng he often quotes from the Bible 他经常引用《圣经》中的语句 quote (… unquote) (in dictation) 引号起（…引号止） colloquial (in speaking) 原话起（…原话止） yuánhuà qǐ(… yuánhuà zhǐ) it was quote ‘the hardest decision of my life’unquote 那是，引文开始，“我一生中最难作出的决定”，引文结束 
② Busn (give price) 报价 bàojià to quote for [something]; 为某物报价 
C.  noun 
① (from speech, book) 引文 yǐnwén a quote from the Bible 引自《圣经》的语句 
② (short statement to journalist) 声明 shēngmíng 
③ Busn (estimate) 报价 bàojià their quote for the job was too high 他们对那单活儿开价太高 
④ Finance (for sale or purchase of stock) 牌价 páijià 
D.  quotes noun plural = quotation marks
quote 
▶verb
1 he quoted a sentence from the book: recite, repeat, reproduce, retell, echo, parrot, iterate; take, extract.
2 she quoted one case in which a girl died: cite, mention, refer to, name, instance, specify, identify; relate, recount; allude to, point out, present, offer, advance.
▶noun
1 a Shakespearean quote. See quotation.
2 a quote from the contractor: estimate, price, bid, costing, charge, figure, tender.

hypothetical 
ˌhīpəˈTHedək(ə)l

▶adjective of, based on, or serving as a hypothesis: that option is merely hypothetical at this juncture. 
• supposed but not necessarily real or true: the hypothetical tenth planet. 
• Logic denoting or containing a proposition of the logical form if p then q. 
▶noun (usually hypotheticals) a hypothetical proposition or statement: Finn talked in hypotheticals, tossing what-if scenarios to Rosen.

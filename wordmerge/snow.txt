snow 
BrE snəʊ, AmE snoʊ 
A. nounUncountable
① (ice crystals) 雪 xuě a fall of snow 下雪 xiàxuě 
② Television [电视或雷达屏幕上的] “雪花” “ xuěhuā ” 
③ colloquial (cocaine) 白面儿 báimiànr 
B.  snows noun plural 降雪 jiàngxuě the eternal snows of the Frozen North 严寒北方的永久积雪 
C.  impersonal verb 下雪 xiàxuě it's snowing; 下雪了 
D.  transitive verb AmE colloquial 用花言巧语蒙骗 yòng huā yán qiǎo yǔ mēngpiàn to snow [somebody] into doing [something]; 用花言巧语哄某人做某事 PHRASAL VERBS snow in: transitive verbusually passive to be snowed in; «person, family» 被雪困住 «house, farm» 被雪封住 bèi xuě fēngzhù snow under: transitive verbusually passive to be snowed under; (covered with snow) «fields, village» 被雪覆盖 figurative (inundated with work, letters, etc.) 应接不暇 yìngjiē bù xiá I'm snowed under with work at the moment 眼下我正忙得不可开交 snow up transitive verb = snow in

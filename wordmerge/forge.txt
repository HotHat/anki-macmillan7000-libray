forge 
BrE fɔːdʒ, AmE fɔrdʒ 

A.  transitive verb

② (in metal) 锻造 duànzào ‹iron, horseshoe›
③ (establish) 建立 jiànlì ‹friendship, links›; 制订 zhìdìng ‹plan›; 订立 dìnglì ‹agreement›
B.  intransitive verb 稳步前进 wěnbù qiánjìn to forge ahead; 稳步前进 wěnbù qiánjìn to forge ahead or forward with; 稳步实施 ‹plan, idea›to forge into the lead 加速跑到前头 
C.  noun 
① (smithy) 铁匠铺 tiějiangpù 
② (furnace, hearth) 锻铁炉 duàntiělú 
③ (workshop) 锻造车间 duànzào chējiān 
④ (factory) 锻造工厂 duànzào gōngchǎng

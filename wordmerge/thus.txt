thus 
T͟Həs

▶adverb literary or formal
1 as a result or consequence of this; therefore: Burke knocked out Byrne, thus becoming champion.
2 in the manner now being indicated or exemplified; in this way: she phoned Susan, and while she was thus engaged, Charles summoned the doctor.
3 [as submodifier] to this point; so: the website has been cracked three times thus far. ORIGIN Old English, of unknown origin.

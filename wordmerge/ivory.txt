ivory 
BrE ˈʌɪv(ə)ri, AmE ˈaɪv(ə)ri 

A.  noun 
① Uncountable(substance) 象牙 xiàngyá ; before noun 象牙制的 xiàngyázhì de an ivory handle 象牙把手 
② Countable(ornament) 象牙制品 xiàngyá zhìpǐn 
③ Uncountable(colour) 象牙色 xiàngyásè 
B.  ivories noun plural colloquial (piano keys) 钢琴键 gāngqínjiàn to tickle the ivories 弹钢琴 
C.  adjective (in colour) 象牙色的 xiàngyásè de ‹skin, complexion›

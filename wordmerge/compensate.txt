compensate 
BrE ˈkɒmpɛnseɪt, ˈkɒmp(ə)nseɪt, AmE ˈkɑmpənˌseɪt 
A. transitive verb 赔偿 péicháng to compensate [somebody] for [something]/doing [something]; 为某事物/做某事赔偿某人 
B.  intransitive verb 补偿 bǔcháng to compensate for [something]; 为…进行补偿 ‹loss, difficulty›to go a long way to compensate or towards compensating for … 费一番周折来弥补… 
compensate 
▶verb
1 you must compensate for what you did: make amends, make up, make reparation, recompense, atone, requite, pay; expiate, make good, rectify.
2 we agreed to compensate him for his loss: recompense, repay, pay back, reimburse, remunerate, recoup, requite, indemnify.
3 his flair compensated for his faults: balance (out), counterbalance, counteract, offset, make up for, cancel out, neutralize, negative.

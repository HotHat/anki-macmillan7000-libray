then 
▶adverb
1 I was living in Cairo then: at that time, in those days; at that point (in time), at that moment, on that occasion.
2 she won the first and then the second game: next, after that, afterward/afterwards, subsequently, later.
3 and then there's another problem: in addition, also, besides, as well, additionally, on top of that, over and above that, moreover, furthermore, what's more, to boot; too.
4 well, if that's what he wants, then he should leave: in that case, that being so, it follows that.

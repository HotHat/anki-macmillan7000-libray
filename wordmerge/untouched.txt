untouched 
ˌənˈtəCHt

▶adjective
1 not handled, used, or tasted: Annabel pushed aside her untouched plate. 
• (of a subject) not treated in writing or speech; not discussed: no detail is left untouched.
2 not affected, changed, or damaged in any way: Prague was relatively untouched by the war.

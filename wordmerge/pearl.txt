pearl 
BrE pəːl, AmE pərl 
A. noun 
① Countable(object) 珍珠 zhēnzhū to cast pearls before swine 明珠暗投 talking to those kids about opera is casting pearls before swine 跟那些孩子谈歌剧无异于对牛弹琴 imitation pearls 人造珍珠 pearl earrings 珍珠耳环 
② Countablefigurative (of dew, sweat) 似珍珠之物 sì zhēnzhū zhī wù pearls of dew 露珠 
③ Countable(precious person) 有价值的人 yǒu jiàzhí de rén (precious object) 有价值的物品 yǒu jiàzhí de wùpǐn a pearl among women 女中俊杰 pearls of wisdom 智慧的结晶 thank you for your pearls of wisdom 谢谢你的金玉良言 
④ Uncountable(substance) = mother-of-pearl 
B.  pearls noun plural 珍珠项链 zhēnzhū xiàngliàn

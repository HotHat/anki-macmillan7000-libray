northwest 
ˌnôrTHˈwest

▶noun (usually the northwest)
1 the point of the horizon midway between north and west: he pointed to the northwest. 
• the compass point corresponding to northwest. 
• the direction in which northwest lies.
2 the northwestern part of a country, region, or town: they had originally come from someplace in the northwest of Mexico. 
▶adjective
1 lying toward, near, or facing the northwest: the northwest corner of the square. 
• (of a wind) blowing from the northwest.
2 of or denoting the northwestern part of a country, region, or town, or its inhabitants: northwest Europe. 
▶adverb to or toward the northwest: he turned onto the highway and headed northwest.

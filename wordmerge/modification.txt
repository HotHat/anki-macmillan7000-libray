modification 
ˌmädəfəˈkāSH(ə)n

▶noun the action of modifying something: the parts supplied should fit with little or no modification. 
• a change made: there will be a number of modifications to the engines. ORIGIN late 15th century (in Scots law, denoting the assessment of a payment): from French, or from Latin modificatio(n-), from modificare (see modify) .

B.  
onward 
ˈänwərd ˈônwərd

▶adverb (also onwards) in a continuing forward direction; ahead: she stumbled onward. 
• forward in time: the period from 1969 onward. 
• so as to make progress or become more successful: the business moved onward and upward. 
▶adjective going further rather than coming to an end or halt; moving forward: oil was pumped to a port for onward shipment 
figurative : the onward march of history.

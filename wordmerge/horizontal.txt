horizontal 
ˌhôrəˈzän(t)l

▶adjective
1 parallel to the plane of the horizon; at right angles to the vertical: a horizontal line. 
• (of machinery) having its parts working in a horizontal direction: a horizontal steam engine.
2 merging companies that are engaged in the same stage or type of production: a horizontal merger. 
• involving social groups of equal status: horizontal class loyalties.
3 of or at the horizon: the horizontal moon. 
▶noun a horizontal line, plane, etc. DERIVATIVES horizontality

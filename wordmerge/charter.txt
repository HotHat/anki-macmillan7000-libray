charter 
▶noun
1 a royal charter: authority, authorization, sanction, dispensation, consent, permission; permit, license, warrant, franchise.
2 the UN Charter: constitution, code, canon; fundamental principles, rules, laws.
3 the charter of a yacht: hire, hiring, lease, leasing, rent, rental, renting; booking, reservation, reserving.
▶verb they chartered a bus: hire, lease, rent; book, reserve.

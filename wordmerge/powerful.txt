powerful 
ˈpou(ə)rfəl

▶adjective having great power or strength: a fast, powerful car 
computers are now more compact and powerful. 
• (of a person, organization, or country) having control and influence over people and events: the world's most powerful nation. 
• having a strong effect on people's feelings or thoughts: his photomontages are powerful antiwar images.

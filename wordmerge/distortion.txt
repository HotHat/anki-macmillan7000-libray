distortion 
BrE dɪˈstɔːʃ(ə)n, AmE dəˈstɔrʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① (of metal) 变形 biànxíng (of sound) 失真 shīzhēn (of features) 扭曲 niǔqū 
② (of truth, fact, history) 歪曲 wāiqū ; (of meaning, words, text) 曲解 qūjiě 
③ (of result, figures, statistics) 篡改 cuàngǎi

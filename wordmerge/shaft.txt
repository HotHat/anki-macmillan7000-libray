shaft 
▶noun
1 the shaft of a golf club: pole, shank, stick, rod, staff; handle, hilt, stem.
2 the shaft of a feather: quill; Ornithology rachis.
3 shafts of sunlight: ray, beam, gleam, streak, finger.
4 a ventilation shaft: hole, mineshaft, tunnel, passage, pit, adit, downcast, upcast; borehole, bore; duct, well, flue, vent.

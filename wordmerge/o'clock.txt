▶adverb used to specify the hour in telling time: the gates will open at eight o'clock. 
• used following a numeral to indicate direction or bearing with reference to an imaginary clock face,
12 o'clock being thought of as directly in front or overhead: “I think we've got some action at
11 o'clock,” he said, gesturing toward the eastern plains.

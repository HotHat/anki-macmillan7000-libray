versus 
ˈvərsəs ˈvərsəz 
(abbreviation v. or vs.) 
▶preposition against (especially in sports and legal use): Penn versus Princeton. 
• as opposed to; in contrast to: weighing the pros and cons of organic versus inorganic produce. ORIGIN late Middle English: from a medieval Latin use of Latin versus ‘toward.’

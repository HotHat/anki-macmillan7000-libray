bulletin 
ˈbo͝olətn ˈbo͝olədən

▶noun a short official statement or broadcast summary of news. 
• a regular newsletter or printed report issued by an organization or society. ORIGIN mid 17th century (denoting an official warrant in some European countries): from French, from Italian bullettino, diminutive of bulletta ‘passport,’ diminutive of bulla ‘seal, bull.’

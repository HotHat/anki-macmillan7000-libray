▶noun a diagram showing the relation between variable quantities, typically of two variables, each measured along one of a pair of axes at right angles. 
• Mathematics a collection of points whose coordinates satisfy a given relation. 
▶verb [with object] plot or trace on a graph. ORIGIN late 19th century: abbreviation of graphic formula.

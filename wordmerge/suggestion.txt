suggestion 
BrE səˈdʒɛstʃ(ə)n, AmE sə(ɡ)ˈdʒɛstʃ(ə)n 
noun 
① Countable(proposal) 建议 jiànyì to put forward or offer or make a suggestion (about [something]) （针对某事物）提出建议 there is no suggestion of any illegality 没人提出违法指控 
② Uncountable(recommendation) 提议 tíyì at or on [somebody's] suggestion; 在某人的提议之下 
③ Countable(hint) 迹象 jìxiàng the merest suggestion of a smile crossed her face 她脸上露出一丝不易察觉的微笑 
④ UncountablePsychology 心理暗示 xīnlǐ ànshì the power of suggestion 暗示的力量

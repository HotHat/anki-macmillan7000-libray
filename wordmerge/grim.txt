grim 
ɡrim

▶adjective (grimmer, grimmest) forbidding or uninviting: his grim expression 
long rows of grim, dark housing developments. 
• (of humor) lacking genuine levity; mirthless; black: some moments of grim humor. 
• depressing or worrying to consider: the grim news of the murder. 
• unrelentingly harsh; merciless or severe: few creatures are able to thrive in this grim and hostile land.

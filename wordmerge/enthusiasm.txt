enthusiasm 
▶noun
1 she worked with enthusiasm: eagerness, keenness, ardor, fervor, passion, zeal, zest, gusto, energy, verve, vigor, vehemence, fire, spirit, avidity; wholeheartedness, commitment, willingness, devotion, earnestness; informal get-up-and-go. ANTONYMS apathy, half-heartedness.
2 he responded to the proposal with enthusiasm: interest, admiration, approval, support, encouragement. ANTONYMS apathy, disinterest.
3 they put their enthusiasms to good use: interest, passion, obsession, mania; inclination, preference, penchant, predilection, fancy; pastime, hobby, recreation, pursuit.

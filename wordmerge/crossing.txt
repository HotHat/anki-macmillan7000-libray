crossing 
ˈkrôsiNG

▶noun
1 a place where two roads, two railroad lines, or a road and a railroad line cross. 
• the action of moving across or over something: the crossing of the Pyrenees. 
• a journey across water in a ship: a short ferry crossing. 
• a place at which one may safely cross something, especially a street. 
• a place at which one can cross a border between countries. 
• Architecture the intersection of a church nave and the transepts.
2 crossbreeding.

A.  noun

② Uncountable and countable(fine jet of liquid) 喷剂 pēnjì a throat/nasal spray 润喉/鼻雾喷剂 to apply [something] in or as a spray 把…喷涂上去 ‹paint, glaze›a spray deodorant 除臭喷剂

③ figurative (emit) «furnace, explosion» 溅落 jiànluò ‹shards›to be sprayed with sparks 溅满火星 to spray bullets 扫射

① literal 喷 pēn water sprayed all over the floor 水喷了一地 
② figurative (shower) «bullets, sparks» 倾泻 qīngxiè to spray all over the room «shards» 溅落至房间各个角落 
spray
1 
▶noun
1 a spray of water: shower, sprinkling, sprinkle, jet, mist, drizzle; spume, spindrift; foam, froth.
2 a perfume spray: atomizer, vaporizer, aerosol, sprinkler; nebulizer.
▶verb
1 water was sprayed around: sprinkle, shower, spatter; scatter, disperse, diffuse; mist; douche; literary besprinkle.
2 water sprayed into the air: spout, jet, gush, spurt, shoot, squirt.

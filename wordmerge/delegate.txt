delegate 
A.  /BrE ˈdɛlɪɡeɪt, AmE ˈdɛləˌɡeɪt
transitive verb 
① (entrust) 委托 wěituō ‹power, responsibility, task›to delegate [something] to [somebody]; 把某事委托给某人 he is reluctant to delegate authority 他不情愿移交权力 you've been delegated to make the tea humorous 你负责沏茶 
② (send) 委派 wěipài to delegate [somebody] to do [something]; 选派某人做某事

delegate

▶verb
1 she must delegate routine tasks: assign, entrust, pass on, hand on/over, turn over, devolve, depute, transfer.

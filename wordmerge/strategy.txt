strategy 
ˈstradəjē

▶noun (plural strategies) a plan of action or policy designed to achieve a major or overall aim: time to develop a coherent economic strategy 
shifts in marketing strategy. 
• the art of planning and directing overall military operations and movements in a war or battle. Often contrasted with tactics (see tactic). 
• a plan for military operations and movements during a war or battle: nonprovocative defense strategies. ORIGIN early 19th century: from French stratégie, from Greek stratēgia ‘generalship,’ from stratēgos (see stratagem) .

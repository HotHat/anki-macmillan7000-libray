prince 
prins

▶noun the son of a monarch. 
• a close male relative of a monarch, especially a son's son. 
• a male royal ruler of a small state, actually, nominally, or originally subject to a king or emperor. 
• (in France, Germany and other European countries) a nobleman, usually ranking next below a duke. 
• (prince of/among) a man or thing regarded as outstanding or excellent in a particular sphere or group: arctic char is a prince among fishes. DERIVATIVES princedom

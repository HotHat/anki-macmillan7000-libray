dimension 
▶noun
1 the dimensions of the room: size, measurements, proportions, extent; length, width, breadth, depth, area, volume, capacity; footage, acreage.
2 the dimension of the problem: size, scale, extent, scope, magnitude; importance, significance.
3 the cultural dimensions of the problem: aspect, feature, element, facet, side.

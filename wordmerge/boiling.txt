boiling 
▶adjective
1 boiling water: at boiling point, at 212 degrees Fahrenheit, at 100 degrees Celsius/centigrade; very hot, piping hot; bubbling. ANTONYMS freezing, ice-cold.
2 informal it was a boiling day: very hot, scorching, blistering, sweltering, sultry, torrid; informal broiling, roasting, baking, sizzling. ANTONYMS cold, freezing.

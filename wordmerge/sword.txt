sword 
sôrd

▶noun a weapon with a long metal blade and a hilt with a hand guard, used for thrusting or striking and now typically worn as part of ceremonial dress. 
• (the sword) literary military power, violence, or destruction: not many perished by the sword. 
• (swords) one of the suits in a tarot pack. 
PHRASES
beat (or turn) swords into ploughshares devote resources to peaceful rather than warlike ends.

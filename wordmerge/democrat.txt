democrat 
ˈdeməˌkrat

▶noun
1 an advocate or supporter of democracy.
2 (Democrat) a member of the Democratic Party. ORIGIN late 18th century (originally denoting an opponent of the aristocrats in the French Revolution of 1790): from French démocrate, on the pattern of aristocrate ‘aristocrat.’

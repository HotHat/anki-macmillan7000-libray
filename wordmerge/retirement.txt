retirement 
BrE rɪˈtʌɪəm(ə)nt, AmE rəˈtaɪ(ə)rmənt 
noun 
① Uncountable(action) 退休 tuìxiū to take early retirement 提前退休 over or above/under or below the age of retirement 超过/未到退休年龄 compulsory retirement 强制退休 
② Countable(instance) 退休个例 tuìxiū gèlì we have had several retirements at the office this year 今年我们办公室有好几个人退休了 
③ (state) 退休生活 tuìxiū shēnghuó to live in retirement 过退休生活 a peaceful retirement 平静的退休生活 he decided to spend his retirement painting 他决定退休后以绘画打发时间

undergo 
ˌəndərˈɡō

▶verb (undergoes, undergoing; past underwent; past participle undergone) [with object] experience or be subjected to (something, typically something unpleasant, painful, or arduous): the baby underwent a life-saving brain operation. ORIGIN Old English undergān ‘undermine’ (see under-, go1) .

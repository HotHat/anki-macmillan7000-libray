radio 
BrE ˈreɪdɪəʊ, AmE ˈreɪdioʊ 
A. noun(plural radios) 
① Uncountable(system) 无线电 wúxiàndiàn to send a message by radio 通过无线电发送信息 
② Countable(apparatus) 收音机 shōuyīnjī to turn the radio up/down 把收音机音量开大/关小 to have the radio on 让收音机开着 an AM/FM radio 中波/调频收音机 a portable/stereo radio 手提式/立体声收音机 
③ Uncountable(broadcasting) 无线电广播 wúxiàndiàn guǎngbō on the radio 在广播中 to listen to the radio 听广播 to be or work in radio 在电台工作 
④ Countable(communication device) 无线电设备 wúxiàndiàn shèbèi a radio receiver/transmitter 无线电接收器/发送器 the taxi driver used his radio to call for help 出租车司机用无线电求助 
B.  transitive verb(present tense radioes; past tense, past participle radioed) 
① (communicate with) 用无线电联系 yòng wúxiàndiàn liánxì ‹person, ship›
② (send) 用无线电发送 yòng wúxiàndiàn fāsòng ‹information›
C.  intransitive verb (present tense radioes; past tense, past participle radioed) 用无线电通讯 yòng wúxiàndiàn tōngxùn to radio for help 用无线电求助

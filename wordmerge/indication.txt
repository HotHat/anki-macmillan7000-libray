indication 
ˌindəˈkāSH(ə)n

▶noun a sign or piece of information that indicates something: the visit was an indication of the improvement in relations between the countries. 
• a reading given by a gauge or meter. 
• a symptom that suggests certain medical treatment is necessary: heavy bleeding is a common indication for hysterectomy.

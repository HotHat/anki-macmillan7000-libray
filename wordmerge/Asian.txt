Asian 
ˈāZHən

▶adjective relating to Asia or its people, customs, or languages. 
▶noun a native of Asia or a person of Asian descent. ORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin Asianus, from Greek Asianos, from Asia (see Asia) . usage: In North America, Asian refers to people from China, Japan, and other countries of East Asia, while in Britain it is used to refer to people who come from (or whose parents came from) India, Pakistan, or elsewhere in South Asia. See also usage at oriental .

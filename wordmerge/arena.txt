arena 
əˈrēnə

▶noun a level area surrounded by seats for spectators, in which sports, entertainments, and other public events are held. 
• a place or scene of activity, debate, or conflict: he has re-entered the political arena. ORIGIN early 17th century: from Latin harena, arena ‘sand, sand-strewn place of combat.’

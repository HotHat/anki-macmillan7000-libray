cater 
BrE ˈkeɪtə, AmE ˈkeɪdər 
A. intransitive verb 
① (provide food) to cater for [somebody]/[something]; 为某人/某事物提供饮食 wèi mǒu rén/mǒu shìwù tígōng yǐnshí they cater for wedding receptions 他们承办婚宴 tāmen chéngbàn hūnyàn 
② (provide) to cater for [somebody]/[something]; 满足某人/某事物的需要 mǎnzú mǒu rén/mǒu shìwù de xūyào to cater for the needs/tastes of … 满足…的需要/迎合…的口味 mǎnzú… de xūyào/yínghé… de kǒuwèi 
③ (consider) to cater for [somebody]/[something]; 考虑到某人/某事物 kǎolǜ dào mǒu rén/mǒu shìwù 
④ (satisfy demands) to cater to [somebody]/[something]; 迎合某人/某事物 yínghé mǒu rén/mǒu shìwù 
B.  transitive verb 为…提供饮食 wèi… tígōng yǐnshí he catered a lunch for 25 他为25个人准备午餐 
cater 
▶verb
1 we cater for vegetarians: provide food for, feed, serve, cook for.
2 a resort catering to older travellers: serve, provide for, meet the needs/wants of, accommodate; satisfy, indulge, pander to, gratify.
3 he seemed to cater to all tastes: take into account, take into consideration, allow for, consider, bear in mind, make provision for, have regard for.

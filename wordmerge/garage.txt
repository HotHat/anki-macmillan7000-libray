garage 
ɡəˈräZH ɡəˈräj

▶noun
1 a building or shed for housing a motor vehicle or vehicles. 
• an establishment that provides services and repairs for motor vehicles.
2 (also garage rock) a style of unpolished energetic rock music associated with suburban amateur bands: [as modifier] : garage band. 
▶verb [with object] put or keep (a motor vehicle) in a garage. ORIGIN early 20th century: from French, from garer ‘to shelter.’

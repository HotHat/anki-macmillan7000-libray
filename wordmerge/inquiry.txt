inquiry 
BrE ɪnˈkwʌɪri, AmE ˈɪnkwəri, ɪnˈkwaɪ(ə)ri 
noun 
① Countable(request for information) 询问 xúnwèn to make an inquiry about or into [something]; 查问某事 on inquiry, it was discovered that … 调查时发现… ‘all inquiries to …’ “所有查询事宜请到…” in answer to or with reference to your inquiry 作为对您所咨询问题的答复 
② Countable(official investigation) 调查 diàochá to hold or conduct an inquiry into [something] 对某事展开调查 
③ Uncountable(informal investigation) 打听 dǎting on inquiry 通过探问 a fruitful line of inquiry 成效不错的探听线索

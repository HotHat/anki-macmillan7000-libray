donation 
dōˈnāSH(ə)n

▶noun something that is given to a charity, especially a sum of money: a tax-deductible donation of $200. 
• the action of donating something. ORIGIN late Middle English: via Old French from Latin donatio(n-), from the verb donare, based on donum ‘gift.’

visa 
ˈvēzə

▶noun an endorsement on a passport indicating that the holder is allowed to enter, leave, or stay for a specified period of time in a country. ORIGIN mid 19th century: via French from Latin visa, past participle (neuter plural) of videre ‘to see.’

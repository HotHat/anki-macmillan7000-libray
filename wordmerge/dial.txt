dial 
BrE dʌɪəl, AmE ˈdaɪ(ə)l 

A.  noun 
① (on telephone) 拨号盘 bōhàopán 
② (of gauge, meter) 刻度盘 kèdùpán 
③ (of TV, radio) 调节器 tiáojiéqì ; (of other equipment) 控制器 kòngzhìqì 
④ (face of watch) 表盘 biǎopán ; (clock face) 钟盘 zhōngpán 
⑤ BrE dated colloquial (face) 脸 liǎn 
B.  transitive verb (present participle etc. -ll- BrE, -l- AmE ) 拨电话给 bō diànhuà gěi ‹person, country›; 拨 bō ‹number›to dial 222 for [something]/[somebody] 做某事/找某人请拨222 to dial the wrong number 拨错号 
C.  intransitive verb (present participle etc. -ll- BrE, -l- AmE ) 拨号 bōhào you can dial direct 你可以直拨

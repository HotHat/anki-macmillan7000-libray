battle 
▶noun
1 he was killed in the battle: fight, armed conflict, clash, struggle, skirmish, engagement, fray, duel; war, campaign, crusade; fighting, warfare, combat, action, hostilities; deathmatch; informal scrap, dogfight, shoot-out; brawl.
2 a battle at the office: conflict, clash, contest, competition, struggle, turf war; disagreement, argument, altercation, dispute, controversy, tug-of-war; vulgar slang shitstorm.
▶verb
1 he has been battling cancer: fight, combat, contend with; resist, withstand, stand up to, confront; war with, feud with; struggle with, strive against.
2 Mark battled his way to the podium: force, push, elbow, shoulder, fight; struggle, labor.

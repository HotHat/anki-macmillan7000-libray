clip
1 
klip

▶noun a device, typically flexible or worked by a spring, for holding an object or objects together or in place. 
• a clip used to hold paper currency. 
• a piece of jewelry fastened by a clip. 
• a metal holder containing cartridges for an automatic firearm. 
▶verb (clips, clipping, clipped) [with adverbial of place] fasten or be fastened with a clip or clips: [with object] : she clipped on a pair of diamond earrings 
[no object] : the panels simply clip onto the framework. ORIGIN Old English clyppan (verb). The noun use dates from the late 15th century.

mortal 
▶adjective
1 mortal remains 
all men are mortal: perishable, physical, bodily, corporeal, fleshly, earthly, this-worldly; human, impermanent, transient, ephemeral.
2 a mortal blow: deadly, fatal, lethal, death-dealing, murderous, terminal.
3 mortal enemies: irreconcilable, deadly, sworn, bitter, out-and-out, implacable.
4 a mortal sin: unpardonable, unforgivable. ANTONYMS venial.
5 living in mortal fear: extreme, (very) great, terrible, awful, dreadful, intense, severe, grave, dire, unbearable.
▶noun we are mere mortals: human being, human, person, man/woman; earthling.

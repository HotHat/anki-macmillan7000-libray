uncertain 
ˌənˈsərtn

▶adjective not able to be relied on; not known or definite: an uncertain future. 
• (of a person) not completely confident or sure of something: I was uncertain how to proceed. 
PHRASES
in no uncertain terms clearly and forcefully: she refused me, in no uncertain terms.

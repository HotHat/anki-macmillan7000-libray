▶noun the state of being equal, especially in status, rights, and opportunities: an organization aiming to promote racial equality. 
• Mathematics a symbolic expression of the fact that two quantities are equal; an equation. ORIGIN late Middle English: via Old French from Latin aequalitas, from aequalis (see equal) .

offense 
▶noun
1 he denied having committed any offense: crime, illegal/unlawful act, misdemeanor, breach of the law, felony, wrongdoing, wrong, misdeed, peccadillo, sin, transgression, infringement; Law malfeasance; informal no-no; archaic trespass; rare malefaction.
2 an offense to basic justice: affront, slap in the face, insult, outrage, violation, slight.
3 I do not want to cause offense: annoyance, anger, resentment, indignation, irritation, exasperation, wrath, displeasure, hard/bad/ill feelings, disgruntlement, pique, vexation, animosity, antipathy.
4 planning our next offense: attack, offensive, assault, onslaught, invasion, incursion, foray, sortie.

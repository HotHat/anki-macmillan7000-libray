zip 
BrE zɪp, AmE zɪp 
A. noun 
① Countableespecially BrE (zipper) 拉链 lāliàn the zip is stuck 拉链卡住了 to do up/undo a zip 拉上/拉开拉链 
② Countablesingular colloquial (sound) 飕飕声 sōu sōu shēng the zip of a bullet 子弹的呼啸声 
③ Uncountablecolloquial (energy) 活力 huólì she doesn't have much zip these days 这些天她有些委靡不振 
④ Countable AmE (for mail) = zip code 
⑤ Uncountable AmE colloquial (nothing) 零 líng to know zip about computers 对电脑一窍不通 
B.  transitive verb(present participle etc. -pp-) 
① (fasten) 拉…的拉链 lā… de lāliàn to zip one's bag open/shut 拉开/拉上袋子的拉链 she zipped herself into her dress 她穿上连衣裙，拉好拉链 
② Computing 压缩 yāsuō ‹file›
③ (send quickly) 使快速移动 shǐ kuàisù yídòng to zip a pass out to the receiver 嗖地把球传给接球手 
C.  intransitive verb(present participle etc. -pp-) 
① (fasten) «skirt, bag» 拉上拉链 lāshang lāliàn the dress zips at the back 这条连衣裙的拉链在背后 
② (move quickly) 快速移动 kuàisù yídòng to zip along 快速向前 a car zipped past us 一辆车从我们身边呼啸而过 to zip through one's work 快速干完工作 the morning zipped by 上午一下子就过去了 PHRASAL VERBS zip on A. transitive verb[zip something on], [zip on something] 用拉链装上 yòng lāliàn zhuāngshang to zip the hood on 用拉链把风帽装上 to zip on a jacket 穿上夹克，拉好拉链 
B.  intransitive verb 用拉链装上 yòng lāliàn zhuāngshang the hood zips on like this 风帽是这样用拉链装上去的 zip up A. transitive verb

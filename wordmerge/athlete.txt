athlete 
ˈaTHˌlēt

▶noun a person who is proficient in sports and other forms of physical exercise. 
• chiefly British a person who is skilled in competitive track and field events (athletics). ORIGIN late Middle English: from Latin athleta, from Greek athlētēs, from athlein ‘compete for a prize,’ from athlon ‘prize.’

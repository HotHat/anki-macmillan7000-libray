drawing 
ˈdroiNG

▶noun
1 a picture or diagram made with a pencil, pen, or crayon rather than paint, especially one drawn in monochrome: a series of charcoal drawings on white paper. 
• the art or skill or making pictures or diagrams with a pencil, pen, or crayon: she took lessons in drawing.
2 the selection of a winner or winners in a lottery or raffle: entrants need not be present at the drawing.
